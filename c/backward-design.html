逆向设计 nìxiàng shèjìbackward design (Understanding by Design)

🔊 backward design /ˈbækwərd dɪˈzaɪn/

威金斯与麦克泰的教学设计法:从'期望的学习成果'倒推。三步——①确定学生应达成的成果与理解;②确定可接受的评估证据（怎么知道达成了）;③再设计教学活动。它防止'为活动而活动',确保教、学、评始终对准同一目标。是澳洲单元规划的主流范式。

Wiggins and McTighe's design method: work backward from the desired learning outcomes. Three stages — (1) identify the outcomes and understandings students should reach; (2) determine acceptable evidence of assessment (how you'll know they got there); (3) only then design the learning activities. It prevents activity-for-activity's-sake and keeps teaching, learning and assessment aimed at the same target. The mainstream paradigm of Australian unit planning.

Etymology 词源

backward（向后）——从终点'向后'倒推设计;亦称 Understanding by Design（UbD,以理解为目标的设计）。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ begin with the end in mind /bɪˈgɪn wɪθ ðə ɛnd ɪn maɪnd/
    'Begin with the end in mind' is backward design borrowed from Covey.
  • ▸ Understanding by Design /ˌəndərˈstændɪŋ baɪ dɪˈzaɪn/
    Understanding by Design plans the assessment before the activities, not after.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ design backward from outcomes
  • ▸ Understanding by Design
  • ▸ evidence before activities
  • ▸ the three stages of UbD

In the wild 语境例句

Backward design plans the assessment task first — 'students will build and evaluate a working game' — then chooses only the lessons that get them there.
