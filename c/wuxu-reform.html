戊戌变法（百日维新） Wùxū biànfǎthe Hundred Days' Reform

⏳ 1898年

《马关条约》消息传京，1895年康有为梁启超联合1300多举人“公车上书”请求拒和迁都变法，拉开维新运动序幕；各地学会报刊宣传（梁启超《时务报》“变者天下之公理也”；严复《国闻报》、译《天演论》宣传“物竞天择，适者生存”）。1898年6月11日光绪帝颁“明定国是”诏变法103天：裁冗官、允官民上书、鼓励私人办厂、改革财政、废八股改策论、办新式学堂（京师大学堂）、裁绿营练新军。触犯以慈禧太后为首的顽固派利益，9月21日政变：囚光绪、捕维新士，谭嗣同等“戊戌六君子”遇害（“有心杀贼，无力回天”）。变法失败，但思想文化影响广泛持久。

News of Shimonoseki detonated the examination halls: 1,300 provincial graduates petitioned the throne — the 'public vehicles memorial' — and reform became a movement, spread by study societies and a new press (Yan Fu's translation of Huxley making 'survival of the fittest' the era's watchword). In summer 1898 the young Guangxu emperor issued a hundred days of edicts: sinecures abolished, private industry encouraged, the eight-legged essay scrapped, a modern university founded, new armies planned. The Empress Dowager Cixi's coup ended it in September — emperor imprisoned, six reformers beheaded (Tan Sitong refusing escape: 'reform needs blood; let it begin with mine'). The institutions died; the ideas, the textbook notes, did not.

In the wild 语境例句

The Hundred Days is China's 'too little, too late' benchmark: every stalled liberalisation gets measured against 1898, and every conservative backlash against Cixi's coup.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

严复译《天演论》使 social Darwinism 成为救亡话语——链 [[darwin]] nuance 的中文接收史在此落地。康梁路线=君主立宪 constitutional monarchy（链 [[glorious-revolution]]、[[meiji-restoration]]——教材问题即比日本）。“百日维新”= the Hundred Days' Reform（拿破仑 Hundred Days 同名异事，防混）。
