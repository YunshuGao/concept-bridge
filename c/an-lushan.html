安史之乱与唐朝衰亡 Ān-Shǐ zhī luànthe An Lushan rebellion and the Tang decline

🔊 rebellion /rɪˈbɛljən/

⏳ 755年–907年

开元末唐玄宗追求享乐任人唯亲（宠杨贵妃、用李林甫杨国忠），朝政腐败；节度使集军政财权于一身、外重内轻，安禄山一身兼三镇。755年安禄山史思明叛乱，陷洛阳逼长安，玄宗奔蜀、肃宗灵武即位，借西北精兵与少数民族援军763年平定。八年战乱北方浩劫（“人烟断绝，千里萧条”），唐由盛转衰，藩镇割据形成。后期宦官专权、藩镇兼并，黄巢起义攻入长安建政权、致命打击唐朝；907年朱温建后梁，唐亡——此后五代十国（北方五代更迭战乱，南方九国相对安定经济续发展），统一仍是必然趋势。

Late Xuanzong traded statecraft for Yang Guifei and flatterers while frontier commanders (jiedushi) accumulated army, purse and province — An Lushan held three commands at once. His revolt (755) took both capitals; the emperor fled to Sichuan, and eight years of war 'emptied a thousand li of cooking-smoke'. The dynasty survived but never recovered: warlord provinces, eunuch-run palaces, then Huang Chao's rebellion through Chang'an, until a rebel-turned-governor deposed the last Tang emperor in 907. Five Dynasties churned in the north, Ten Kingdoms prospered quietly in the south — and reunification, the textbook insists, remained history's standing default.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“节度使”= military governors/jiedushi；“藩镇割据”= warlord separatism of the garrisons。杨贵妃 = Yang Guifei（马嵬驿之死入《长恨歌》——白居易该诗有多个经典英译）。“外重内轻”可译 a heavy periphery and a hollow centre——极好的分析短语。
