智慧宫与阿拉伯文化 Zhìhuìgōngthe House of Wisdom and Arab learning

⏳ 8世纪–13世纪

830年哈里发在巴格达设“智慧宫”，集科学院、图书馆、翻译馆于一体，以与译著等重的黄金付酬延揽人才，大量希腊、波斯、印度典籍译为阿拉伯文。阿拉伯人改造印度计数法为“阿拉伯数字”、创立完整代数学；《医学集成》《医典》长期为欧洲医学经典；《天方夜谭》为文学瑰宝。阿拉伯人沟通东西方：中国造纸术、指南针、火药及印度棉花、食糖经其传入欧洲。

Baghdad's House of Wisdom (830) — academy, library and translation bureau, paying translators their books' weight in gold — turned Greek, Persian and Indian learning into Arabic. Arab scholars refined Indian numerals into 'Arabic' ones and founded algebra; Avicenna's Canon ruled European medicine for centuries; the Arabian Nights became a world classic. As civilisation's middlemen, Arabs carried Chinese paper, the compass and gunpowder — and Indian cotton and sugar — into Europe.

词源富矿：algebra ← al-jabr（还原）；algorithm ← 数学家花拉子米 al-Khwarizmi 之名；《医典》= Avicenna's Canon of Medicine（伊本·西那拉丁名 Avicenna）；《天方夜谭》= the Arabian Nights / One Thousand and One Nights。“沟通东西方” = cultural intermediaries/middlemen；此期常称 the Islamic Golden Age。
