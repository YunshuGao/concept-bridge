罗马帝国 Luómǎ dìguóthe Roman Empire

🔊 principate /ˈprɪnsɪpeɪt/

⏳ 前27年–476年

前27年屋大维首创“元首制”掌握实权，共和国演变为帝国；他表面维护共和传统、不称帝而受尊号“奥古斯都”。2世纪帝国进入黄金时期，版图横跨欧亚非，地中海成为其“内湖”。

Born in 27 BC when Octavian, styling himself merely 'first citizen' (the Principate) while keeping the Senate's forms, took the honorific Augustus and all real power; by the 2nd century the empire spanned three continents and the Mediterranean was effectively a Roman lake.

Etymology 词源

principate 源自拉丁 princeps（第一人）——“元首制”：帝制穿着共和外衣。

Synonyms 近义词

    Imperial administration is distance mastered by paperwork.

In the wild 语境例句

Augustus perfected the autocrat's oldest trick: keep the republic's stage set standing while quietly rewriting every role — emperors have imitated the method ever since.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“元首制”专名 the Principate（princeps 第一公民）；“奥古斯都”Augustus（至尊者）后成皇帝头衔并留在历法里（August 八月）。“黄金时期”即英语史学的 Pax Romana（罗马和平）；“内湖”对应罗马人自称的 mare nostrum。
