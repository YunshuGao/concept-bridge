投资政策声明（IPS） tóuzī zhèngcè shēngmíngthe Investment Policy Statement (IPS)

🔊 Investment Policy Statement /ˌɪnˈvɛstmənt ˈpɑləsi ˈsteɪtmənt/

投资管理的宪法性文件:明确客户的目标(回报要求)与约束条件——用'RR-TTLLU'框架:回报(Return)、风险(Risk)、时间跨度(Time)、税收(Tax)、流动性(Liquidity)、法律(Legal)、特殊情况(Unique)。它把'该怎么投?'锚定在客户的真实处境上,是CFA三级与专业理财的起点与准绳。

The constitutional document of investment management: it spells out a client's objectives (return, risk) and constraints — via the framework of return, risk, time horizon, taxes, liquidity, legal, and unique circumstances. It anchors 'how should this be invested?' to the client's real situation, and is the starting point and yardstick of CFA Level III and professional advising.

Etymology 词源

policy 源自希腊 politeia（治理、政体）——IPS即组合的'治理章程';如同宪法之于国家。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ objectives and constraints /əˈbʤɛktɪvz ənd kənˈstreɪnts/
    Objectives and constraints — return, risk, time, tax, liquidity, legal, unique — are the IPS in one breath.
  • ▸ the investment mandate /ðə ˌɪnˈvɛstmənt ˈmænˌdeɪt/
    The investment mandate written in the IPS binds the manager as a fiduciary.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

A 45-year-old business owner retiring in 15 years, worried about inflation — the IPS turns that human situation into explicit return, risk, liquidity and time-horizon terms that drive the allocation.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

IPS区分风险'承受能力'(ability,客观:财力、时间跨度)与'承受意愿'(willingness,主观:心理),二者取其低。与YOS的'宪法'同构:先定原则,再谈执行。
