第一次世界大战 dìyīcì shìjiè dàzhànthe First World War

🔊 attrition /əˈtrɪʃən/

also: World War I · the Great War

⏳ 1914年–1918年

1914年6月28日奥匈皇储斐迪南大公夫妇在萨拉热窝遇刺（导火索），奥匈对塞宣战，两大集团相继卷入。欧洲三条战线；1916年凡尔登战役历时10个月、伤亡近百万，称“绞肉机”。1917年美国因无限制潜艇战参战（中国亦加入协约国、派十几万劳工赴欧），俄国十月革命后退出；1918年11月德国投降。历时4年余、30多国15亿人卷入、伤亡超3000万——列强重新瓜分世界、争夺霸权的帝国主义战争；大大削弱欧洲优势地位，促进殖民地半殖民地民族觉醒。

Sparked at Sarajevo on 28 June 1914 by the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand, the alliance system dragged the powers in within weeks. Verdun's ten-month 'mincing machine' cost a million casualties; unrestricted submarine warfare brought America in (and China, which sent 140,000 labourers to the trenches), revolution took Russia out, and Germany capitulated in November 1918. Four years, thirty nations, thirty million casualties — in the textbook's verdict an imperialist war for redividing the world, which broke Europe's global primacy and woke the colonised world.

'All Quiet on the Western Front' and 'over by Christmas' entered English as the war's twin epitaphs — the illusion of a short war and the reality of the trenches.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

英语惯称一战为 the Great War（当时无人知道会有第二次）。“绞肉机”对应英语 the mincing machine/meat grinder（凡尔登）与 war of attrition（消耗战）。tank（坦克）本义“水柜”——为保密取的代号直接成为正式名，是词源趣史。“无限制潜艇战”= unrestricted submarine warfare。
