🔊 antifragility /ˌæntifrəˈdʒɪlɪti/

塔勒布造词:超越'坚固'（robust,在冲击中不受损)的更高境界——反脆弱系统能从波动、压力与混乱中受益、变得更强,如肌肉因训练而强壮、免疫系统因暴露而增强。它主张拥抱适度的波动与试错,而非追求虚假的稳定。

Taleb's coinage for a property beyond robustness (which merely withstands shocks): antifragile systems gain from volatility, stress and disorder, growing stronger — like muscle from training or immunity from exposure. It argues for embracing measured volatility and trial-and-error rather than chasing a false stability.

Etymology 词源

anti-（反）+ fragile（脆弱,拉丁 frangere 打破）——塔勒布造词:不是'不碎',而是'越受压越强'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ gains from disorder /geɪnz frəm dɪˈsɔrdər/
    Gains from disorder describe the trader, the startup and the immune system alike.
  • ▸ optionality /ˌɒpʃəˈnælɪti/
    Optionality — small capped losses, large uncapped gains — is how you build antifragility.

In the wild 语境例句

An antifragile portfolio — mostly safe assets plus small bets with unlimited upside — doesn't just survive a crash, it profits from the chaos.
