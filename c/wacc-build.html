WACC 的搭建 WACC de dājiànbuilding the WACC

🔊 building the WACC /ˈbɪldɪŋ ðə wæk/

DCF贴现率的实操构建:①股权成本用CAPM=无风险利率+β×股权风险溢价,其中β常取可比公司β去杠杆(卸掉各自资本结构影响)再按目标公司资本结构重新加杠杆;②债务成本用税后利率;③按市值权重加权。每一步的假设都可辩论——WACC是DCF中最易被'凑'出目标估值的旋钮。

The hands-on construction of the DCF discount rate: (1) cost of equity via CAPM = risk-free rate + beta × equity risk premium, where beta is often taken from comparables, unlevered to remove each one's capital structure, then relevered to the target's; (2) after-tax cost of debt; (3) weighted by market values. Every step's assumptions are debatable — WACC is the knob most easily 'tuned' to a desired DCF answer.

见 weighted-average-cost-of-capital 节点;'build'指在模型中逐项搭建这个贴现率。

Building WACC, the analyst pulls five peers' betas, unlevers each, averages, then relevers to the target's debt mix — a defensible beta, not a guessed one.
