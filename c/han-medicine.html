张仲景与华佗（中医奠基） Zhāng Zhòngjǐng / Huà TuóZhang Zhongjing, Hua Tuo and classical Chinese medicine

⏳ 东汉末

中医在两汉发展到较高水平：扁鹊已创望闻问切四诊法，《黄帝内经》奠定理论基础。东汉末张仲景广集民间药方结合临床写成《伤寒杂病论》，提出辨证分析对症治疗、发展“治未病”思想——中医临床理论体系开创者，后世尊“医圣”。华佗擅针灸汤药并能外科手术，发明麻醉药剂“麻沸散”，创编强身健体的“五禽戏”。

Han medicine systematised a tradition: the four diagnostics (looking, listening, asking, pulse-taking) from Bian Que, theory from the Yellow Emperor's Inner Canon. Zhang Zhongjing's Treatise on Cold Damage founded clinical method — pattern diagnosis, treatment to the case, and 'treating illness before it arises' — earning him the title Sage of Medicine; Hua Tuo performed surgery under his own anaesthetic, mafeisan, and prescribed exercise in the Five Animal Frolics.

In the wild 语境例句

'治未病' translates uncannily as preventive medicine — public-health economists cite the 2,000-year-old phrase when arguing that health systems pay for sickness and should pay for health.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

中医 = traditional Chinese medicine (TCM，标准缩写)。“辨证论治”= pattern/syndrome differentiation and treatment（TCM 英语文献术语）。华佗麻醉早于西方乙醚约1600年——医学史常引；“刮骨疗毒”等典故出《三国演义》而非正史，引用须辨。
