都江堰 Dūjiāngyànthe Dujiangyan waterworks

⏳ 前256年

前256年蜀郡郡守李冰主持，在成都附近岷江上修建的综合性水利枢纽：鱼嘴分水（内江灌溉、外江分洪）、宝瓶口引水、飞沙堰泄洪排沙，集防洪灌溉水运于一体。建成后成都平原“水旱从人，不知饥馑”，成为“天府之国”；2200多年持续发挥作用（灌溉面积今超千万亩），2008年汶川8级地震中主体安然无恙——世界水利史上绝无仅有。

Built from 256 BC by Li Bing, governor of Shu, on the Min river above Chengdu: a fish-mouth levee splitting the flow (inner channel for irrigation, outer for flood), a rock-cut 'bottle-neck' intake, a spillway shedding silt and floodwater — flood control, irrigation and navigation in one gravity-driven system, no dam at all. It made the Chengdu plain 'the land of heaven's abundance', still waters ten million mu today, and rode out the magnitude-8 quake of 2008 essentially intact: nothing else in hydraulic history comes close.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“天府之国”= the land of abundance/heaven's storehouse。魏特夫“治水社会”（hydraulic society/Oriental despotism）理论以中国水利立论而广受批判——链 [[absolute-monarchy]] 的 nuance 警告。都江堰 vs 大坝的对比是可持续工程叙事的常用素材。
