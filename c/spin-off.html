分拆上市 fēnchāi shàngshìthe spin-off

🔊 spin-off /spɪn-ɔf/

母公司把一块业务分拆为独立上市公司，按比例把新公司股份分配给现有股东——不出售、不套现，而是让市场分别定价两家更纯粹的公司，以消除多元化折价、释放隐藏价值。

A parent separates a business into an independently listed company and distributes its shares to existing shareholders pro rata — no sale, no cash raised, but letting the market price two purer companies separately to erase the conglomerate discount and unlock hidden value.

Etymology 词源

spin off（甩出、旋出）——像旋转中甩出的部件，子公司被“甩”成独立实体。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ demerger /diˈmərʤər/
    A demerger splits one ticker into two, hoping the sum re-rates upward.
  • ▸ pure-play /pjʊr-pleɪ/
    A pure-play company lets investors buy exactly the exposure they want.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ spin off a division
  • ▸ a demerger
  • ▸ create two pure-plays
  • ▸ re-rate after separation

In the wild 语境例句

After the spin-off, the fast-growing software unit traded at a tech multiple instead of being dragged down by its industrial parent's rating.
