平台商业模式 píngtái shāngyè móshìthe platform business model

🔊 platform business /ˈplætˌfɔrm ˈbɪznɪs/

平台不自产价值，而是撮合两组或多组用户在其上交易与互动（商家-顾客、司机-乘客、开发者-用户），靠网络效应做大。价值创造从“管道”（自产自销）转向“平台”（协调他人）。

A platform doesn't produce the value itself; it matches two or more user groups to transact and interact on it (merchants-shoppers, drivers-riders, developers-users), scaling on network effects. Value creation shifts from pipes (make and sell) to platforms (orchestrate others).

Etymology 词源

platform 源自法语 plateforme（平的表面、台）——商业平台即供各方“站上来交易的台”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the pipe-to-platform shift /ðə paɪp-tɪ-ˈplætˌfɔrm ʃɪft/
    The pipe-to-platform shift trades making things for orchestrating those who do.
  • ▸ marketplace /ˈmɑrkɪtˌpleɪs/
    A marketplace is a platform whose product is other people's supply.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ build a platform
  • ▸ orchestrate an ecosystem
  • ▸ chicken-and-egg problem
  • ▸ platform envelopment

In the wild 语境例句

The most valuable firms increasingly own no inventory: they run platforms where others' supply meets the platform's demand.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

平台的启动难题是“先有鸡还是先有蛋”:没有商家就没有顾客，反之亦然——冷启动策略（补贴一侧、单边先行）是关键。
