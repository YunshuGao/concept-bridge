毕业教师表现评估（GTPA） bìyè jiàoshī biǎoxiàn pínggūthe Graduate Teacher Performance Assessment (GTPA)

🔊 Graduate Teacher Performance Assessment /ˈgræʤəˌweɪt ˈtiʧər pərˈfɔrməns əˈsɛsmənt/

澳大利亚职前教师在最后一年教育实习中完成的顶点评估（capstone）,要求以证据证明自己'具备入职课堂的能力'（classroom-ready）。它围绕一个完整教学循环的五项相互关联的实践——计划、教学、评估、反思、研判——展开,核心是证明教学对学生学习产生的影响。是全国认可的两大教师表现评估（TPA）之一。

A capstone assessment completed by preservice teachers during their final-year professional experience, requiring them to evidence that they are classroom-ready. It is built around five interrelated practices of a full teaching cycle — planning, teaching, assessing, reflecting and appraising — with the core aim of demonstrating the impact of teaching on student learning. One of the two nationally recognised Teaching Performance Assessments (TPAs).

Etymology 词源

capstone（拱顶石）——建筑收顶的最后一块石,喻教师资格的'收顶'评估;GTPA 由澳大利亚天主教大学 ILSTE 研发。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ a capstone assessment /ə ˈkæpˌstoʊn əˈsɛsmənt/
    A capstone assessment gathers a whole degree's learning into one culminating demonstration.
  • ▸ teaching performance assessment /ˈtiʧɪŋ pərˈfɔrməns əˈsɛsmənt/
    A teaching performance assessment judges what you can do in a classroom, not what you can recite.

In the wild 语境例句

The GTPA asks you to show, with evidence, that your teaching moved student learning — not that you planned a nice lesson, but that the lesson worked.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

GTPA 与 AfGT 是澳洲两大认证 TPA,各校择一;都对标 APST 毕业阶段(Graduate)。关键区别于普通实习报告:GTPA 要求'数据素养'与'对学习影响的研判',而非仅描述你做了什么。
