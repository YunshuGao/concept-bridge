宗法制 zōngfǎzhìthe patrilineal lineage system

⏳ 西周

与西周分封制互为表里的制度：以父系血缘为纽带，嫡长子继承制为核心（嫡长子为大宗、余子为小宗），解决统治阶级内部在权力和财产分配方面的矛盾；家国同构，政治权力分配与血缘关系相结合——对中国社会结构、家族观念影响绵长深远。

The twin of Zhou enfeoffment: descent-line law built on primogeniture — the eldest son by the principal wife heading the great line, younger sons founding lesser lines — allocating power and property inside the ruling clan and fusing family with state ('the state as family writ large'). Its residue outlived every dynasty: ancestral halls, lineage trusts, and the family-first grammar of Chinese social life.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

嫡长子继承制 = primogeniture（欧洲同制——[[fengjian]] 与 feudalism 比较的又一支点）。大宗小宗可译 the major/minor lines of descent。“家国同构”= the homology of family and state——理解中国政治文化的钥匙概念，英语汉学用 familism/family-state 讨论。
