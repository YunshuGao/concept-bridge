澳大利亚政制 àodàlìyà zhèngzhìAustralian politics: Westminster with compulsory voting

🔊 preferential /ˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/

威斯敏斯特体制的澳洲变体:强制投票（不投票罚款,投票率95%+）、偏好排序投票制（preferential voting）、参众两院、联邦制、总督代表王室;“民主香肠”（democracy sausage）为投票日文化图腾。

Westminster, antipodean edition: compulsory voting (fines for abstention, 95%+ turnout), preferential voting, two houses, federalism, a Governor-General for the Crown — and the democracy sausage as election-day liturgy.
