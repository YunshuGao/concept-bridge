责任逐步移交 zérèn zhúbù yíjiāothe gradual release of responsibility (I do / we do / you do)

🔊 the gradual release of responsibility /ðə ˈgræʤuəl riˈlis əv riˌspɑnsəˈbɪləti/

教学责任从教师逐步转移到学生的模式:①我做（I do,教师示范与出声思考）;②我们一起做（we do,师生共同练习、支架密集）;③你们做（you do together,同伴协作）→你独立做（you do alone,独立应用）。它是显性教学的骨架,确保学生在支架撤除前已具备能力。

A model shifting responsibility from teacher to student in stages: (1) I do (the teacher models with think-alouds); (2) we do (guided practice together, heavily scaffolded); (3) you do together (peer collaboration) → you do alone (independent application). It is the skeleton of explicit teaching, ensuring students are capable before the scaffold is removed.

Etymology 词源

release（松手、放开）——把学习责任'逐步松手'交给学生;由 Pearson 与 Gallagher(1983)提出。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ I do, we do, you do /aɪ du wi du ju du/
    'I do, we do, you do' hands the work over only as fast as competence grows.
  • ▸ modelled to independent practice /ˈmɒdəld tɪ ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt ˈpræktɪs/
    Modelled to independent practice withdraws support in step with rising skill.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ I do, we do, you do
  • ▸ model, guide, release
  • ▸ withdraw the scaffold
  • ▸ guided to independent practice

In the wild 语境例句

Teaching a loop, you code one aloud (I do), write the next together (we do), then set students their own (you do) — releasing responsibility only as they're ready.
