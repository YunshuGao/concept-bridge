🔊 Porter's Five Forces /ˈpɔːrtərz faɪv ˈfɔrsɪz/

波特的行业结构分析框架:一个行业的长期盈利能力由五种力量决定——现有竞争者的对抗、新进入者威胁、替代品威胁、买方议价力、供方议价力。战略的第一课:选对行业往往比经营得好更重要。

Porter's industry-structure lens: long-run profitability is set by five forces — rivalry, threat of entry, threat of substitutes, buyer power and supplier power. Strategy's first lesson: which industry you're in often matters more than how well you run.

Etymology 词源

force 源自拉丁 fortis（强壮）——“力”即强度；波特1979年《哈佛商业评论》成文，把产业组织经济学翻译成经理人语言。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ industry structure /ˈɪndəstri ˈstrəkʧər/
    Industry structure explains why airlines starve and software feasts.
  • ▸ structural analysis /ˈstrəkʧərəl æˈnælɪsɪs/
    Structural analysis asks not who is winning but why winning is possible at all.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ run a five-forces analysis
  • ▸ an attractive/unattractive industry
  • ▸ structural profitability
  • ▸ the forces are shifting

In the wild 语境例句

Before betting on a market, a partner runs the five forces: strong buyers and easy entry mean margins will bleed no matter how good the operator.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

五力分析行业不分析公司——它解释行业为何赚钱，不解释某家公司为何领先（后者靠 competitive-advantage）。静态框架，须配合动态变化使用。
