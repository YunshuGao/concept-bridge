🔊 NESA and teacher accreditation /ˈniːsə ənd ˈtiʧər əˌkrɛdəˈteɪʃən/

新南威尔士教育标准局（NESA）是本州教师认证、课程大纲（syllabus）与高考（HSC）的主管机构。所有教师须经NESA认证方可执教,并按APST从毕业阶段晋升至熟练阶段、持续维持认证（专业发展学时）。它把全国标准落地为本州的法定要求。

The NSW Education Standards Authority (NESA) is the state body governing teacher accreditation, syllabuses and the HSC. All teachers must be NESA-accredited to teach, progressing from the Graduate to the Proficient stage under the APST and maintaining accreditation through professional development hours. It turns the national standards into the state's statutory requirements.
