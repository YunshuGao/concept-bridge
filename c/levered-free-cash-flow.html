有杠杆自由现金流 yǒu gànggǎn zìyóu xiànjīnliúlevered free cash flow (FCFE)

🔊 levered free cash flow /ˈlɛvərd fri kæʃ floʊ/

扣除利息与偿债后、真正归属股东的现金流:净利润+折旧摊销−资本开支−营运资本增加−强制还本+净新增借款。它用股权成本贴现,直接得股权价值。FCFF得企业价值、FCFE得股权价值——用错贴现率或现金流口径是新手大忌。

The cash flow that remains for shareholders after interest and debt repayment: net income + D&A − capex − increase in working capital − mandatory debt repayment + net new borrowing. It is discounted at the cost of equity to give equity value directly. FCFF yields enterprise value, FCFE yields equity value — mismatching the discount rate to the cash flow is a classic rookie error.

Etymology 词源

levered（加杠杆的）——已考虑债务(利息、还本)后的现金流;FCFE=Free Cash Flow to Equity（归属股东的）。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ free cash flow to equity /fri kæʃ floʊ tɪ ˈɛkwəti/
    Free cash flow to equity is what's left for owners after the lenders are paid.
  • ▸ cash available to shareholders /kæʃ əˈveɪləbəl tɪ ˈʃɛˌrhoʊldərz/
    Cash available to shareholders discounts at the cost of equity, not WACC.

In the wild 语境例句

For a bank, where debt is raw material, analysts value equity directly with levered FCFE and the cost of equity rather than the unlevered FCFF approach.
