全面质量管理 quánmiàn zhìliàng guǎnlǐtotal quality management (TQM)

🔊 total quality management /ˈtoʊtəl kˈwɑləti ˈmænɪʤmənt/

戴明等人开创的管理哲学:质量是全员、全流程、全周期的持续追求,而非事后检验。核心信念——质量提升同时降低成本（返工与浪费减少）,并以顾客满意为终极标尺。它奠定了日本制造的崛起。

A management philosophy from Deming and others: quality is a continuous pursuit by everyone, across every process and stage — not inspection after the fact. Its core belief is that improving quality lowers cost (less rework and waste) while customer satisfaction is the ultimate yardstick. It underpinned the rise of Japanese manufacturing.

Etymology 词源

total（全面的）——质量是'全员、全过程'之事;Deming 的思想在战后日本落地,反哺全球。
