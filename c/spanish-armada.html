无敌舰队 Wúdí jiànduìthe Spanish Armada

⏳ 1588年

16世纪西班牙为保障海上交通与海外利益所建舰队：百余艘战舰、三千余门炮、数万士兵，自称“无敌”。但战舰巨大笨重，1588年英吉利海峡（加莱）海战中被船小速快炮多的英国海军以少胜多击败——英西实力对比逆转，英国渐成海上霸主。

Spain's grand fleet — over a hundred ships, three thousand guns, tens of thousands of men, styled 'invincible' — was outsailed and outgunned in the Channel in 1588 by England's smaller, faster, better-armed ships: the defeat that announced England's rise to naval mastery.
