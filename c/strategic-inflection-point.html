战略转折点 zhànlüè zhuǎnzhédiǎnthe strategic inflection point

🔊 strategic inflection point /strəˈtiʤɪk ˌɪnˈflɛkʃən pɔɪnt/

格鲁夫:某种10倍级的力量（技术、监管、客户行为）根本改变行业基础规则的时刻——旧战略突然失效，企业要么及时转向，要么衰落。“唯有偏执狂才能生存。”

Andy Grove's term for the moment a 10x force (technology, regulation, customer behaviour) fundamentally changes an industry's rules — the old strategy suddenly fails, and a firm must pivot in time or decline. 'Only the paranoid survive.'

Etymology 词源

inflection point 借自微积分（曲线凹凸转变之点）——格鲁夫把数学术语搬入管理，指趋势方向突变的时刻。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ a 10x force /ə ɛks fɔrs/
    A 10x force is change so large that adapting slowly is the same as not adapting.
  • ▸ the pivot /ðə ˈpɪvət/
    The pivot is what a strategic inflection point demands and inertia resists.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ reach an inflection point
  • ▸ a 10x change
  • ▸ pivot in time
  • ▸ only the paranoid survive

In the wild 语境例句

The shift to smartphones was a strategic inflection point: firms that treated it as a sustaining trend, not a rule-change, are footnotes now.
