⏳ 学科体系

书库教师专业书群的共同知识底座：认知负荷理论（工作记忆有限——Willingham《为什么学生不喜欢上学》：记忆是思考的残留物）；Rosenshine 教学原则（小步子、大量提问、示范、检查理解、独立练习高成功率）；显性教学（Explicit Instruction, Archer）；Lemov《教无不胜》63技（冷不丁点名 cold call、不容退出 no opt out）；课堂行为管理（Bennett《掌控教室》：常规先于关系）；文化响应教学（Hammond：高期待+脚手架）；伍尔福克《教育心理学》总纲。NSW 教师职业语汇的原文层。

The shared evidence base of your professional shelf: cognitive load theory and Willingham's 'memory is the residue of thought'; Rosenshine's principles (small steps, many checks, models, high-success practice); explicit instruction (Archer); Lemov's 63 techniques (cold call, no opt out); Bennett's behaviour-first classroom (routines before relationships); Hammond's culturally responsive teaching (high expectations plus scaffolds); Woolfolk's Ed Psych as the umbrella. This is the working English of NSW staffrooms and PD days — your trade's terminology at the source.
