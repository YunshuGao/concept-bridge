社会主义核心价值观 shèhuìzhǔyì héxīn jiàzhíguānthe core socialist values

⏳ 2012年至今

国家层面：富强、民主、文明、和谐；社会层面：自由、平等、公正、法治；个人层面：爱国、敬业、诚信、友善——十二个词二十四字，是当代中国精神的集中体现，凝结着全体人民共同的价值追求；回答了建设什么样的国家、什么样的社会、培育什么样的公民的重大问题。要内化于心、外化于行，与中华优秀传统文化相承接（讲仁爱、重民本、守诚信、崇正义、尚和合、求大同）。

The twelve-word canon on every school wall and construction hoarding, tiered by level: prosperity, democracy, civility, harmony (nation); freedom, equality, justice, rule of law (society); patriotism, dedication, integrity, friendliness (citizen). Framed as the distillation of 'the contemporary Chinese spirit', continuous with the traditional virtues ([[chinese-culture-core]]'s benevolence, people-first, good faith, harmony), and — the observation every visiting journalist makes — including 'democracy' and 'freedom' on their own official definitions: the value vocabulary whose translation is never innocent.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

官方译文固定：prosperity, democracy, civility, harmony; freedom, equality, justice, the rule of law; patriotism, dedication, integrity, friendship——各词的官方义与西方义的错位（democracy=whole-process 义）正是阅读练习。“和合”“大同”承 [[chinese-culture-core]]、《礼记》天下为公（链 [[xia-dynasty]] 的“大道之行”）。
