现金流质量 xiànjīnliú zhìliàngcash flow quality

🔊 cash flow quality /kæʃ floʊ kˈwɑləti/

最难被操纵的报表是现金流量表。核心检验:经营现金流(CFO)是否持续跟得上净利润?'应计比率'(净利润远超CFO)、CFO靠出售应收款或压票据周转做高、把经营支出塞进投资活动——这些都是盈利质量恶化的信号。现金是事实,利润是观点。

The hardest statement to manipulate is the cash flow statement. The core test: does operating cash flow (CFO) keep pace with net income over time? A high accruals ratio (net income far above CFO), CFO propped up by factoring receivables or stretching payables, or operating costs shoved into investing activities — all signal deteriorating earnings quality. Cash is a fact, profit an opinion.

Etymology 词源

quality（质量）——现金流的'质量'即其可信度与可持续性;与'盈利质量'互为镜像。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the accruals ratio /ðə əˈkruəlz ˈreɪʃiˌoʊ/

  • ▸ cash conversion /kæʃ kənˈvərʒən/
    Poor cash conversion means the profit exists on paper but never reaches the bank.
