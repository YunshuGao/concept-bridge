病毒式传播 bìngdú shì chuánbōvirality (the K-factor)

🔊 virality /vaɪˈrælɪti/

衡量用户自发带来新用户的能力:K因子=每个用户平均发出的邀请数×邀请转化率。K>1 意味着指数式自增长（每个用户带来不止一个新用户），是零获客成本增长的圣杯，但极少数产品能持续实现。

A measure of how effectively users spontaneously bring in new users: the K-factor = invitations sent per user × conversion rate of those invitations. K > 1 means exponential self-growth (each user brings more than one), the holy grail of zero-CAC growth — which very few products sustain.

Etymology 词源

viral 源自 virus（拉丁'毒液、黏液'）——传播如病毒般人传人;K因子借自流行病学的基本传染数 R0。
