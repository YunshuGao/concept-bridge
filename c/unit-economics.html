单位经济模型 dānwèi jīngjì móxíngunit economics

🔊 unit economics /ˈjunɪt ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪks/

把商业模式还原到单个客户或单笔交易层面的盈亏:一个客户带来多少收入与利润（LTV），获取和服务它花多少（CAC 与服务成本）。若单个单位不赚钱，规模只会放大亏损——增长掩盖不了破碎的单位经济。

Reducing a business model to the profit or loss of a single customer or transaction: the revenue and profit one customer brings (LTV) versus the cost to acquire and serve it (CAC and cost-to-serve). If the unit doesn't make money, scale only magnifies the loss — growth cannot paper over broken unit economics.

Etymology 词源

unit（单位）——把整个生意拆到'一个客户/一单'的最小盈利单元来审视；是精益创业与 SaaS 分析的核心。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ per-customer profit /pər-ˈkəstəmər ˈprɑfɪt/
    Per-customer profit is the truth a billion-dollar valuation sometimes buries.
  • ▸ the LTV/CAC ratio /ðə ˌɛltiːˈviː kæk ˈreɪʃiˌoʊ/
    The LTV/CAC ratio is unit economics compressed into a single verdict.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ profitable unit economics
  • ▸ LTV over CAC
  • ▸ does the unit make money?
  • ▸ scale amplifies the unit

In the wild 语境例句

A delivery startup grew explosively while losing money on every order — its unit economics were negative, so each new customer deepened the hole.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

'规模会解决亏损'的说法只在单位经济为正时成立:若每单亏损，规模只放大亏损。识破'烧钱换增长'骗局的第一问就是单位经济。
