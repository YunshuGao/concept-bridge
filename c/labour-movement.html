工人运动的兴起 gōngrén yùndòngthe rise of the labour movement

also: the workers' movement · Chartism

⏳ 19世纪30–40年代

工业革命使资产阶级愈富而工人待遇很差；19世纪三四十年代工人为改善劳动生活条件、提高政治地位积极斗争：法国里昂工人起义、英国宪章运动、德国西里西亚织工起义——工人阶级开始作为独立政治力量登上历史舞台，迫切需要革命理论指导。

Industrialisation enriched owners and ground workers; in the 1830s–40s labour fought back — the Lyon silk-weavers' risings, Britain's Chartist movement for the vote, the Silesian weavers' revolt — the working class stepping onto the political stage as a force of its own, in search of a theory.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“宪章运动”= Chartism/the Chartist movement（因《人民宪章》the People's Charter 得名，与《大宪章》呼应）。“工会”= trade union（英）/labor union（美）；“罢工”= strike；“集体谈判”= collective bargaining——劳工报道核心词汇群。
