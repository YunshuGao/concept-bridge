豆豆小说（文化属性与见路不走） Dòudòu xiǎoshuōDoudou's novels: cultural attributes and 'seeing the road, not walking it'

🔊 first principles /fərst ˈprɪnsəpəlz/

⏳ 2005年/2013年

豆豆“天道三部曲”中的两部：《遥远的救世主》（电视剧《天道》原著）提出“文化属性”——强势文化造就强者、弱势文化期待救世主（“靠”的文化）；扶贫王庙村的“杀富济贫”实验演绎市场规律与救世主悖论：真正的救世主是实事求是的规律本身。《天幕红尘》以“见路不走”立论——不住一法、不拘经验教条，条件许可什么就做什么，是“实事求是”的个体化表述（叶子农：“见路不走就是实事求是”）。商界读者奉为思维小说，其概念已成中文互联网流行分析框架。

Two of Doudou's cult 'novels of ideas': The Distant Saviour (adapted as the TV drama Tian Dao) coins 'cultural attributes' — strong cultures produce agency, weak ones wait for saviours — and runs a poverty-alleviation experiment to its merciless conclusion: the only saviour is reality's own law. Its sequel-in-spirit Tianmu Hongchen turns on 'see the road, don't walk it' (jianlu buzou): cling to no method, copy no precedent, do what conditions permit — the novelistic translation, the hero says outright, of 'seek truth from facts'. Business China reads them as strategy scripture; the vocabulary saturates Chinese internet analysis.
