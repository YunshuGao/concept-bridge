MECE 原则 MECE yuánzéMECE (mutually exclusive, collectively exhaustive)

🔊 MECE /ˈmiːsiː/

麦肯锡分析基石:把一个问题拆成若干子项，彼此不重叠（互斥）、合起来无遗漏（穷尽）。MECE 的拆分让分析既不重复劳动，也不留死角，是结构化思考的第一纪律。

McKinsey's analytic bedrock: break a problem into parts that don't overlap (mutually exclusive) and together miss nothing (collectively exhaustive). A MECE cut ensures analysis neither double-counts nor leaves gaps — the first discipline of structured thinking.

Etymology 词源

由 Barbara Minto 在麦肯锡提出的缩略语（读作 'mee-see'）——结构化沟通与分析的核心检验。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ clean decomposition /klin ˌdikəmpəˈzɪʃən/
    A clean decomposition is one where every item has exactly one home.
  • ▸ no gaps, no overlaps /noʊ gæps noʊ ˈoʊvərˌlæps/
    'No gaps, no overlaps' is MECE translated into plain English.

In the wild 语境例句

Splitting customers by 'young, old, and loyal' fails MECE — the buckets overlap; 'under 30 / 30-plus' passes.
