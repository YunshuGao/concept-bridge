宋元都市文化与宋词元曲 Sòngcí YuánqǔSong-Yuan urban culture: ci poetry and zaju drama

⏳ 960年–1368年

开封临安大都为世界著名大都市：大相国寺庙会万人，瓦子（娱乐兼营商业场所）勾栏（演出圈子）说书唱曲杂剧蹴鞠；市民阶层壮大、文化生活丰富；春节元宵中秋等节俗至今流传。词为可歌的长短句，宋代大盛：苏轼豪迈飘逸扩词之境；李清照委婉细腻、南渡后血泪忧患；辛弃疾承豪放而气势磅礴。元曲（散曲杂剧南戏）综合歌舞念白，关汉卿《窦娥冤》以浪漫手法鞭挞黑暗，与马致远郑光祖白朴并称“元曲四大家”。张择端《清明上河图》绘汴京繁华；司马光《资治通鉴》编年通史，与《史记》并耀、“史学两司马”。

Song cities invented commercial popular culture: the washe pleasure precincts and their railed goulan stages — storytellers, puppeteers, football artists, opera troupes — playing nightly to a paying urban public, while festivals took the shapes they keep today. Poetry moved with the money: the ci lyric, written to popular tunes, ran from Su Shi's cosmic bravado through Li Qingzhao's exile-sharpened grief to Xin Qiji's sword-rattling splendour; under the Yuan, drama took over, Guan Hanqing's Injustice to Dou E making the stage a court of appeal against corrupt power. Zhang Zeduan's Qingming scroll painted the boom; Sima Guang's Comprehensive Mirror chronicled 1,300 years to instruct it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

词 = ci (lyric) poetry；元杂剧 = zaju (variety drama)。瓦子勾栏可释 entertainment quarters and theatre railings——宋代都市娱乐业与 [[medieval-towns]] 同期欧洲城市的对比极富张力（开封百万人口 vs 伦敦4万）。《资治通鉴》= the Comprehensive Mirror in Aid of Governance；“鉴”的政治史学观（history as mirror）是中式史学的自我定义。
