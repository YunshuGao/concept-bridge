🔊 margin of safety /ˈmɑːrdʒɪn ʌv ˈseɪfti/

豪泽尔:理财是行为问题而非知识问题。核心词——尾部事件（tails drive everything:极少数决定绝大部分回报）、“够了”（enough）、留足冗余（margin of safety）、财富是你看不见的那部分（wealth is what you don't see）。

Doing well with money is behaviour, not knowledge. Tails drive everything; know what 'enough' means; keep a margin of safety; wealth is the cars not bought — what you don't see.

Etymology 词源

enough 古英语 genōg——日耳曼老词;凯瑟琳·海勒对约瑟夫·海勒说“我们有 enough”——豪泽尔全书以这则轶事定调,词根即主题。
