先例交易分析（实操） xiānlì jiāoyì fēnxī shícāoprecedent transactions (mechanics)

🔊 precedent transactions mechanics /ˈprɛsɪdənt trænˈzækʃənz məˈkænɪks/

用过往同类并购的成交倍数给目标定价的实操:①搜集近年可比行业的并购交易(交易数据库);②算出各交易的隐含EV/EBITDA等倍数,含控制权溢价;③剔除极端与市况异常的交易;④应用中位数。因含协议控制权溢价,倍数通常高于二级市场comps,常用于并购卖方定价的下限参考。

The hands-on use of past comparable M&A deal multiples to price a target: (1) gather recent industry deals (transaction databases); (2) compute each deal's implied EV/EBITDA and other multiples, control premium included; (3) exclude outliers and deals struck in abnormal markets; (4) apply the median. Because these embed a control premium, multiples usually run above public comps — often a floor reference in sell-side M&A pricing.
