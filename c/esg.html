ESG（环境、社会、治理） ESGESG (environmental, social, governance)

🔊 ESG /ˌiːɛsˈdʒiː/

从环境、社会责任、公司治理三维度评估企业可持续性与非财务风险的框架。支持者视其为长期风险管理与价值创造;批评者指其标准混乱、易沦为'漂绿'、并牵扯政治。无论立场,它已深刻影响资本配置与企业战略。

A framework for assessing a company's sustainability and non-financial risk across three dimensions: environmental, social and governance. Supporters see it as long-term risk management and value creation; critics point to inconsistent standards, 'greenwashing' and political entanglement. Either way, it now shapes capital allocation and corporate strategy profoundly.

Etymology 词源

Environmental/Social/Governance 首字母缩略——2004年联合国报告普及,成为可持续投资的通用语。
