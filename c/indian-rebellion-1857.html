印度民族大起义 Yìndù mínzú dàqǐyìthe Indian Rebellion of 1857

🔊 mutiny /mˈjutəni/

also: the Sepoy Mutiny (dated) · the First War of Independence (Indian usage)

⏳ 1857年–1858年

19世纪中期印度沦为英国殖民地：英国棉纺织品倾销摧毁印度发达的手工棉纺织业，殖民者兼并王公领地（无嗣即归英国）、虐待“土兵”并不尊重其宗教信仰。1857年土兵率先起义，农民、手工业者与被夺权的王公响应，席卷印度北部中部，夺取德里；章西女王率军激战、壮烈牺牲。起义沉重打击英国殖民者，反映印度民族意识觉醒；英国政府随之取消东印度公司统治权（1858年撤销该公司），改为直接统治印度。

By mid-century Britain had beggared India's famous handloom industry with machine-made cloth, annexed princely states whenever a ruler died heirless, and abused its sepoy soldiers' pay and faith. In 1857 the sepoys rose, joined by peasants, artisans and dispossessed princes; Delhi fell to them, and the Rani of Jhansi died leading her troops. The rising was crushed, but it ended Company rule — from 1858 the British Crown governed India directly — and it announced the awakening of Indian national feeling.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“土兵”= sepoy（波斯语借词，英印军队的印度士兵）。命名即立场：英方旧称 the Sepoy Mutiny（兵变），现代学界中性称 the Indian Rebellion/Uprising of 1857，印度官方称第一次独立战争。“无嗣归英”政策专名 the doctrine of lapse。1858年后的英属印度称 the (British) Raj——Economist 关于印度的报道常用。
