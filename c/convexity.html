凸性 tūxìngconvexity

🔊 convexity /kənˈvɛksɪti/

债券价格-收益率关系的曲率,是久期(线性近似)的二阶修正。因为这条曲线是凸的,久期会高估收益率上升时的跌幅、低估下降时的涨幅——凸性对债券持有者有利:大跌少一点、大涨多一点。利率大幅波动时,只看久期会算错。

The curvature of a bond's price-yield relationship — the second-order correction to duration (a linear approximation). Because the curve is convex, duration overstates the price fall when yields rise and understates the rise when they fall — convexity works in the bondholder's favour: less downside, more upside on big moves. Ignore it and you misprice bonds when rates swing sharply.

Etymology 词源

convex 源自拉丁 convexus（拱起的、弯曲的）——凸性即价格-收益率曲线'向上拱起'的弧度。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the curvature correction /ðə ˈkərvəʧər kərˈɛkʃən/
    The curvature correction is why duration alone misprices a bond in a big rate move.
  • ▸ positive convexity /ˈpɑzətɪv kənˈvɛksɪti/
    Positive convexity means gains accelerate and losses decelerate — a free option for the holder.

In the wild 语境例句

For a large rate shock, an analyst adds a convexity adjustment to the duration estimate — duration alone would overstate the loss and understate the gain.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

负凸性(如可赎回债、MBS)反过来伤害持有者:利率下降时价格涨幅被'封顶'(发行人赎回)。凸性是久期的必要补充,尤其在利率剧烈波动时。
