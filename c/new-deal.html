罗斯福新政 Luósīfú xīnzhèngthe New Deal

⏳ 1933年–1939年

1933年罗斯福就任即宣布新政，采用国家干预手段扭转经济：整顿金融（《紧急银行法案》）；《全国工业复兴法》制定公平竞争法规、定最低工资限工时、保障工人谈判权；《农业调整法》限产稳价；“以工代赈”兴建水库公路等公共设施；《社会保障法》建立社会保障制度。经济缓慢复苏，增强政府宏观调控能力、恢复人民信心，对资本主义世界影响深远——但未改变资本主义本质、无法解决根本矛盾。

Taking office in 1933, FDR answered the slump with state intervention: banks reopened under the Emergency Banking Act, industry codes fixed minimum wages and hours and secured collective bargaining, farm output was managed to steady prices, public works from dams to parks put the jobless to work, and the Social Security Act built a safety net. Recovery came slowly; what endured was the enlarged state — a policy revolution within capitalism, not against it, as the textbook takes care to note.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“国家干预”= state/government intervention；“宏观调控”= macroeconomic management（中文政策词，英译需意译）。“以工代赈”= work relief/public works programmes。罗斯福名句 The only thing we have to fear is fear itself 与“炉边谈话”fireside chats 均为英语政治修辞的标准库存。凯恩斯主义 Keynesianism 是理解新政的英语理论框架（教材未点名）。
