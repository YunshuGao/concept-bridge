平台包抄 píngtái bāochāoplatform envelopment

🔊 platform envelopment /ˈplætˌfɔrm ɪnˈvɛləpmənt/

一个平台利用其现有用户基础与共享数据,进入并吞并相邻平台的市场——如支付平台切入借贷、社交平台切入电商。它是'范围经济×网络效应'的进攻性运用,也是大型科技平台不断扩张、引发反垄断关注的核心机制。

A platform leverages its existing user base and shared data to enter and absorb an adjacent platform's market — a payments platform moving into lending, a social platform into commerce. It is the aggressive use of scope economies × network effects, and the core mechanism behind big tech platforms' relentless expansion and the antitrust scrutiny it draws.

Etymology 词源

envelop（包围,源自古法语 enveloper 包裹）——平台'包抄、吞并'邻近市场,故名。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ bundling into adjacencies /ˈbəndəlɪŋ ˈɪntu əˈdʒeɪsənsiz/
    Bundling into adjacencies lets a platform enter a market with users it already owns.
  • ▸ the aggregator's advance /ðə aggregator*'z ədˈvæns/
    The aggregator's advance turns one dominant platform into many.
