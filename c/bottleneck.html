瓶颈 píngjǐngthe bottleneck

🔊 bottleneck /ˈbɑtəlˌnɛk/

限制整个流程产能的最狭窄环节——如瓶子的颈部限制倒出速度。系统的最大产出永远等于瓶颈的产出;非瓶颈环节的闲置或加速都不改变全局。找到并扩宽瓶颈,是提升产出最有效的单点干预。

The narrowest stage that limits a whole process's capacity — like a bottle's neck limiting the pour. A system's maximum output always equals the bottleneck's output; idle time or speed-ups elsewhere change nothing. Finding and widening the bottleneck is the single most effective intervention to raise output.

Etymology 词源

bottle（瓶）+ neck（颈）——瓶颈限制流出速度,喻限制系统产能的最窄环节。
