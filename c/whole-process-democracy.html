人民民主专政与全过程人民民主 quánguòchéng rénmín mínzhǔpeople's democratic dictatorship and whole-process people's democracy

🔊 consultation /ˌkɑnsəlˈteɪʃən/

⏳ 现行制度

我国国体：工人阶级领导的、以工农联盟为基础的人民民主专政的社会主义国家——对人民实行民主、对少数敌对分子实行专政；本质是人民当家作主。全过程人民民主是社会主义民主政治的本质属性：全链条（民主选举、协商、决策、管理、监督）、全方位、全覆盖，是最广泛、最真实、最管用的民主——不仅有完整的制度程序（人大、政协、基层自治等），而且有完整的参与实践；民主是各国人民的权利而不是少数国家的专利，一个国家民主不民主关键在于人民是否真正当家作主。

The state's official nature: a people's democratic dictatorship — democracy among the people, dictatorship over its enemies, worker-led on a worker-peasant alliance — whose essence is 'the people as masters'. Its current elaboration is whole-process people's democracy (2019-): democracy claimed as full-chain (election, consultation, decision-making, management, oversight), 'the broadest, most genuine, most effective', with the pointed corollary aimed westward: democracy is every people's right, not a few countries' patent, and the test is outcomes, not form. This is the counter-narrative to 'democracy v. autocracy' framing — quotable, contestable, and unavoidable in current affairs.
