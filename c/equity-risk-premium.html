股权风险溢价 gǔquán fēngxiǎn yìjiàthe equity risk premium

🔊 equity risk premium /ˈɛkwəti rɪsk ˈprimiəm/

投资股市相对无风险国债所要求的额外回报——补偿承担股票风险的代价。历史上约4–6%，但前瞻估计争议大。它是所有股权估值的隐形地基:溢价假设变一个百分点，估值天翻地覆。

The extra return investors demand for holding equities over risk-free government bonds — compensation for bearing stock risk. Historically around 4–6%, though forward estimates are contentious. It is the hidden foundation of all equity valuation: shift the premium by a point and valuations lurch.

Etymology 词源

premium 源自拉丁 praemium（奖赏、酬报）——风险溢价即“承担风险应得的酬报”。
