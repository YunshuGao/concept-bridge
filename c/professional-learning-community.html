专业学习共同体（PLC） zhuānyè xuéxí gòngtóngtǐthe professional learning community (PLC)

🔊 the professional learning community /ðə prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈlərnɪŋ kəmˈjunɪti/

教师定期协作、共同探究如何提升学生学习的团队实践:分享数据与作品、共同备课与评分调节、互相观课、循证改进。它把教学从孤立的个人劳动转为集体专业学习,呼应APST标准7(与同事专业互动)。'没有人比我们所有人更聪明'——PLC是教师持续成长最有力的组织形式之一。

A team practice where teachers collaborate regularly, jointly inquiring into how to improve student learning: sharing data and work, planning and moderating together, observing each other, improving on evidence. It turns teaching from isolated individual labour into collective professional learning, echoing APST Standard 7 (engaging professionally with colleagues). 'None of us is as smart as all of us' — the PLC is among the most powerful organisational forms for teachers' ongoing growth.

Etymology 词源

community 源自拉丁 communis（共同）——专业'共同体'即以共同探究为纽带的教师团队。

  • ▸ collective efficacy /kəˈlɛktɪv ˈɛfɪkəsi/
