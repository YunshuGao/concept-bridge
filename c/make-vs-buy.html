自制还是外购 zìzhì háishì wàigòuthe make-vs-buy decision

🔊 make versus buy /meɪk ˈvərsəz baɪ/

企业边界的基本抉择:某活动应内部自制还是市场外购？取决于交易成本、战略重要性、能力、控制需求与灵活性。核心能力自制，非核心外包，是通行原则。

The fundamental boundary choice: should an activity be done in-house or bought from the market? It turns on transaction costs, strategic importance, capability, control and flexibility. Make the core, buy the rest is the working rule.

Etymology 词源

这是科斯《企业的性质》(1937) 的实践版:企业存在，正因为某些交易在内部完成比走市场更便宜。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the firm's boundary /ðə fərmz ˈbaʊndəri/
    Every make-vs-buy call redraws the firm's boundary by one activity.
