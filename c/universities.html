中世纪大学 zhōngshìjì dàxuéthe medieval university

⏳ 12世纪起

12世纪西欧教育学术的新气象，被誉为中世纪教育“最美好的花朵”。“大学”（universitas）本义即行会——由教师或学生组成的自保团体：博洛尼亚为学生社团主导，巴黎为教师行会。大学享免赋税、司法、教育自主三特权；课程先修文法、修辞、逻辑、算术、几何、天文、音乐，再攻法学、医学、神学——既受教会影响，也反映经济社会发展要求。

The 'fairest flower' of medieval education, born in the 12th century as a guild — universitas — of teachers or students protecting their own: student-run Bologna, master-run Paris. Universities won exemption from taxes, their own courts and freedom to teach; students climbed from the seven liberal arts — grammar, rhetoric, logic, arithmetic, geometry, astronomy, music — to law, medicine or theology, a curriculum shaped by the Church yet answering a commercialising society.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

universitas 本义“团体/行会”——“大学=学者行会”是理解其自治传统的钥匙。七艺 = the seven liberal arts（前三 trivium、后四 quadrivium；trivial“琐碎的”即源自 trivium 之“初级”）。“学术自由/大学自治”= academic freedom / university autonomy；城镇与大学的紧张关系英语固定说法 town and gown。
