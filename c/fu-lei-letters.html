《傅雷家书》 fùléi jiāshūFu Lei's family letters

🔊 cultivation /ˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/

翻译家傅雷致钢琴家傅聪的家书:艺术修养、做人先于做艺术家、“赤子之心”——中文家教书信的正典。傅雷亦是罗曼·罗兰《约翰·克利斯朵夫》的经典中译者:家书与译笔同一血脉。

The translator Fu Lei's letters to his pianist son Fu Cong: artistic cultivation, being a person before being an artist, the pure heart (赤子之心). Fu Lei also gave Chinese its classic Jean-Christophe — the letters and the translations share one bloodstream.

Etymology 词源

赤子之心 出《孟子》——英译常作 a child's pure heart / the heart of a newborn;candour(坦诚)源自拉丁 candor(纯白之光),与“赤子”意象暗合。

Nuance 陷阱与辨析
