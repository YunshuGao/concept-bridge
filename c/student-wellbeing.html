🔊 student wellbeing and duty of care /ˈstudənt wɛlˈbiːɪŋ ənd ˈduti əv kɛr/

教师负有法律与专业上的'关护义务'（duty of care）——采取合理措施保护学生免受可预见的伤害,涵盖身体安全、心理健康与儿童保护（强制报告）。学生福祉是有效学习的前提:马斯洛在前,布鲁姆在后——基本安全与归属未满足,认知学习难以发生。APST标准4的核心。

Teachers hold a legal and professional duty of care — taking reasonable steps to protect students from foreseeable harm, spanning physical safety, mental health and child protection (mandatory reporting). Wellbeing is the precondition for effective learning: Maslow before Bloom — without basic safety and belonging, cognitive learning struggles to happen. Central to APST Standard 4.

Etymology 词源

duty 源自古法语 deu（应尽的,拉丁 debere 亏欠）——'关护义务'即教师亏欠学生的照护责任。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ Maslow before Bloom /ˈmæzloʊ ˌbiˈfɔr blum/
    Maslow before Bloom: a frightened or hungry child cannot climb the cognitive ladder.
  • ▸ mandatory reporting /ˈmændəˌtɔri rɪˈpɔrtɪŋ/
    Mandatory reporting makes child protection a legal duty, not a discretionary kindness.
