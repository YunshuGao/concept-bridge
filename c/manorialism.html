西欧庄园 Xī'ōu zhuāngyuánthe manor (manorialism)

also: the manorial system

⏳ 9世纪–15世纪

9世纪起流行、11世纪遍布欧洲的农业经济组织：领主统治下独立的自给自足经济政治单位。耕地分领主“自营地”（收入归领主）与佃户“份地”（以耕种自营地为条件，通常每周服劳役三天）；周围林地荒地为全体佃户共用的“共用地”。

The self-sufficient economic and political cell of the medieval countryside, universal by the 11th century: the lord's demesne, worked by tenants as the price of their own plots (typically three days' labour a week), ringed by common land — woods, waste and pasture — shared by all.

In the wild 语境例句

Company towns are manorialism with smokestacks: one landlord, tied labour, and a store you cannot refuse to use.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

术语对照：庄园 = manor；庄园制 = manorialism（经济组织），与 feudalism（政治军事关系）相区分——中文教材两者都在“封建”名下，英语必须分开。自营地 = demesne（读 /dɪˈmeɪn/）；份地 = the tenants' holdings/strips；自给自足 = self-sufficient/subsistence economy。
