纳瓦尔宝典 nàwǎěrNaval Ravikant: leverage and specific knowledge

🔊 leverage /ˈlɛvərɪdʒ/

硅谷天使投资人的财富与幸福语录:特定知识（specific knowledge,无法培训他人替代的知识）;三种杠杆——劳动力、资本、以及零边际成本的复制杠杆（代码与媒体）;“靠出租时间无法致富”。

Naval's almanack: specific knowledge (what can't be trained into a replacement), and three kinds of leverage — labour, capital, and the permissionless kind with zero marginal cost: code and media. You won't get rich renting out your time.

Etymology 词源

leverage 源自 lever（杠杆）← 拉丁 levare（举起）——金融杠杆、政治筹码、人生杠杆共用一词,阿基米德的支点隐喻活在华尔街。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ permissionless /pərˈmɪʃənləs/
    Code and media are permissionless leverage: no one has to hire you.
  • ▸ specific knowledge /spəˈsɪfɪk ˈnɒlɪdʒ/
    Specific knowledge is found by following genuine curiosity, not job listings.

In the wild 语境例句

One teacher with a tutorial site out-leverages a department: Naval's media leverage, demonstrated at yunshugao.github.io.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

与《单干》《make.》同题异语:超级个体=specific knowledge×permissionless leverage——个人网站、课程、开源皆是复制杠杆;此节点即本知识库的自我说明。
