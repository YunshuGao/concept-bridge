盈利质量 yínglì zhìliàngquality of earnings

🔊 quality of earnings /kˈwɑləti əv ˈərnɪŋz/

判断报告利润的'含金量':是否由可持续的核心业务、真实现金支撑，还是靠一次性收益、激进会计、盈余管理粉饰。高质量盈利与现金流同步、可重复、不依赖估计。尽职调查与投资分析的核心工作。

Judging how 'real' reported profit is: whether it comes from sustainable core operations backed by cash, or from one-off gains, aggressive accounting and earnings management. High-quality earnings track cash flow, recur, and don't lean on estimates. A central task of due diligence and investment analysis.

Etymology 词源

quality（质量）——不问'赚了多少'，而问'这份利润有多可信、多可持续'；缩写 QoE，是尽调标准环节。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ earnings quality /ˈərnɪŋz kˈwɑləti/
    Earnings quality asks not how big the profit is but how much you'd trust it.
  • ▸ the accruals ratio /ðə əˈkruəlz ˈreɪʃiˌoʊ/
    A high accruals ratio — profit far above cash flow — is earnings quality's warning light.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ a quality-of-earnings review
  • ▸ earnings backed by cash
  • ▸ one-off vs recurring
  • ▸ aggressive accounting

In the wild 语境例句

A quality-of-earnings review found half the target's profit came from a one-time asset sale and aggressive revenue timing — not the durable engine the seller advertised.
