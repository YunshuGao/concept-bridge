创伤知情教育实践 chuāngshāng zhīqíng jiàoyù shíjiàntrauma-informed practice

🔊 trauma-informed practice /ˈtrɔmə-ˌɪnˈfɔrmd ˈpræktɪs/

认识到许多行为问题源于创伤与压力,以'这孩子经历了什么'取代'这孩子有什么毛病'的视角。它强调安全、可预期的常规、稳定的关系、情绪调节支持——先连接、后纠正。它把行为管理从惩罚转向理解与调节,与创伤心理学、依恋理论一脉相承,是学生福祉的重要维度。

Recognising that much problem behaviour stems from trauma and stress, replacing 'what's wrong with this child?' with 'what happened to this child?'. It emphasises safety, predictable routines, stable relationships and support for emotional regulation — connect before you correct. It shifts behaviour management from punishment to understanding and regulation, of a piece with trauma psychology and attachment theory, and a key dimension of student wellbeing.

Etymology 词源

trauma 希腊语'伤口'——创伤知情即'知晓伤口的存在'而据以调整回应;informed 见前。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ connect before correct /kəˈnɛkt ˌbiˈfɔr kərˈɛkt/
    Connect before you correct calms the nervous system before it addresses the behaviour.
  • ▸ what happened to you /wət ˈhæpənd tɪ ju/
    'What happened to you?' replaces 'what's wrong with you?' and changes everything downstream.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

A student who explodes when rushed may be dysregulated, not defiant — trauma-informed practice offers a predictable routine and a calm-down step before any consequence.
