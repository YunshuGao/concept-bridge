模型风险 móxíng fēngxiǎnmodel risk

🔊 model risk /ˈmɑdəl rɪsk/

过度依赖数学模型带来的风险:模型的假设在现实(尤其极端情况)中失效,却因其精确的数字外表而被误信。'所有模型都是错的,有些是有用的'——危险在于忘记前半句。LTCM 崩盘、2008危机皆有模型风险之影。

The risk from over-relying on mathematical models: their assumptions fail in reality (especially in extremes), yet are trusted because of the precise numbers they output. 'All models are wrong, but some are useful' — the danger is forgetting the first half. The LTCM collapse and the 2008 crisis both bear the fingerprints of model risk.

Etymology 词源

model 源自拉丁 modulus（小尺度、标准）——模型即'现实的简化尺度';模型风险即'误把地图当领土'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the map is not the territory /ðə mæp ɪz nɑt ðə ˈtɛrɪˌtɔri/
    'The map is not the territory' is model risk stated as philosophy.
  • ▸ spurious precision /ˈspəriəs priˈsɪʒən/
    Spurious precision is a wrong answer dressed in three decimal places.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ all models are wrong
  • ▸ spurious precision
  • ▸ garbage in, garbage out
  • ▸ the map is not the territory

In the wild 语境例句

Traders trusted a model that assumed markets never jump; when they jumped, the precise-looking numbers were precisely wrong, and the fund blew up.
