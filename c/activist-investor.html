激进投资者 jījìn tóuzīzhěthe activist investor

🔊 activist investor /ˈæktɪvɪst ˌɪnˈvɛstər/

买入公司大量股份后，公开施压管理层改变战略、治理或资本配置以提升股价——要求分拆、回购、换帅、卖公司。他们是市场对低效管理的“纠错机制”，也常被批为追逐短期收益、牺牲长期投资。

An investor who buys a large stake then publicly pressures management to change strategy, governance or capital allocation to lift the share price — demanding spin-offs, buybacks, new leadership or a sale. They are the market's corrective for underperforming management, and are also criticised for chasing short-term gains at the expense of long-term investment.

Etymology 词源

activist 源自拉丁 agere（做、驱动）——“激进/行动派”投资者即主动出手改变公司的股东，与被动持有者相对。
