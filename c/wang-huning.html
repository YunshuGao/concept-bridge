🔊 ideologue /ˈaɪdiəlɒɡ/

1994-95年复旦教授王沪宁的日记:读书清单、美国观察(另著《美国反对美国》)、新权威主义思考——此后入中枢辅佐三代领导核心。研究中国政治思想的英文文献必引其人;“文胆”一词的当代标本。

The 1994-95 diary of a Fudan professor — reading lists, reflections on neo-authoritarianism, America observed (his America Against America) — written just before he entered the leadership's inner circle for three successive cores. Anglophone China-watching cites him compulsively.
