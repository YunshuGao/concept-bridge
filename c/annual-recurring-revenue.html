年度经常性收入 niándù jīngchángxìng shōurùannual recurring revenue (ARR / MRR)

🔊 annual recurring revenue /ˈænjuəl rɪˈkərɪŋ ˈrɛvəˌnu/

订阅制企业每年（ARR）或每月（MRR）可预期重复获得的合同化收入——SaaS 商业模式的核心指标。经常性收入比一次性销售更可预测、更可估值，配合低流失与净收入留存率，构成订阅经济的估值基础。

The contracted, repeatable revenue a subscription business expects each year (ARR) or month (MRR) — the core metric of the SaaS model. Recurring revenue is more predictable and more valuable than one-off sales; combined with low churn and high net revenue retention, it underpins the valuation of the subscription economy.

Etymology 词源

recurring 源自拉丁 recurrere（跑回、重现）——'经常性'即收入年复一年'跑回来'；ARR/MRR 是订阅时代的关键指标。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ recurring revenue /rɪˈkərɪŋ ˈrɛvəˌnu/
    Recurring revenue is valued more highly because it is revenue you don't have to win twice.
  • ▸ net revenue retention /nɛt ˈrɛvəˌnu riˈtɛnʃən/
    Net revenue retention above 100% means existing customers grow faster than others churn.
