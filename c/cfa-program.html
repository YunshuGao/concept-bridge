CFA 特许金融分析师 CFA tèxǔ jīnróng fēnxīshīthe CFA Program (Chartered Financial Analyst)

🔊 CFA /ˌsiːɛfˈeɪ/

CFA协会的三级投资分析认证,对应知识→分析→判断的进阶:一级'这是什么?'(建立金融语言),二级'如何估值?'(分析与估值,公认最难),三级'该怎么做?'(投资组合与财富管理决策)。十大科目贯穿三级,权重随级别从记忆转向应用再转向判断。

The CFA Institute's three-level investment credential, mirroring knowledge → analysis → judgement: Level I ('what is it?' — the language of finance), Level II ('how do I value it?' — analysis and valuation, widely considered the hardest), Level III ('what should I do?' — portfolio and wealth-management decisions). Ten topic areas run through all three, with emphasis shifting from recall to application to judgement.

Etymology 词源

Chartered（特许的,源自 charter 特许状）+ Financial Analyst——'特许金融分析师';如同学医:一级学解剖,二级诊断,三级为真实病人定治疗方案。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the charter /ðə ˈʧɑrtər/
    Earning the charter takes most candidates several years and hundreds of hours per level.
  • ▸ buy-side credential /baɪ-saɪd krɪˈdɛnʃəl/
    The CFA is prized on the buy-side; investment banking leans more on modelling than on the charter.

In the wild 语境例句

For a strategy-and-consulting path, the CFA's most valuable parts are financial statement analysis, corporate finance, economics, equity and fixed-income valuation, and ethics — the tools for analysing companies and making business decisions.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

CFA重投资分析(买方),与投行的建模/交易(卖方)侧重不同;想进高盛投行或麦肯锡战略,单靠CFA不够,宜配合财务建模、会计、演示沟通与案例面试。
