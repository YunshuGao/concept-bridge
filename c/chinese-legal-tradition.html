中华法系（礼法结合） Zhōnghuá fǎxìthe Chinese legal tradition: law wedded to ritual

🔊 code /koʊd/

⏳ 653年定型

先秦德治法治之争（儒家孔孟主德治仁政；法家商鞅韩非主法治——铸刑书为中国最早成文法）后，汉魏以降律令儒家化：以经注律、引经决狱。《唐律疏议》（653年）继承汉魏以来法律制定和阐释的经验、是中国现存最早最完整的封建法典，是中华法系确立的标志——礼法结合的典范（“德礼为政教之本，刑罚为政教之用”）。宋以后理学深入族规家训，明清乡约（明太祖“六谕”、康熙“圣谕十六条”）宣讲教化与法律合流——法律与教化并用为中国古代国家治理的基本特征。

From the classical quarrel — Confucian rule by virtue against Legalist rule by law — imperial China synthesised: statutes read through the classics, verdicts argued from scripture, until the Tang Code with Commentary (653), the oldest complete Chinese code extant, perfected the marriage ('virtue and ritual are the root of government; punishment its instrument') and founded what jurists call the Chinese legal family, exported wholesale to Japan, Korea and Vietnam. Below the code ran the pedagogy — clan rules, village covenants, imperial maxims read aloud — law and moral instruction as one governing technology.

Comparative lawyers class the world into common law, civil law, Islamic law — and the Chinese legal family, whose Tang Code governed a quarter of humanity's ancestors; 'rule of law v. rule of virtue' remains a live official formula (依法治国与以德治国相结合).

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

中华法系 = the Chinese legal family/tradition（法系 legal family 为比较法学术语）。《唐律疏议》= the Tang Code (with Commentary)。“律令儒家化”= the Confucianisation of law（瞿同祖名题，英语汉学经典）。乡约 = village covenants/community compacts。当代“依法治国”= law-based governance（官方译，非 rule of law——细微而要害）。
