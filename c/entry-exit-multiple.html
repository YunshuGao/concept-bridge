🔊 entry and exit multiples /ˈɛntri ənd ˈɛksət ˈməltəpəlz/

LBO中收购时支付的估值倍数(进入)与卖出时实现的倍数(退出)。核心原则:进入倍数越低、退出倍数越高,回报越好——'低买高卖'的杠杆版。谨慎的基金假设退出倍数等于或低于进入倍数(不指望倍数扩张),把回报建立在可控的运营改善上,而非市场的慷慨。

The valuation multiple paid at acquisition (entry) and realised at sale (exit) in an LBO. The core principle: the lower the entry multiple and the higher the exit multiple, the better the return — 'buy low, sell high' with leverage. Prudent funds assume an exit multiple equal to or below entry (not banking on expansion), basing returns on controllable operational improvement rather than the market's generosity.
