市场规模三层 shìchǎng guīmó sāncéngTAM, SAM, SOM

🔊 TAM SAM SOM /tæm sæm sɒm/

市场规模的三个同心层:TAM 总可服务市场（若人人都买）、SAM 可服务市场（你的产品与渠道实际能触达）、SOM 可获得市场（近期现实能拿下的份额）。投资人用它检验野心与现实。

Three nested measures of market size: TAM (total addressable market, if everyone bought), SAM (serviceable available market, what your product and channels can actually reach), SOM (serviceable obtainable market, the share you can realistically win near-term). Investors use it to test ambition against reality.

Etymology 词源

三个首字母缩略（Total/Serviceable Available/Serviceable Obtainable Market）——创业融资演示的标准“市场三段论”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ market sizing /ˈmɑrkɪt ˈsaɪzɪŋ/
    Market sizing is where optimism and arithmetic have their first fight.
  • ▸ bottom-up sizing /ˈbɑtəm-əp ˈsaɪzɪŋ/
    Bottom-up sizing (units × price × buyers) beats a top-down 'if we get 1% of China'.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ size the market
  • ▸ top-down vs bottom-up
  • ▸ a credible SOM
  • ▸ the addressable market

In the wild 语境例句

A pitch claiming a trillion-dollar TAM but no credible path to a modest SOM signals ambition untethered from a real go-to-market.
