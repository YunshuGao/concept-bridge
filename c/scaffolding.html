支架式教学 zhījià shì jiàoxuéscaffolding

🔊 scaffolding /ˈskæfoʊldɪŋ/

教师提供临时性的支持（提示、模板、句式起句、分步指引、示例）帮助学生完成暂时超出其独立能力的任务,随着能力增长逐步撤除支架,直至学生独立完成。它建立在维果茨基'最近发展区'之上——支架的作用正是跨越'能独立做'与'在帮助下能做'之间的差距。

The teacher providing temporary supports (prompts, templates, sentence starters, step-by-step guides, examples) to help students accomplish a task momentarily beyond their independent ability, then gradually removing the scaffold as competence grows until they can do it alone. It rests on Vygotsky's zone of proximal development — the scaffold bridges the gap between what a learner can do alone and what they can do with help.

Etymology 词源

scaffold（脚手架）——建筑施工的临时支撑,建成后拆除;教学支架同理,能力建成即撤。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ temporary support /ˈtɛmpərˌɛri səˈpɔrt/
    Temporary support is scaffolding's defining feature — it is meant to come down.
  • ▸ fading the support /ˈfeɪdɪŋ ðə səˈpɔrt/
    Fading the support turns assisted performance into independent competence.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ fade the scaffold

In the wild 语境例句

A partially-completed code template scaffolds a struggling student into a working program, then you remove a little more of it each lesson until they write it unaided.
