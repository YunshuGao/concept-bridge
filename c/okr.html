目标与关键结果 mùbiāo yǔ guānjiàn jiéguǒOKRs (objectives and key results)

🔊 OKR /ˌoʊkeɪˈɑːr/

源自英特尔、由谷歌发扬的目标管理法:设定少数鼓舞人心的定性目标（Objective），每个配3–5个可量化的关键结果（Key Results）衡量进展。强调聚焦、对齐、透明与延展性目标。

A goal-setting method from Intel, popularised by Google: set a few inspiring qualitative Objectives, each with 3–5 measurable Key Results tracking progress. It stresses focus, alignment, transparency and stretch targets.

Etymology 词源

Objectives and Key Results 缩略——Andy Grove 在英特尔创立，John Doerr 带入谷歌后风行科技界。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ stretch goals /strɛʧ goʊlz/
    Stretch goals aim for 0.7, because hitting 1.0 means you aimed too low.
  • ▸ aligned objectives /əˈlaɪnd əˈbʤɛktɪvz/
    Aligned objectives let a thousand people pull in one direction without a memo each.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ set OKRs
  • ▸ the objective and its key results
  • ▸ cascade goals
  • ▸ stretch targets

In the wild 语境例句

An OKR pairs an ambitious objective — 'delight new users' — with hard key results like activation rate, so inspiration stays measurable.
