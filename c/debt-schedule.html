🔊 debt schedule /dɛt ˈskɛʤʊl/

LBO/三表模型中逐年追踪每层债务余额、利息、强制与选择性偿还(现金清扫cash sweep)的明细表。它按优先级瀑布偿债:先还高级债,循环贷(revolver)用于弥补现金短缺。它是模型循环引用的核心(利息取决于债务余额,债务偿还取决于现金,现金取决于利息)——分析师须懂'开启迭代计算'。

The schedule in an LBO/three-statement model that tracks, year by year, each debt tranche's balance, interest, and mandatory and optional repayment (the cash sweep). It repays by priority waterfall — senior debt first, the revolver plugging cash shortfalls. It is the heart of the model's circularity (interest depends on the debt balance, repayment depends on cash, cash depends on interest) — analysts must know to enable iterative calculation.

Etymology 词源

schedule（明细表,源自拉丁 scedula 小纸条）——列出每层债务'逐年'偿还的明细;cash sweep 即'现金清扫'还债。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the cash sweep /ðə kæʃ swip/
    The cash sweep uses every spare dollar to pay down debt ahead of schedule.
  • ▸ the debt waterfall /ðə dɛt ˈwɔːtərfɔːl/
    The debt waterfall repays senior tranches before junior ones ever see a cent.

In the wild 语境例句

The debt schedule swept all excess cash to repay the senior term loan first — the deleveraging that, in the returns bridge, converts into growing equity value.
