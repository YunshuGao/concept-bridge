李光耀与新加坡模式 Lǐ GuāngyàoLee Kuan Yew and the Singapore model

⏳ 2000年

《从第三世界到第一世界》：李光耀自述新加坡1965年被迫独立后的立国工程——廉洁高薪养贤的精英治理（meritocracy——与科举传统的现代对话）、组屋制度（居者有其屋）、中央公积金、双语政策（英语工作语+母语传承——对用户处境极具镜鉴）、严刑峻法与社会纪律、招商引资的发展型国家。“新加坡模式”成为威权发展主义的样板：中国改革者研究它，西方以它质问民主与增长的关系。

From Third World to First is the founder's own account of statecraft after unwanted independence in 1965: meritocratic, corruption-proofed governance (well-paid mandarins — the examination tradition modernised), near-universal public housing, the Central Provident Fund, bilingualism (English for work, mother tongue for roots — a policy with personal resonance for any bilingual professional), discipline by law, and courtship of multinationals. 'The Singapore model' became the exhibit in every debate on development without democracy — studied in Beijing, brandished in op-eds everywhere.
