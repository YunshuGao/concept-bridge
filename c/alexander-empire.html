亚历山大帝国 Yàlìshāndà dìguóAlexander's empire

also: Alexander the Great · the Macedonian empire

⏳ 前336年–前323年

马其顿国王亚历山大自前334年率军东征，十年间灭波斯帝国，建立西起希腊、东到印度河、地跨欧亚非的空前大帝国；东征具侵略性、带来灾难，客观上促进东西方文化大交汇与贸易往来，所建数十座新城成为经济文化中心。

The vast realm Alexander of Macedon conquered in a decade from 334 BC — Persia destroyed, his rule stretching from Greece to the Indus. The campaigns were predatory and devastating, yet they fused East and West: trade followed the army, and dozens of new-founded cities became lasting centres of commerce and culture.
