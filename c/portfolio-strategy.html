🔊 portfolio strategy /pɔrtˈfoʊliˌoʊ ˈstrætəʤi/

如何配置资本与注意力于多个业务:哪些投资成长、哪些维持挤现金、哪些出售。好的组合平衡现金流与增长、风险与回报，并持续修剪不再契合的业务。

How to allocate capital and attention across multiple businesses: which to grow, which to hold and milk, which to sell. A good portfolio balances cash flow against growth and risk against return, continually pruning businesses that no longer fit.

Etymology 词源

portfolio 源自意大利 portafoglio（携带 portare + 纸张 foglio）——本指“文件夹”，引申为一组持有的资产或业务。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ capital allocation /ˈkæpɪtəl ˌæləˈkeɪʃən/
    Capital allocation is the CEO's most consequential and least visible job.
  • ▸ active portfolio management /ˈæktɪv pɔrtˈfoʊliˌoʊ ˈmænɪʤmənt/
    Active portfolio management means selling winners into strength and cutting losers early.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ rebalance the portfolio
  • ▸ allocate capital
  • ▸ prune the portfolio
  • ▸ grow, hold or harvest

In the wild 语境例句

A conglomerate that treats its businesses as a portfolio sells a strong cash cow at a good price to fund three faster-growing bets.
