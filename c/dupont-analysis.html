杜邦分析 dùbāng fēnxīthe DuPont analysis

🔊 DuPont analysis /djuːˈpɒnt æˈnælɪsɪs/

把净资产收益率（ROE）拆成三因子:净利润率（盈利能力）× 资产周转率（运营效率）× 权益乘数（财务杠杆）。它诊断出高 ROE 究竟来自赚得多、转得快，还是借得狠——同一数字，三种来源。

Decomposing ROE into three factors: net profit margin (profitability) × asset turnover (operating efficiency) × equity multiplier (financial leverage). It diagnoses whether a high ROE comes from earning more, turning assets faster, or borrowing harder — one number, three very different sources.

Etymology 词源

1920年代美国杜邦公司财务人员 Donaldson Brown 发明——以公司名命名的经典财务拆解框架。

  • ▸ the ROE decomposition /ðə ˌɑːroʊˈiː ˌdikəmpəˈzɪʃən/
    The ROE decomposition refuses to let leverage masquerade as excellence.
  • ▸ margin × turnover × leverage /ˈmɑrʤən ˈtərˌnoʊvər ˈlɛvərɪdʒ/
    Margin × turnover × leverage tells you which lever is really driving the return.

In the wild 语境例句

A retailer and a luxury house can share an ROE, but DuPont shows one wins on turnover (thin margins, fast sales) and the other on margin.
