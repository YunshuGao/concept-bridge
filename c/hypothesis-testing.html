假设检验 jiǎshè jiǎnyànhypothesis testing

🔊 hypothesis testing /haɪˈpɑθəsəs ˈtɛstɪŋ/

统计推断的核心程序:设原假设(H0,如'该策略无超额收益')与备择假设,计算检验统计量与p值,在给定显著性水平下决定拒绝或不拒绝H0。它让'这个结果是真的还是运气?'成为可量化的判断——量化分析与实证金融的地基。

The core procedure of statistical inference: state a null hypothesis (H0, e.g. 'this strategy has no excess return') and an alternative, compute a test statistic and p-value, and decide whether to reject or fail to reject H0 at a chosen significance level. It makes 'is this result real or luck?' a quantifiable judgement — the foundation of quantitative analysis and empirical finance.

Etymology 词源

hypothesis 源自希腊 hypo（在下）+ thesis（放置）——'假设'即置于论证之下的前提;p-value 的 p 为 probability（概率）。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ significance testing /sɪgˈnɪfɪkəns ˈtɛstɪŋ/
    Significance testing asks how surprised we should be if the null were true.
  • ▸ the p-value /ðə pi-ˈvælju/
    A small p-value means the data would be unlikely if the null hypothesis held.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ reject the null hypothesis
  • ▸ the p-value
  • ▸ statistical significance
  • ▸ Type I and Type II error

In the wild 语境例句

Before trusting a backtested strategy, an analyst tests whether its returns are statistically distinguishable from luck — a low p-value, not a pretty equity curve, is the evidence.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

统计显著≠经济显著≠真实(见 model-risk);p值被广泛误用(p-hacking)。'拒绝H0'不等于'证明备择为真',只是'数据与H0不符'。
