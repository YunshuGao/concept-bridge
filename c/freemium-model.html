免费增值模式 miǎnfèi zēngzhí móshìthe freemium model

🔊 freemium model /ˈfriːmiəm ˈmɑdəl/

提供有价值的免费基础版本以大规模获客,再向需要更多功能、容量或专业特性的少数用户收费。成败关键在'免费'与'付费'之间的界线划得是否精准——免费要足以吸引和留住,付费墙要恰好卡在真实需求出现之处。

Offering a genuinely valuable free tier to acquire users at scale, then charging the minority who need more features, capacity or professional capabilities. Success hinges on drawing the line between free and paid precisely — free must be enough to attract and retain, while the paywall must sit exactly where real need emerges.

Etymology 词源

free（免费）+ premium（增值/付费）合成——'免费增值';2006年由风投 Fred Wilson 的博客读者创造此词。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the free tier /ðə fri tir/
    The free tier is a marketing cost that also happens to be the product.
  • ▸ the paywall placement /ðə ˈpeɪwɔːl ˈpleɪsmənt/
    Paywall placement is where freemium economics are won or lost.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ a freemium tier
  • ▸ the free-to-paid conversion
  • ▸ where to put the paywall
  • ▸ free to acquire, paid to monetise

In the wild 语境例句

Freemium works when the free tier is generous enough to build a habit and the paywall sits exactly at the feature users can no longer do without.
