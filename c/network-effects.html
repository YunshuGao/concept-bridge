网络效应 wǎngluò xiàoyìngnetwork effects

🔊 network effects /ˈnɛtˌwərk ˈifɛkts/

用户越多，产品对每个用户越有价值——电话、社交、市场平台、支付。网络效应制造赢家通吃与自增强的护城河:领先者的领先会自我放大（正反馈）。

The more users, the more valuable the product is to each — phones, social networks, marketplaces, payments. Network effects create winner-take-all dynamics and a self-reinforcing moat: the leader's lead compounds.

Etymology 词源

network = net（网）+ work（活计）——本指渔网的编结，19世纪转指铁路电报之“网”，再抽象为用户互联的价值网。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ Metcalfe's law /ˈmɛtkəlf'z lɔ/
    Metcalfe's law prices a network by the square of its users, roughly.
  • ▸ demand-side scale /dɪˈmænd-saɪd skeɪl/
    Demand-side scale is a moat customers dig for you, one signup at a time.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ direct/indirect network effects
  • ▸ reach critical mass
  • ▸ the flywheel
  • ▸ tipping point

In the wild 语境例句

A marketplace with liquidity wins twice: more sellers pull more buyers, who pull more sellers — network effects the challenger can't bootstrap.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

网络效应≠病毒传播:前者是“更多用户使产品更好用”（价值），后者是“用户带来用户”（增长）。二者常同现但机制不同。
