郡县制 jùnxiànzhìthe commandery-county system

⏳ 前221年起

秦进一步废除分封制，建立由中央直接管辖的郡县制：全国36郡（后40多），郡守县令均由朝廷直接任免，县下设乡里亭——皇帝和朝廷牢牢控制全国权力，把政治法律军事土地赋役制度推向全国，开创此后历代王朝地方行政的基本模式。汉武帝又建刺史制度监察地方。

Qin abolished enfeoffment outright: the realm became 36 (later 40-odd) commanderies and their counties, every governor and magistrate appointed — and dismissable — by the throne, with wards and villages below. Power now ran from the centre to the fields, the template of Chinese local government for the next two thousand years; Han Wudi added circuit inspectors to watch the watchers.

Political scientists call imperial China 'the first modern state' on this evidence: salaried, appointed, transferable officials a thousand years before Europe had them — Fukuyama's Origins of Political Order builds its whole argument here.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

郡 = commandery（史学定译）；后世的州县多译 prefectures and counties。这一节点是驳“中国两千年封建社会”直译的证据核心：appointed officials 正是 feudal lords 的反面。福山、韦伯对中国官僚制的论述是 Economist 谈中国治理的常用底本。
