甲午中日战争与瓜分狂潮 Jiǎwǔ Zhōng-Rì zhànzhēngthe First Sino-Japanese War and the scramble for China

⏳ 1894年–1899年

1894年日本借朝鲜东学党起义蓄意挑起战争：平壤陷落（左宝贵牺牲）、黄海海战邓世昌致远舰壮烈殉国、旅顺大屠杀两万余人、1895威海卫北洋舰队全军覆没（丁汝昌自杀殉国）。《马关条约》：割辽东半岛（后三国干涉还辽、索“赎辽费”3000万两）台湾全岛及澎湖列岛，赔白银2亿两，开沙市重庆苏杭，允许日本在通商口岸开设工厂——外国侵略势力深入腹地，大大加深半殖民地化。台湾军民抗日百余战。列强掀起抢夺利权、强租租借地、划分“势力范围”的瓜分中国狂潮（时局图）；1899年美国提出“门户开放”照会，列强共同奴役中国。

Japan's rebuilt state tested itself on China's half-rebuilt one in 1894 and the verdict was total: Pyongyang fell, the Beiyang fleet was shattered at the Yalu (Captain Deng Shichang ramming his sinking ship at the enemy), Port Arthur's population massacred, the fleet's remnant scuttled at Weihaiwei. The Treaty of Shimonoseki took Taiwan and the Penghus, two hundred million taels, new ports, and — the clause with teeth — the right to build factories in China; Taiwan's people fought the occupation through a hundred battles. Victory's real price came after: the powers, seeing the giant was hollow, scrambled for leaseholds and 'spheres of influence', while America's Open Door notes (1899) proposed sharing the meal rather than carving the diner.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

《马关条约》英语名 the Treaty of Shimonoseki。“瓜分”原意切瓜——carving up the (Chinese) melon 是当时英语报刊实际用语。三国干涉还辽 = the Triple Intervention。“门户开放”承接 [[washington-conference]] 节点完成美国对华政策线。甲午对日本=立国之战、对中国=体制审判——两国教科书叙事对读极有价值。
