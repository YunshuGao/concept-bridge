朱镕基与九十年代经济改革 Zhū RóngjīZhu Rongji and the reforms of the 1990s

⏳ 1991年–2003年

《朱镕基讲话实录》（四卷）是1990年代改革攻坚的第一手文献：1994年分税制改革（中央与地方按税种划分收入——重建中央财力、奠定此后央地财政关系；讲话中反复向各省算账说服）；金融整顿（治理三角债、约束乱集资、央行专业化）；1998年国企三年脱困与“抓大放小”（下岗分流 layoffs 的痛苦转型）；住房制度改革（终结福利分房——商品房时代开端）；应对亚洲金融危机（人民币不贬值承诺）；漫长的入世谈判（2001年收官）。“不管前面是地雷阵还是万丈深渊，我都将勇往直前”——技术官僚改革者的标本。

The four volumes of Zhu Rongji's recorded remarks are the primary source for reform's hardest decade: the 1994 tax-sharing reform that rebuilt central revenue (the transcripts show him haggling province by province — the fiscal constitution of today's China being negotiated aloud); banking cleaned up; the brutal three-year SOE rescue with its tens of millions of layoffs ('grasp the large, release the small'); housing marketised; the RMB held firm through the Asian financial crisis; and the WTO accession he closed in 2001. His famous vow — 'be it minefield or abyss, I will march forward' — made him the archetype of the technocratic reformer, and this book his ledger.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

分税制 = the tax-sharing system/reform（央地财政关系的分水岭——链 [[chinese-taxation]] 的当代章）；下岗 = xiagang/layoffs（一代人的社会记忆词）；抓大放小 = grasp the big, let go of the small。朱镕基英语世界形象（'economic tsar'——链 [[peter-the-great]] 的 czar 用法！）。
