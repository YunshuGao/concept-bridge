空想社会主义 kōngxiǎng shèhuìzhǔyìutopian socialism

⏳ 19世纪初

资本主义形成初期，圣西门、傅立叶、欧文等看到资本主义弊端，设计未来社会美好蓝图（欧文的“新和谐公社”实验），是科学社会主义的思想来源；但仅从理性、正义等原则出发，主张阶级调和、反对阶级斗争，没有找到社会变革的正确途径，故称“空想”。马克思恩格斯创立唯物史观和剩余价值学说，使社会主义从空想变为科学。

Saint-Simon, Fourier and Owen saw industrial capitalism's miseries early and drafted blueprints for the good society — Owen even bankrolling his New Harmony commune — supplying scientific socialism's raw material. The verdict built into the Chinese term: 'utopian' because they appealed to reason and justice rather than class struggle, designing paradise without a road to it; Marx's twin discoveries (historical materialism, surplus value) turned the dream into a doctrine.

In the wild 语境例句

Every failed intentional community from New Harmony to modern co-living startups gets the Owen citation — 'utopian' as both compliment and diagnosis, exactly the double edge Engels gave it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“空想”定译 utopian（恩格斯书名 Socialism: Utopian and Scientific 的框架直接构成本课）。utopia 出自莫尔《乌托邦》（1516——文艺复兴节点的邻居）。“新和谐公社”= New Harmony。
