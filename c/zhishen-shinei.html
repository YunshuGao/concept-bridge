《置身事内》（土地财政与地方政府经济学） zhìshēn shìnèiIn the Thick of It: local government and China's growth machine

🔊 land finance /lænd ˈfaɪˌnæns/

⏳ 2021年

兰小欢的现象级中国政经解读：1994年分税制后地方政府财权上收、事权仍重，遂以土地财政补缺——低价征地招商引资（工业用地补贴换税基）+高价出让住宅用地（土地出让金），以城投公司（LGFV 地方政府融资平台）抵押土地融资建设——“地方政府公司化”驱动了基建奇迹与房价、债务、产能问题的同一枚硬币两面。理解当下房地产化债、卖地收入下滑新闻的最佳底本。

Lan Xiaohuan's bestselling anatomy of the growth machine: after the 1994 tax-sharing reform starved local budgets, land became the fiscal workaround — industrial land sold cheap to lure factories (subsidise the tax base), residential land sold dear (land-transfer revenue), and local-government financing vehicles (LGFVs) borrowing against it all to build the infrastructure miracle. 'The corporatised local state' explains both China's roads and its property-and-debt hangover — the single best decoder for every land-auction, LGFV and 化债 headline the Economist runs.
