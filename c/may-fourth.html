五四运动 Wǔsì yùndòngthe May Fourth Movement

⏳ 1919年

1919年巴黎和会上中国代表提出取消“二十一条”、收回山东权益等正当要求遭拒（列强将德国在山东权益转予日本）。5月4日北京学生天安门集会游行：“外争主权，内除国贼”“誓死力争，还我青岛”，火烧赵家楼；遭镇压后6月起上海工人罢工商人罢市——工人阶级成为运动主力，运动中心移至上海。北洋政府被迫释放学生、罢免曹汝霖等、中国代表拒签和约——五四运动的直接目标得到实现。是一次彻底反帝反封建的伟大爱国革命运动，中国工人阶级开始登上政治舞台，是中国旧民主主义革命走向新民主主义革命的转折点，为中共成立作了思想干部准备。

Versailles handed Germany's Shandong rights to Japan, and on 4 May 1919 Beijing's students answered in Tiananmen Square: 'Defend sovereignty abroad, punish traitors at home'. Arrests only spread it — by June Shanghai's workers were on strike, the first entrance of Chinese labour onto the political stage — until the government released the students, sacked the pro-Japanese ministers, and refused to sign the treaty. The textbook's verdict is architectural: a thoroughly anti-imperialist, anti-feudal awakening, the hinge between the old democratic revolution and the new, and the direct preparation — in minds and cadres — for the party founded two years later.
