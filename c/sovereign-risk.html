主权风险 zhǔquán fēngxiǎnsovereign risk

🔊 sovereign risk /ˈsɒvrɪn rɪsk/

一国政府无力或不愿偿还其债务的风险。与企业不同,主权国家难以被'清算',违约多是政治选择与经济能力的混合。它推高该国借贷成本、引发货币贬值与资本外逃,严重时酿成主权债务危机(希腊、阿根廷)。

The risk that a national government cannot or will not repay its debt. Unlike a company, a sovereign can't easily be 'liquidated', so default is a mix of political choice and economic capacity. It raises the country's borrowing costs, triggers currency depreciation and capital flight, and in severe cases a sovereign debt crisis (Greece, Argentina).

Etymology 词源

sovereign 见 westphalia 节点(拉丁 super 至上)——主权风险即'至高无上的国家也可能赖账'的风险。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ country risk /ˈkəntri rɪsk/
    Country risk bundles a government's willingness and ability to honour its word.
  • ▸ the sovereign spread /ðə ˈsɒvrɪn sprɛd/
    The sovereign spread over safe bonds is the market's live verdict on default risk.
