佛教 FójiàoBuddhism

🔊 Buddhism /ˈbudɪzəm/

⏳ 前6世纪起

公元前6世纪由乔达摩·悉达多创立于印度：早期反对婆罗门特权、提出“众生平等”、不拒低种姓入教，又宣扬“忍耐顺从”而获国王与富人支持；前3世纪起向外传播，经中亚传入中国，南经锡兰传至东南亚。

Founded in India in the 6th century BC by Siddhartha Gautama; early Buddhism rejected Brahmin privilege and preached the equality of all beings while counselling patient acceptance — winning both low-caste converts and royal patronage — and from the 3rd century BC spread through Central Asia to China and via Ceylon across Southeast Asia.

Etymology 词源

Buddha 梵语“觉醒者”（budh 觉）+ -ism——“佛教”即觉者之教。

In the wild 语境例句

Religions travel with trade: Buddhism reached China not by conquest but in the baggage of Silk Road merchants and pilgrim monks.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“众生平等”可译 the equality of all (sentient) beings；“忍耐顺从”教材语境指 forbearance and acceptance。注意约10世纪后佛教在印度本土衰落、被印度教（Hinduism）取代——今日印度并非佛教国家，这是常见误区。
