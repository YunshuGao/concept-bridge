经验曲线 jīngyàn qūxiànthe experience (learning) curve

🔊 experience curve /ɪkˈspɪriəns kərv/

BCG 的经验发现:累计产量每翻一番，单位成本可预测地下降约20–30%——源于学习、流程改进、规模与创新。它是激进定价与抢占份额战略的理论依据:先亏后赚，以量降本。

BCG's empirical finding: each doubling of cumulative output cuts unit cost by a predictable 20–30% — from learning, process improvement, scale and innovation. It justifies aggressive pricing to grab share: lose now, win on volume-driven cost.

Etymology 词源

experience 源自拉丁 experiri（试验、经历，与 experiment 同根）——经验曲线即“做得越多，越便宜”的量化规律。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ learning curve /ˈlərnɪŋ kərv/
    The learning curve is why the tenth factory costs less than the first.
  • ▸ cumulative volume /ˈkjumjələtɪv ˈvɑljum/
    Cumulative volume, not annual output, drives the cost decline.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ ride the experience curve
  • ▸ cost declines with volume
  • ▸ the learning rate
  • ▸ price ahead of cost

In the wild 语境例句

Solar panels fell 90% in a decade almost exactly along the experience curve — each doubling of installed capacity cut costs on schedule.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

经验曲线看累计产量（历史总量），规模经济看当期产量（当下规模）——两者常混，但一个是时间积累，一个是瞬时规模。
