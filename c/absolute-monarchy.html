君主专制与中央集权 jūnzhǔ zhuānzhì / zhōngyāng jíquánabsolute monarchy and centralised power

also: autocracy · despotism · centralisation

⏳ 古代–近代

君主个人集军、政、财、司法大权于一身（君主专制），并将权力收归中央（中央集权）的统治形态；汉谟拉比是教材中最早的典型。

Rule in which one monarch concentrates military, administrative, fiscal and judicial power in his own hands, drawing authority to the centre; Hammurabi is the textbook's first exemplar of the pattern.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ absolute rule
  • ▸ centralise power
  • ▸ an autocratic state
  • ▸ concentrate power in one's own hands

In the wild 语境例句

Autocrats' to-do lists have barely changed in 3,800 years: control the army, control the courts, control the water.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

术语雷区：君主专制 = absolute monarchy/autocracy；中央集权 = centralisation（是权力结构，不必然专制）。despotism 带强烈贬义，而 'Oriental despotism'（东方专制主义）是已被批判的旧史学框架，慎用。描述当代政体多用 authoritarian。
