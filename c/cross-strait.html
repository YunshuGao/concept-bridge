海峡两岸的交往 hǎixiá liǎng'àncross-strait relations

🔊 rapprochement /ræˈprɒʃmɒ̃/

⏳ 1979年至今

新中国成立后党和政府把实现台湾和祖国大陆统一作为神圣使命，对台方针从“解放台湾”发展为“和平统一、一国两制”基本方针。1987年台湾当局被迫调整“三不”政策、开放赴大陆探亲，两岸同胞近38年隔绝状态被打破；1992年海协会海基会达成“九二共识”（海峡两岸同属一个中国，共同努力谋求国家统一）；1993年汪辜会谈；2005年连战“和平之旅”；2008年两岸“三通”（通邮通航通商）；2015年习近平马英九新加坡会面——1949年以来两岸领导人首次会面。两岸经济文化交流蓬勃发展；祖国完全统一是全体中华儿女的共同心愿。

Policy evolved from 'liberate Taiwan' to 'peaceful reunification, one country two systems'; contact evolved with it — family visits from 1987 ending 38 years of severance, the 1992 Consensus ('both sides belong to one China', each side's phrasing its own), the 1993 Wang-Koo talks, direct post-flights-trade from 2008, and the Xi-Ma handshake in Singapore (2015), the first leaders' meeting since 1949. The textbook closes on the axiom: complete reunification is the shared aspiration of all Chinese — the sentence every Taiwan Strait news story is ultimately about.

Whether the '1992 Consensus' exists, and what it contains, decides Taiwanese elections — a two-word historical citation carrying an entire geopolitical standoff.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“九二共识”= the 1992 Consensus（其内涵表述之争——one China, respective interpretations——是台海报道核心）。统一 = reunification（中方用词，含 re- 的立场；部分西媒作 unification——前缀即政治）。三通 = the three links。与 [[zheng-chenggong]]、[[jiawu-war]]、[[victory-1945]] 的台湾史链贯通。
