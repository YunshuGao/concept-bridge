经济护城河 jīngjì hùchénghéthe economic moat

🔊 economic moat /ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk moʊt/

巴菲特术语:保护企业超额利润免受竞争侵蚀的结构性屏障——无形资产（品牌、专利）、转换成本、网络效应、成本优势、规模。护城河越宽越持久，超额回报越安全。

Buffett's term for the structural barrier that protects a firm's excess profits from competition — intangibles (brand, patents), switching costs, network effects, cost edge and scale. The wider and deeper the moat, the safer the returns.

Etymology 词源

moat 源自古法语 mote（土丘、护城河堤）——城堡的护城河转喻为企业的竞争屏障，巴菲特使其成为投资通用语。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ durable advantage /ˈdʊrəbəl ædˈvæntɪʤ/
    A durable advantage is a moat that time widens rather than silts up.
  • ▸ defensibility /dɪˌfɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/
    Investors price defensibility; anyone can price a good quarter.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

A network effect is the best moat: each new user makes the product better and the next competitor's climb steeper.
