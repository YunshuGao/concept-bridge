共同比分析 gòngtóngbǐ fēnxīcommon-size analysis

🔊 common-size analysis /ˈkɑmən-saɪz æˈnælɪsɪs/

把利润表各项表示为营收的百分比、资产负债表各项表示为总资产的百分比,消除规模差异,便于跨公司、跨年度比较结构。它一眼揭示成本结构、利润率构成与资产配置的变化——绝对数看不出的趋势,百分比一目了然。

Expressing every income-statement line as a percent of revenue and every balance-sheet line as a percent of total assets, removing size differences so structure can be compared across companies and years. It reveals cost structure, margin composition and asset mix at a glance — trends invisible in absolute numbers jump out as percentages.

Etymology 词源

common-size（统一规模）——把不同规模的公司'化为同一尺度'（都以100%为基准）以便比较。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ vertical analysis /ˈvərtɪkəl æˈnælɪsɪs/
    Vertical analysis reads a single period as percentages of one base line.
  • ▸ percentage-of-sales /pərˈsɛnɪʤ-əv-seɪlz/
    A percentage-of-sales view exposes the cost creeping up while revenue distracts you.
