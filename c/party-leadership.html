中国共产党的领导与执政方式 dǎng de lǐngdǎoParty leadership and its mode of governance

⏳ 现行制度

中国共产党领导是中国特色社会主义最本质的特征、制度的最大优势——历史和人民的选择。党的性质（两个先锋队）、宗旨（全心全意为人民服务）、执政理念（立党为公、执政为民）；指导思想与时俱进。党的领导方式：政治领导、思想领导、组织领导；执政方式：科学执政、民主执政、依法执政（党领导立法、保证执法、支持司法、带头守法）。坚持和加强党的全面领导——“党政军民学，东西南北中，党是领导一切的”；全面从严治党、自我革命。

The constitutional keystone: Party leadership as 'the most essential feature' of Chinese socialism, exercised — in the official taxonomy this chapter teaches — politically, ideologically and organisationally, and practised as 'scientific, democratic, law-based governance'. The scope formula is total ('Party, government, army, society, education — east, west, south, north, centre — the Party leads everything'), the discipline internal ('self-revolution', 'comprehensive strict governance'). Whatever one's analysis of the party-state, this is its self-description — the grammar every NPC report and every western explainer must parse.

'The Party leads everything' returned to the constitution's General Programme in 2017 — a one-line amendment that every embassy cable and China-desk column has been footnoting since.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“最本质的特征”= the most essential/defining feature（官方译）。party-state（党国体制）为外部分析术语，官方不用——内外话语的分界线即本节点的学习要点。依法执政 = law-based exercise of state power。“先锋队”= vanguard（列宁主义组织学词汇，链 [[ccp-founding]]）。
