法兰克王国 Fǎlánkè wángguóthe Frankish kingdom

⏳ 481年–843年

西罗马灭亡前后日耳曼人所建“蛮族王国”中最强大者（481年建立）。开国者克洛维皈依基督教、承认罗马教会地位，保留罗马大地主土地并将国有与无主土地赐给教会和部下，由此获得广泛支持，版图不断扩张。

The strongest of the Germanic successor-kingdoms (founded 481). Its founder Clovis converted to Christianity, courted the Roman Church, confirmed Roman landowners in their estates and granted crown and ownerless land to church and followers — a coalition-building masterclass that powered steady expansion.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

Franks 一词衍生 France/French——“法兰克”与“法兰西”的谱系值得记住；frank（坦率的）据说亦源于此族名（自由人之意）。“蛮族王国”英语史学称 successor kingdoms 或 barbarian kingdoms。
