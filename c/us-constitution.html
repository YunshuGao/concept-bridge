1787年美国宪法 Měiguó xiànfǎthe US Constitution (1787)

⏳ 1787年

独立后各州各行其是、中央软弱；1787年费城制宪会议经争论妥协制定宪法，依分权制衡原则设计联邦制共和国：行政、立法、司法三权分立，总统、国会、最高法院相互制衡；联邦与地方分权；总统议员由选举产生。世界第一部资产阶级成文宪法——但允许奴隶制存在，不承认妇女、黑人和印第安人享有平等政治权利。

With the young union drifting apart under a feeble centre, the Philadelphia Convention of 1787 hammered out — through argument and compromise — a federal republic on the principle of checks and balances: powers separated among president, Congress and Supreme Court, sovereignty shared between federal and state governments, offices filled by election. The first written national constitution of its kind — which also countenanced slavery and excluded women, Black people and Native Americans from equal citizenship.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

三权分立 = the separation of powers；制衡 = checks and balances；联邦制 = federalism——这三个词是美国政治报道的骨架。“制宪会议”= the Constitutional Convention；“妥协”著名者如 the three-fifths compromise（五分之三妥协，即奴隶制条款）。孟德斯鸠的分权理论在此落地。
