净资产收益率 jìngzīchǎn shōuyìlǜreturn on equity (ROE)

🔊 return on equity /rɪˈtərn ɔn ˈɛkwəti/

净利润除以股东权益——衡量股东每投入一元自有资本获得的回报。高 ROE 可能来自真实的经营优势，也可能来自高杠杆的放大。杜邦分析把它拆成利润率、资产周转、权益乘数三部分以辨真伪。

Net profit divided by shareholders' equity — the return earned on each dollar of shareholders' own capital. High ROE can reflect genuine operating strength or merely amplification by leverage. DuPont analysis splits it into margin, asset turnover and the equity multiplier to tell which.

Etymology 词源

见 cost-of-equity；ROE 即“股东这份 equity 每年生出的回报率”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ shareholder return on capital /ˈʃɛˌrhoʊldər rɪˈtərn ɔn ˈkæpɪtəl/
    Shareholder return on capital flatters itself when debt does the lifting.
  • ▸ levered return /ˈlɛvərd rɪˈtərn/
    A levered return can look brilliant right up until the cycle turns.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ a high ROE

In the wild 语境例句

Two firms both post 20% ROE — but DuPont reveals one earns it on operations and the other borrowed its way there, and only one is safe.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

ROE 会被杠杆美化:借更多债、缩小股权基数即可抬高 ROE，却同时放大风险——单看 ROE 会误判，须配合 ROIC 与负债率。
