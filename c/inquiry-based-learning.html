探究式学习 tànjiū shì xuéxíinquiry-based learning

🔊 inquiry-based learning /ˌɪnkˈwaɪˌri-beɪst ˈlərnɪŋ/

以问题、情境或挑战驱动,让学生通过调查、实验、探索建构理解的教学法。它培养提问、批判与自主学习能力,契合技术学科的设计与问题解决。但证据显示:对缺乏先备知识的新手,纯发现式效率低——探究更适合已有基础、需要迁移与应用之时（'引导式探究'优于'纯发现'）。

Teaching driven by questions, scenarios or challenges, where students build understanding through investigation, experiment and exploration. It develops questioning, critique and self-directed learning, and suits the design and problem-solving of technology subjects. But evidence shows pure discovery is inefficient for novices lacking prior knowledge — inquiry works better once a foundation exists and transfer and application are the goal (guided inquiry beats pure discovery).

Etymology 词源

inquire 源自拉丁 in-（入）+ quaerere（寻求）——探究即'向内寻求答案';project-based learning 是其常见形式。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ guided inquiry /ˈgaɪdɪd ˌɪnkˈwaɪˌri/
    Guided inquiry gives novices enough structure to explore without drowning.
  • ▸ problem-based learning /ˈprɑbləm-beɪst ˈlərnɪŋ/
    Problem-based learning starts from a genuine problem and pulls knowledge in to solve it.

In the wild 语境例句

Once students know the basics, an inquiry task — 'design a game that teaches fractions' — pushes transfer; given to novices with no coding, it would just frustrate.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

探究与显性教学的争论常被误设为对立;循证共识是'先显性后探究':新手需显性打底,有基础后探究促迁移。GTPA中能说清'为何此时选此法'比选边站更重要。
