戚继光抗倭与葡占澳门 Qī Jìguāng kàngwōQi Jiguang, the wokou wars and Macau

⏳ 16世纪中期

元末明初日本武士奸商组成海盗武装“倭寇”侵扰东南沿海；明中期国力减弱海防松懈，倭寇与中国海盗奸商勾结，倭患严重。戚继光临危受命，招募农民矿工万余严格训练成纪律严明的“戚家军”，创“鸳鸯阵”，1561年台州九战九捷平浙东倭患，又赴闽粤，东南倭患基本解除——反侵略的民族英雄（“封侯非我意，但愿海波平”）。同期欧洲殖民者东来：1553年葡萄牙殖民者买通地方官攫取在澳门的居住权（1573年改纳明政府，澳门主权仍属中国、明设守澳官管理）。

Japanese rōnin and smugglers — the wokou, soon thickened with Chinese pirates and corrupt merchants — ravaged the prosperous southeast until the young general Qi Jiguang built his own answer: ten thousand recruited farmers and miners drilled into the incorruptible 'Qi Family Army', fighting in his interlocking 'mandarin-duck' squads. Nine victories in nine battles at Taizhou (1561) broke the scourge in Zhejiang, then Fujian and Guangdong ('a noble title is not my wish — only that the seas be calm'). In the same decades Europe arrived: Portuguese traders bribed their way into a Macau foothold in 1553 — rent paid, sovereignty retained, a Chinese official watching over them.
