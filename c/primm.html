PRIMM 编程教学法 PRIMM biānchéng jiàoxuéfǎPRIMM (Predict-Run-Investigate-Modify-Make)

🔊 PRIMM /prɪm/

专为编程教学设计的循序渐进框架:①预测（Predict,阅读代码猜其行为）②运行（Run,实际执行验证预测）③调查（Investigate,追踪代码为何如此运作）④修改（Modify,改动代码达成新目标）⑤创造（Make,从零编写自己的程序)。它先读后写、降低认知负荷,契合你的IST与Game On教学,是计算机教育界的循证明星方法。

A staged framework designed for teaching programming: (1) Predict (read code and guess its behaviour); (2) Run (execute it to test the prediction); (3) Investigate (trace why it works as it does); (4) Modify (change it to meet a new goal); (5) Make (write your own program from scratch). Reading before writing lowers cognitive load, fits your IST and Game On teaching, and is an evidence-based star of computing education.

Etymology 词源

PRIMM = Predict/Run/Investigate/Modify/Make 首字母缩略——Sue Sentance 等基于社会建构主义与'先读后写'理念提出。
