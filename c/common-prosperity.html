收入分配与共同富裕 gòngtóng fùyùincome distribution and common prosperity

🔊 redistribution /ˌridɪstrəˈbjuʃən/

⏳ 现行制度

按劳分配为主体、多种分配方式并存（劳动、资本、土地、知识、技术、管理、数据等要素按贡献参与分配）。居民收入分初次分配（市场：效率）、再分配（政府：税收、社会保障、转移支付——更加注重公平）、第三次分配（社会：慈善公益）。完善个人收入分配，扎实推进共同富裕——共同富裕是社会主义的本质要求，是全体人民的富裕、人民群众物质生活和精神生活都富裕，不是少数人的富裕、也不是整齐划一的平均主义，要靠共同奋斗、分阶段促进。健全多层次社会保障体系（社会保险、社会救助、社会福利、社会优抚）。

Distribution runs in three rounds: primary (markets rewarding labour and, notably, capital, technology, management and data as factors), redistribution (taxes, transfers, social insurance — 'with greater attention to fairness'), and a 'third distribution' through philanthropy. Above it sits the goal that moved global markets in 2021: common prosperity — defined in the textbook exactly as officials define it to investors: prosperity for all through common effort, in stages; neither the wealth of a few nor absolute egalitarianism. The safety net (insurance, assistance, welfare, veterans' provision) completes the architecture.
