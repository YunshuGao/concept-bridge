加权平均资本成本 jiāquán píngjūn zīběn chéngběnWACC (weighted average cost of capital)

🔊 WACC /wæk/

企业债务成本与股权成本按各自市值权重加权的综合资本成本，是 DCF 中贴现企业自由现金流的标准贴现率。因债务利息可抵税，适度举债能降低 WACC——但过高杠杆又抬升风险成本。

The blended cost of a firm's debt and equity, weighted by their market values — the standard discount rate for a firm's free cash flows in a DCF. Because interest is tax-deductible, moderate debt lowers WACC — but excessive leverage raises risk costs again.

Etymology 词源

Weighted Average Cost of Capital 缩略（读作 'wack'）——把不同来源的资金成本按比例调和为一个数。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ blended cost of capital /ˈblɛndɪd kɔst əv ˈkæpɪtəl/
    The blended cost of capital mixes cheap debt with expensive equity.
  • ▸ the discount rate /ðə ˈdɪskaʊnt reɪt/
    In most DCFs, the discount rate simply is the WACC.
