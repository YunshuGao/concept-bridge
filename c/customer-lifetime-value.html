客户终身价值 kèhù zhōngshēn jiàzhícustomer lifetime value (LTV / CLV)

🔊 customer lifetime value /ˈkəstəmər ˈlaɪfˌtaɪm ˈvælju/

一个客户在其与企业关系全周期内贡献的总利润现值。它是判断'为获取一个客户能花多少钱'的上限，与获客成本（CAC）之比决定商业模式是否成立——LTV 远大于 CAC 才能可持续盈利地增长。

The present value of all the profit a customer contributes over the whole life of the relationship. It sets the ceiling on what you can spend to acquire a customer; its ratio to acquisition cost (CAC) determines whether the model works — LTV must comfortably exceed CAC to grow profitably.

Etymology 词源

lifetime（一生）——客户在'与品牌关系的一生'中创造的总价值;是订阅与电商时代的核心指标。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ lifetime value /ˈlaɪfˌtaɪm ˈvælju/
    Lifetime value tells you how much a customer is worth, not just what they spent today.
  • ▸ customer equity /ˈkəstəmər ˈɛkwəti/
    Customer equity is the whole customer base valued as an asset.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

If a subscriber's LTV is 900 and costs 300 to acquire, the 3:1 LTV/CAC ratio says spend more to grow; flip it, and growth just burns cash.
