多党合作与基层群众自治 duōdǎng hézuòmultiparty cooperation and grassroots self-governance

⏳ 现行制度

中国共产党领导的多党合作和政治协商制度是我国一项基本政治制度：中共是执政党，八个民主党派是参政党（参加国家政权，参与重要方针政策、领导人选协商，参与国家事务管理，参政议政、民主监督）——“长期共存、互相监督、肝胆相照、荣辱与共”；人民政协是专门协商机构（政治协商、民主监督、参政议政）。基层群众自治制度：农村村民委员会、城市居民委员会——自我管理、自我服务、自我教育、自我监督（民主选举、民主协商、民主决策、民主管理、民主监督），是人民当家作主最有效、最广泛的途径。

Two of the 'basic' institutions: the CCP-led multiparty cooperation and political consultation system — eight minor 'participating parties' (not opposition: they 'participate in state power' under the sixteen-character covenant 'long-term coexistence, mutual supervision, treating each other with sincerity, sharing weal and woe'), with the CPPCC as the dedicated consultative chamber; and grassroots self-governance — elected villagers' and residents' committees running the 'four selfs' of local life, billed as democracy's widest, most practical channel. Together with the NPC and regional autonomy, they complete the official institutional map of Chinese democracy.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

参政党 = participating parties（against opposition parties——一词定performance整个制度逻辑）。人民政协 = the CPPCC；统一战线 = the united front（链 [[northern-expedition]] 的概念史）。村民委员会 = villagers' committees（1980年代基层选举曾是西方观察热点）。
