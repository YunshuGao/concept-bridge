墨西哥资产阶级革命与1917年宪法 Mòxīgē gémìngthe Mexican Revolution and the 1917 Constitution

⏳ 1910年–1917年

墨西哥19世纪20年代摆脱西班牙独立后政局动荡、外国势力乘虚而入；1876年迪亚斯政变后军事独裁，土地资源集中于大地主、教会与外国势力。1910年革命爆发推翻迪亚斯，在保守势力与美国干涉下艰难进行；1917年革命结束、颁布宪法：自然资源归国家所有、外国人不得随意开采，收回出卖的租借地，大地产拆分给农民，8小时工作制、承认工会、保护女工童工。

Independent from Spain in the 1820s but unstable and penetrated by foreign capital, Mexico fell under Díaz's long dictatorship, its land and minerals cornered by hacendados, Church and foreigners. The 1910 revolution — fought through conservative backlash and American meddling — closed in 1917 with the era's most radical constitution: national ownership of natural resources, the great estates broken up, the eight-hour day, unions recognised.
