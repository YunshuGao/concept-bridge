原住民视角与文化回应式教学 yuánzhùmín shìjiǎoAboriginal perspectives and culturally responsive teaching

🔊 Aboriginal perspectives and culturally responsive teaching /ˌæbərˈɪʤənəl pərˈspɛktɪvz ənd ˈkəlʧərəˌli rɪˈspɑnsɪv ˈtiʧɪŋ/

在教学中真实、尊重地融入原住民与托雷斯海峡岛民的知识、历史与视角(NESA跨课程重点之一),并采用文化回应式教学——将学生的文化背景视为学习的资源而非障碍。'8 Ways'教学框架（讲故事、图像化、非线性、亲身实践等)提供了本土教学法的具体路径。要求教师避免刻板印象、与社区合作。

Authentically and respectfully embedding Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander knowledge, histories and perspectives in teaching (a NESA cross-curriculum priority), and adopting culturally responsive teaching — treating students' cultural backgrounds as a resource for learning, not a barrier. The '8 Ways' framework (story-sharing, symbols and images, non-linear, land-linked, hands-on, etc.) offers concrete Indigenous pedagogies. It requires teachers to avoid stereotypes and work with community.
