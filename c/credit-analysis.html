信用分析 xìnyòng fēnxīcredit analysis

🔊 credit analysis /ˈkrɛdɪt æˈnælɪsɪs/

评估借款人(公司或国家)按期还本付息的能力与意愿,即违约风险。工具:信用评级(投资级/高收益)、财务比率(杠杆、利息保障)、'信用五C'、行业与经营分析。它决定债券的信用利差与定价,是固定收益与信贷投资的核心工作。

Assessing a borrower's (a company's or country's) ability and willingness to repay interest and principal on time — its default risk. Tools: credit ratings (investment-grade vs high-yield), financial ratios (leverage, interest coverage), the 'five Cs of credit', and industry and business analysis. It drives a bond's credit spread and pricing — the core work of fixed-income and credit investing.

Etymology 词源

credit 源自拉丁 credere（相信）——信用即'可被相信偿还';与 creed、credible 同根。
