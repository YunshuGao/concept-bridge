品牌资产 pǐnpái zīchǎnbrand equity

🔊 brand equity /brænd ˈɛkwəti/

品牌名字本身所承载的、超越产品功能的价值:知名度、认知质量、联想、忠诚。强品牌带来定价权、降低获客成本、抵御竞争与危机——它是资产负债表上看不见却最真实的护城河之一。

The value a brand name carries beyond the product's function: awareness, perceived quality, associations, loyalty. Strong brands command price premiums, lower acquisition costs, and buffer against competition and crises — an invisible line on the balance sheet and one of the most real moats there is.

Etymology 词源

brand 源自古诺斯 brandr（烙印）——牧场主给牲畜'烙印'以示所有;品牌资产即这枚烙印积累的信任价值。
