供方议价力 gōngfāng yìjiàlìsupplier (bargaining) power

🔊 supplier power /səˈplaɪər paʊər/

五力之一:当供应商集中、投入品不可替代、转换成本高时，供方能抬价或限量，压缩买方利润。上游垄断（如光刻机、稀土、关键芯片）是供方权力的极端形态。

One of the five forces: concentrated suppliers of irreplaceable inputs can raise prices or ration supply, squeezing their customers' margins. Upstream chokepoints — lithography machines, rare earths, leading-edge chips — are supplier power at its sharpest.

Etymology 词源

supply 源自拉丁 supplere（填满、补足）——供应即“补足需求的缺口”；供方权力则是控制这份补给。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ input concentration /ˈɪnˌpʊt ˌkɑnsənˈtreɪʃən/

  • ▸ chokepoint /ˈtʃoʊkpɔɪnt/
    A supply chokepoint converts a technical monopoly into geopolitical leverage.
