核心竞争力 héxīn jìngzhēnglìcore competence

🔊 core competence /kɔr ˈkɑmpətɪns/

哈默尔与普拉哈拉德:企业独有、难以模仿、可通向多个市场的能力集合——不是产品，而是产品背后的技能与知识（如索尼的小型化、佳能的光学）。战略应围绕核心能力而非业务单元组织。

Hamel and Prahalad's idea: the unique, hard-to-imitate skills that open many markets — not products but the know-how behind them (Sony's miniaturisation, Canon's optics). Organise strategy around competences, not business units.

Etymology 词源

competence 源自拉丁 competere（一同追求、相称）——能力即“胜任、够格”；与 compete（竞争）同根:能力与竞争本是一词两面。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ distinctive capability /dɪˈstɪŋktɪv ˌkeɪpəˈbɪləti/
    A distinctive capability is what your rivals would need years to grow, not buy.
  • ▸ the crown jewels /ðə kraʊn ʤuəlz/
    Protect the crown jewels; outsource the rest.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

Amazon's core competence isn't retail — it's operating complex logistics and computing at scale, which is why AWS was a natural adjacency.
