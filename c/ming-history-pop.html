🔊 popular history /ˈpɒpjʊlər ˈhɪstəri/

当年明月以网络连载体重写明史:帝王心术、文官集团、言官制度、张居正改革——历史通俗写作的现象级样本(销量千万级)。其方法:正史底本+心理推演+段子文体。

Ming history rewritten as serialised internet prose: imperial psychology, the scholar-official machine, the censorate, Zhang Juzheng's reforms. A publishing phenomenon that defined Chinese popular history — orthodox sources, psychological reconstruction, meme-ready style.

Etymology 词源

censorate(言官系统)为汉学英语术语:censor 源自罗马监察官(censere 评估)——罗马与明朝各自发明了“专职批评官员”,一词两制。
