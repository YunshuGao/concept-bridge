企业风险管理 qǐyè fēngxiǎn guǎnlǐenterprise risk management (ERM)

🔊 enterprise risk management /ˈɛnərˌpraɪz rɪsk ˈmænɪʤmənt/

把分散在各部门的风险管理提升为全企业统一、自上而下的体系,识别相互关联、可能叠加的风险,并与战略和资本配置挂钩。它防止'各扫门前雪'式的孤岛管理漏掉那些跨部门累积、足以致命的系统性风险。

Elevating siloed, departmental risk management into a firm-wide, top-down system that identifies interconnected and compounding risks and ties them to strategy and capital allocation. It prevents the fragmented, everyone-guards-their-own-patch approach from missing the cross-cutting, accumulating risks that can prove fatal.

Etymology 词源

enterprise（企业整体）——把风险管理从部门层面提升到'全企业'视角,故名 ERM。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the risk-aware culture /ðə rɪsk-əˈwɛr ˈkəlʧər/

  • ▸ the three lines of defence /ðə θri laɪnz əv dɪˈfɛns/
    The three lines of defence separate risk-taking, risk oversight and independent audit.

In the wild 语境例句

Siloed risk management saw each exposure as small; enterprise risk management revealed they were all correlated to one factor — and together, existential.
