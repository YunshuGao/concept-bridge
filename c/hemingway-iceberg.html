🔊 Hemingway /ˈhɛmɪŋˌweɪ/

《老人与海》作者的写作学说：八分之七沉在水下——只写表面动作，情感靠省略传达。Hemingwayesque＝极简、短句、白描。名句“人可以被毁灭，但不能被打败”。

From the author of The Old Man and the Sea: show the eighth above water, sink the rest. Hemingwayesque means short declarative sentences and surgical omission. 'A man can be destroyed but not defeated.'

Etymology 词源

iceberg 借自荷兰语 ijsberg（冰山，berg=山）——理论名取“水下八分之七”之喻，见于《午后之死》。
