魏晋南北朝的科技与文化 Wèi-Jìn kējì wénhuàscience and arts of the Wei-Jin era

⏳ 220年–589年

贾思勰《齐民要术》为我国现存最早完整农书：总结农林牧副渔技术，强调顺天时量地利、因地制宜、多种经营——世界农学史地位重要。祖冲之运用刘徽割圆术把圆周率精确到小数点后第七位（领先世界近千年），创《大明历》（岁差仅50秒）、造指南车水碓磨。书法成为专门艺术：锺繇创楷书，东晋王羲之《兰亭集序》“天下第一行书”、被誉“书圣”；顾恺之《女史箴图》《洛神赋图》；云冈龙门石窟融合中外雕塑艺术；范缜《神灭论》系统阐述无神论。（郦道元《水经注》为综合地理巨著。）

Division did not dim invention: Jia Sixie's Essential Techniques for the Common People, China's oldest complete agricultural treatise, codified farming by season, soil and diversification; Zu Chongzhi fixed pi between 3.1415926 and 3.1415927 — unmatched for nearly a thousand years — and calibrated the year to within 50 seconds. Calligraphy became high art: Wang Xizhi's Orchid Pavilion Preface remains 'the finest running script under heaven'; Gu Kaizhi's scroll paintings, and the colossal cave sculptures of Yungang and Longmen — Chinese tradition fused with Buddhist art off the Silk Road — crowned the age; Fan Zhen's Essay on the Extinction of the Soul even argued systematic atheism against it all.
