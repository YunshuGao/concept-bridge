企业价值 qǐyè jiàzhíenterprise value (EV)

🔊 enterprise value /ˈɛnərˌpraɪz ˈvælju/

收购整个企业运营所需的总价:股权市值 + 净债务（+ 少数股东权益、优先股等）。它衡量“不论谁出资，这门生意值多少”，独立于资本结构，故适合跨公司比较，配对 EBITDA 等税前、付息前指标。

The total price to acquire a firm's operations: equity market value + net debt (+ minority interest, preferred, etc.). It measures what the business is worth regardless of how it's financed — capital-structure-neutral, so ideal for cross-company comparison and pairing with pre-interest, pre-tax metrics like EBITDA.

Etymology 词源

enterprise 源自古法语 entreprise（所承担之事，entre 之间 + prendre 拿）——企业即“一桩被承担经营的事业”，其价值涵盖全部资本方。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ total firm value /ˈtoʊtəl fərm ˈvælju/
    Total firm value is what you'd pay to own the operations debt and all.
  • ▸ takeover value /ˈteɪˌkoʊvər ˈvælju/
    Enterprise value is the cheque an acquirer really writes, cash and debt included.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ EV to EBITDA
  • ▸ equity value plus net debt
  • ▸ capital-structure-neutral
  • ▸ the bridge to equity value

In the wild 语境例句

A firm with a 1,000 equity value and 400 net debt has a 1,400 enterprise value — the real price to buy and run it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

股权价值（equity value/市值）≠企业价值（EV）:前者归股东，后者归全体资本方。P/E 用股权价值，EV/EBITDA 用企业价值——配错分子分母是估值常见硬伤。
