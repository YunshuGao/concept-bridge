🔊 Bloom's taxonomy /blumz tækˈsɒnəmi/

把认知目标从低到高分为六层:记忆、理解、应用、分析、评价、创造（修订版）。它帮助教师设计不同思维层次的问题与任务,避免只停留在记忆层。动词是关键:'列出'是记忆,'设计一个游戏'是创造。用于撰写学习目标、分层提问与差异化任务。

Classifying cognitive goals from lower to higher order: remember, understand, apply, analyse, evaluate, create (the revised version). It helps teachers design questions and tasks across levels of thinking, avoiding a diet of mere recall. The verbs are the key: 'list' is remembering, 'design a game' is creating. Used to write learning objectives, tier questions and differentiate tasks.

Etymology 词源

taxonomy 源自希腊 taxis（排列）+ nomos（法则）——分类学即'排列的法则';以 Benjamin Bloom 命名(1956,2001修订)。

  • ▸ the cognitive hierarchy /ðə ˈkɑgnɪtɪv ˈhaɪˌrɑrki/
    The cognitive hierarchy climbs from remembering a fact to inventing something new with it.
