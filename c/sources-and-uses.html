🔊 sources and uses of funds /ˈsɔrsəz ənd ˈjuzɪz əv fəndz/

任何交易(收购、LBO、重组)的第一张表:'运用'列出所需资金(收购股权、偿还既有债务、交易费用),'来源'列出资金从何来(各层债务、股权出资、目标现金)。两侧必须相等。它决定了交易的资本结构与初始杠杆,是LBO模型的起点。

The first schedule of any deal (acquisition, LBO, restructuring): 'uses' lists the funds needed (buying equity, repaying existing debt, fees), 'sources' lists where they come from (each debt tranche, equity contribution, target cash). The two sides must equal. It sets the deal's capital structure and initial leverage — the starting point of the LBO model.

Etymology 词源

sources（来源）与 uses（用途）——资金从哪来、到哪去的一张平衡表,两侧金额必然相等。
