古埃及文明 gǔ Āijí wénmíngancient Egyptian civilisation

🔊 civilisation /ˌsɪvəlɪˈzeɪʃən/

also: ancient Egypt · pharaonic Egypt

⏳ 前3100年–前30年

兴起于尼罗河下游的早期文明，约公元前3100年初步统一，后历经分裂与外族入侵，先后被波斯、亚历山大帝国和罗马帝国占领，文明未能延续至今。

One of the world's earliest civilisations, unified along the lower Nile around 3100 BC; after cycles of division and foreign conquest — Persian, Macedonian, then Roman — its native tradition was eventually extinguished rather than continuously inherited.

Etymology 词源

civilisation 源自拉丁 civis（公民）→ 城市生活之总和。

In the wild 语境例句

Unlike China, whose script and statecraft show an unbroken lineage, ancient Egyptian civilisation was cut short — by the time Rome annexed it, even hieroglyphic literacy was dying out.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

中文教材强调古埃及文明“没有延续下去”，与中华文明的连续性对比；英语中表达这种对比常用 continuity vs rupture、an unbroken tradition 等说法。
