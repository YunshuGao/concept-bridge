股权成本 gǔquán chéngběnthe cost of equity

🔊 cost of equity /kɔst əv ˈɛkwəti/

股东要求的回报率——因股东承担最末位、最高的风险，其要求高于债权人。通常用资本资产定价模型（CAPM）估算:无风险利率 + β × 股权风险溢价。

The return shareholders demand — higher than lenders' because equity holders bear the last, riskiest claim. Usually estimated with the CAPM: the risk-free rate plus beta times the equity risk premium.

Etymology 词源

equity 源自拉丁 aequitas（公平）——股权本指“公平分享剩余”的权利；其成本即股东要的补偿。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ required equity return /rikˈwaɪərd ˈɛkwəti rɪˈtərn/
    The required equity return is what shareholders could earn on equally risky bets elsewhere.
  • ▸ the CAPM rate /ðə ˈkæpəm reɪt/
    The CAPM rate turns a stock's market risk into a required return.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ estimate via CAPM
  • ▸ risk-free rate plus premium
  • ▸ the equity risk premium
  • ▸ levered vs unlevered

In the wild 语境例句

A volatile tech stock's cost of equity might be 12% while a utility's is 7% — beta captures how much market risk each shareholder bears.
