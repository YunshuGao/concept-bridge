抗美援朝 kàng-Měi yuán-Cháothe War to Resist America and Aid Korea

⏳ 1950年–1953年

1950年朝鲜内战爆发，美国出兵干涉并越过三八线扩大侵略、轰炸中国边境城市、第七舰队入侵台湾海峡，威胁新中国安全。应朝方请求，1950年10月彭德怀率中国人民志愿军入朝，与朝鲜军民并肩作战，五战五捷；上甘岭战役中黄继光舍身堵枪眼、邱少云严守纪律烈火中牺牲——志愿军被誉“最可爱的人”。1953年7月美国被迫在《朝鲜停战协定》上签字。抗美援朝战争的胜利，抵御了帝国主义侵略扩张，捍卫了新中国安全，保卫了中国人民和平生活，稳定了朝鲜半岛局势，维护了亚洲和世界和平，大大提高了我国国际地位。

When the Korean civil war internationalised — UN forces driving north to the Yalu, US planes over Chinese towns, the Seventh Fleet in the Taiwan Strait — China answered with the People's Volunteers under Peng Dehuai (October 1950), who threw the front back to the 38th parallel and held it through battles like Shangganling until the 1953 armistice. The textbook frames it as the founding victory: security defended, world standing won, and the martyr-heroes — Huang Jiguang, Qiu Shaoyun — installed in the national pantheon as 'the most beloved people'.

In the wild 语境例句

Same war, three names — Korea's 'Fatherland Liberation War', America's 'forgotten war', China's 'Resist America, Aid Korea' — the naming triangle is the historiography lesson in itself.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

英语通称 the Korean War；中方名直译 the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea（官方英译）。停战协定 = armistice（至今无和约——朝鲜半岛新闻的法律背景板）。志愿军 = the (Chinese People's) Volunteers——“志愿”名义的外交功能值得注意。
