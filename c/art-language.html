美术语言与中西绘画 měishù yǔyánthe language of art: Chinese and Western painting compared

🔊 perspective /pərˈspɛktɪv/

⏳ 教材体系

美术语言的基本元素：点、线、面、形状、明暗、色彩（三要素：色相、明度、纯度；冷暖对比）、肌理、空间；组织原理：构图、比例、对称均衡、节奏韵律。中国画（工具：笔墨纸砚；分人物、山水、花鸟；技法：工笔与写意；以线造型、墨分五色、留白、诗书画印一体、散点透视——可游可居的长卷空间）与西方绘画（焦点透视——文艺复兴确立的科学空间；明暗造型、色彩体系；油画材质）各成体系：中国画重意境神韵（“妙在似与不似之间”），西画重形体光色——各美其美、美美与共。美术门类：绘画、雕塑、建筑、设计、书法（中国特有）、篆刻等；欣赏方法：描述—分析—解释—评价。

Art's grammar as the six junior volumes teach it: elements (line, shape, value, colour's three properties, texture, space) and principles (composition, proportion, balance, rhythm) — plus the great comparative lesson: Chinese painting's linear brush idiom, ink's 'five colours', deliberate blankness, the poem-calligraphy-seal ensemble and the scrolling multi-point perspective you travel through, against Western painting's single-vanishing-point science of space (Renaissance-born), modelling in light and oil's material depth. Xu Beihong's dictum 'the marvel lies between likeness and unlikeness' names the Chinese criterion; 'each beauty its own, beauties shared' (Fei Xiaotong) names the textbook's verdict. Appreciation runs describe-analyse-interpret-judge — the same four steps as any English-language art review.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

散点/焦点透视 = shifting (multi-point) vs linear (fixed-point) perspective（中西空间观的教科书对立——链 [[renaissance]] 透视法、[[weijin-culture]] 顾恺之）。工笔/写意 = gongbi (meticulous) / xieyi (freehand spirit-writing)（拼音直用于英语艺评）。留白 = negative space/deliberate blankness。“妙在似与不似之间”（齐白石）= the marvel lies between likeness and unlikeness。费孝通十六字 = each beauty its own... （链 [[chinese-culture-core]] 和而不同）。
