梵高 FàngāoVincent van Gogh

🔊 post-impressionism /poʊst-ˌɪmˈprɛʃəˌnɪzəm/

⏳ 1853年–1890年

荷兰画家，27岁始作画，短暂生涯中创作大量油画素描版画，《夜间的咖啡馆》《向日葵》为代表——明亮艳丽的黄色花瓣表达对生命的赞美与对美好生活的向往。生前作品不受重视、生活困窘，靠弟弟接济度日。

The Dutch painter who started at 27 and burned out in a decade, leaving the Sunflowers and The Night Café — colour as pure feeling. Unsold and unsung in life, supported by his brother's allowance, he became after death the very archetype of the unrecognised genius.

Etymology 词源

impression（印象）←拉丁 imprimere（压印）——后印象派：印象之后再造形。
