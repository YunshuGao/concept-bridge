薇依《重负与神恩》 wēiyīSimone Weil: gravity, grace and attention

🔊 Weil /veɪ/

法国哲学家-神秘主义者：重力（gravity）指灵魂下坠的自然倾向（自我中心、填补空虚）,神恩（grace）是唯一的反重力。核心概念“关注”（attention）：完全无我的注视即祈祷——“绝对纯粹的关注就是祈祷”。

Weil's physics of the soul: gravity is its natural downward drag (ego, void-filling); grace the only counterforce. Her master concept is attention — utterly selfless attention, she says, is prayer.

Etymology 词源

attention 源自拉丁 ad-（朝向）+ tendere（伸展）——“关注”即“朝向对象伸展自己”,薇依用词源本义。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ attentiveness /əˈtɛntɪvnəs/
    Attentiveness gives the other person the rarest gift: your absence.
  • ▸ decreation /ˌdiːkriˈeɪʃən/
    Decreation is Weil's word for unmaking the ego God's way.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ absolutely unmixed attention is prayer
  • ▸ the needs of the soul
  • ▸ affliction (malheur)
  • ▸ to be rooted

In the wild 语境例句

In an attention economy, Weil reads like sabotage: attention as the substance of prayer, not the inventory of platforms.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

Weil 读音近法语“韦伊”/veɪ/,勿照英文拼读作“威尔”,也勿与社会学家马克斯·韦伯（Weber）混淆。她的 attention 与正念（mindfulness）不同:朝向他者与真理,而非朝向自身状态。
