🔊 transaction cost economics /trænˈzækʃən kɔst ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪks/

科斯与威廉姆森的理论:市场交易本身有成本——搜寻、谈判、签约、监督、违约。当这些成本高于内部组织成本时，活动就被纳入企业。它解释了企业为何存在、边界在哪里。

Coase and Williamson's theory: market transactions carry costs — search, negotiation, contracting, monitoring, enforcement. When these exceed the cost of organising internally, the activity moves inside the firm. It explains why firms exist and where their boundaries fall.

Etymology 词源

transaction 源自拉丁 trans（越过）+ agere（做）——交易即“做成一桩来回”；科斯1937年之问:既然市场有效，为何还要有公司?
