整合技术的学科教学知识（TPACK） zhěnghé jìshù de xuékē jiàoxué zhīshiTPACK (technological pedagogical content knowledge)

🔊 TPACK /ˈtiːpæk/

描述有效技术整合所需的三类知识及其交集:学科内容知识(CK)、教学法知识(PK)、技术知识(TK),三者交叠处即TPACK——知道用什么技术、以什么教法、教什么内容最有效。它提醒:光有技术工具不够,须让技术服务于特定内容的特定教法。是数字教学与你TAS教学的核心框架。

A framework describing the three knowledge types (and their intersections) needed for effective technology integration: Content Knowledge (CK), Pedagogical Knowledge (PK) and Technological Knowledge (TK), overlapping at TPACK — knowing which technology, with which pedagogy, teaches which content best. It warns that having the tool is not enough; technology must serve a specific pedagogy for specific content. A core framework for digital teaching and your TAS practice.

Etymology 词源

TPACK = Technological Pedagogical Content Knowledge——Mishra 与 Koehler(2006)在舒尔曼PCK基础上加入技术维度。

  • ▸ more than the tool /mɔr ðən ðə tul/
    More than the tool: TPACK asks what pedagogy and content justify using it at all.
