孔雀王朝 Kǒngquè wángcháothe Maurya Empire

🔊 empire /ˈɛmpaɪər/

also: the Mauryan dynasty

⏳ 约前324年–前187年

古代印度文明的鼎盛时期（约前324–前187年）：除半岛最南端外基本统一印度，农业与工商业繁荣，首都华氏城是当时世界上最繁华、人口最多的城市之一。

Ancient India's high-water mark (c. 324–187 BC), unifying nearly the whole subcontinent; agriculture and commerce flourished, and the capital Pataliputra ranked among the largest, busiest cities on earth.

Etymology 词源

empire 源自拉丁 imperium（号令权）——帝国即“号令所及”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ imperium /ɪmˈpɪəriəm/
    Imperium ran from the Khyber to the Bay.
  • ▸ hegemony /hɪˈdʒɛməni/
    Hegemony is empire on a handshake.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ at its height/zenith
  • ▸ unify the subcontinent
  • ▸ a flourishing trade

In the wild 语境例句

Two thousand years before 'Make in India', Mauryan Pataliputra was already the subcontinent's commercial showcase.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“鼎盛时期”地道表达：at its height / at its zenith / the golden age of…。华氏城英语名为 Pataliputra（今巴特那 Patna）。
