经济大危机 jīngjì dàwēijīthe Great Depression

⏳ 1929年–1933年

20年代美国繁荣表象下工资增长缓慢、购买力不足导致生产过剩，全民股票投机成风。1929年10月24日“黑色星期四”股价暴跌，危机席卷资本主义世界：1929–33年工业产量降1/3、贸易额减2/3；美国1500–1700万人失业，资本家为保价格销毁牛奶粮食，穷人栖身“胡佛村”棚户区。教材归因于资本主义制度的基本矛盾。

Beneath the Roaring Twenties lay stagnant wages, glutted output and a stockmarket running on borrowed money; Black Thursday (24 October 1929) brought it all down. World industrial output fell by a third, trade by two-thirds; up to 17m Americans were jobless while milk was poured into rivers to hold prices, and shanty 'Hoovervilles' rose in the parks — for the textbook, the basic contradiction of capitalism made visible.
