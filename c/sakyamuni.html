释迦牟尼 Shìjiāmóuníthe Buddha (Siddhartha Gautama)

also: Sakyamuni · Gautama Buddha

⏳ 前6世纪

佛教创始人，迦毗罗卫城释迦族王子；相传因目睹乞丐、病人与弃尸而感悟“人生皆苦”，离家修行，创立佛教，被尊为“佛陀”。

Founder of Buddhism, a prince of the Shakya clan of Kapilavastu who — shaken, tradition says, by the sight of beggary, sickness and death — renounced his palace to seek liberation from suffering, and came to be honoured as the Buddha, 'the awakened one'.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

Buddha 是尊号（“觉悟者”），本名 Siddhartha Gautama；Sakyamuni（释迦族圣人）是汉传佛教常用称呼，英语学术写作多用 the Buddha 或 Gautama。“佛陀”“悟道”= enlightenment/awakening。
