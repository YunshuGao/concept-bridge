🔊 theories of motivation /ˈθɪriz əv ˌmoʊtəˈveɪʃən/

解释人为何工作、如何被激发。马斯洛需求层次:生理→安全→归属→尊重→自我实现。赫茨伯格双因素:'保健因素'（薪资、条件）缺失致不满但存在不激励,'激励因素'（成就、认可、成长）才真正驱动投入。

Explanations of why people work and how they are energised. Maslow's hierarchy: physiological → safety → belonging → esteem → self-actualisation. Herzberg's two factors: 'hygiene factors' (pay, conditions) cause dissatisfaction if absent but don't motivate if present, while 'motivators' (achievement, recognition, growth) truly drive engagement.

Etymology 词源

motivate 源自拉丁 movere（移动）——动机即'使人行动的内在推力',与 motive、emotion 同根。

  • ▸ hygiene vs motivators /ˈhaɪdʒiːn ˈvərsəz ˈmoʊtəveɪtərz/
    Hygiene factors stop dissatisfaction; only motivators create drive.
