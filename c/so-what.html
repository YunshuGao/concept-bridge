🔊 so what /soʊ wət/

对每一项分析、每一页幻灯片反复追问:“所以呢？对决策意味着什么？”——把数据提炼为洞见、把洞见提炼为建议。没通过 so-what 测试的内容，只是信息，不是价值。

Relentlessly asking of every analysis and every slide: 'so what? what does it mean for the decision?' — distilling data into insight and insight into recommendation. Anything that fails the so-what test is information, not value.

Etymology 词源

咨询行话——最简短也最锋利的自我审查:逼作者从“我发现了什么”走到“这对客户意味着什么”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the insight /ðə ˈɪnˌsaɪt/
    An insight is a fact that has survived the 'so what?' question.
  • ▸ the implication /ðə ˌɪmpləˈkeɪʃən/
    The implication is what the client should do differently because of the number.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ pass the so-what test
  • ▸ from data to insight
  • ▸ the 'now what'
  • ▸ distil the implication

In the wild 语境例句

'Sales fell 8%' is a fact; the so-what — 'so we're losing the price war and must reposition' — is the insight the client is paying for.
