新航路的开辟 xīn hánglù de kāipìthe Age of Discovery

🔊 navigation /ˌnævəˈgeɪʃən/

also: the Age of Exploration · the opening of new sea routes

⏳ 1487年–1522年

欧洲市场扩大、资产阶级渴求新市场；东方商品经层层转手价格涨8–10倍，奥斯曼灭拜占庭后又控制商道征高额关税——欧洲人遂寻通往东方的新航路。地圆说流行、中国指南针经阿拉伯人传入、造船术突破为其提供条件。迪亚士1488年抵好望角，达·伽马1498年达印度获利数十倍。新航路使欧洲与亚非美建立直接商业联系，世界开始连为一个整体。

With markets swelling and Eastern goods marked up eight- to ten-fold through middlemen — then taxed at will once the Ottomans seized the trade routes — Europeans went looking for a sea road to Asia, armed with the round-earth revival, the Chinese compass (via Arab traders) and bigger ships. Dias rounded the Cape of Good Hope (1488), da Gama reached India (1498) at a profit of dozens of times his costs — and the world's regions, long sealed from one another, began to knit into one.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

英语通称 the Age of Discovery/Exploration；近年学界多用中性说法 European expansion（“发现”暗含欧洲中心视角——教材给“发现”加引号正是同一自觉）。好望角命名故事：Storm Cape → Cape of Good Hope（约翰二世改名，做品牌管理的鼻祖）。“世界连为一体”即 the birth of a single world / globalisation's first act。
