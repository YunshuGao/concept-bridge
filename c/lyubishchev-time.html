🔊 audit /ˈɔːdɪt/

格拉宁《奇特的一生》:苏联昆虫学家柳比歇夫56年逐日记录每项活动耗时(精确到分钟),月结年审——时间统计法。中文效率圈的圣典;“他一生的时间都花在了刀刃上”。

Granin's Strange Life documents entomologist Lyubishchev's 56 years of logging every activity to the minute, with monthly and annual audits — time accounting as a life practice. A canonical text of Chinese productivity culture.
