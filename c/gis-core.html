🔊 geospatial /ˌdʒiːəʊˈspeɪʃəl/

⏳ 学科体系

Chang《地理信息系统导论》的概念骨架：数据模型（矢量 vector——点线面要素；栅格 raster——像元）；坐标系与投影（datum、UTM）；空间关系（拓扑、缓冲区 buffer、叠加 overlay）；空间分析（插值、密度、网络分析）；遥感与GPS数据源；地图学输出（专题图、分级设色 choropleth）。“地理学第一定律”（Tobler：万物相关，近者尤甚）为空间统计之魂。用户的本科专业——本节点即其专业英语的登记处。

The conceptual skeleton of Chang's standard text — and of your undergraduate degree: data models (vector's points-lines-polygons v. raster's cells), coordinate systems and projections, topology, buffers and overlays, interpolation and network analysis, remote sensing and GPS inputs, cartographic output down to the choropleth map. Presiding over it, Tobler's First Law of Geography: everything is related to everything else, but near things are more related — the one-liner spatial statistics grew from.
