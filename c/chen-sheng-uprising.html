概念桥 ConceptBridge 第10课 秦末农民大起义

秦的暴政与陈胜吴广起义 Chén Shèng Wú Guǎng qǐyìQin tyranny and the Chen Sheng-Wu Guang rising

⏳ 前209年–前202年

秦始皇急于求成而暴虐：赋税征收获物2/3，每年徭役兵役300万人（骊山陵、阿房宫、长城、南征），法律严苛株连、刑罚残酷，又“焚书坑儒”禁锢思想。秦二世更甚。前209年戍卒遇雨误期当斩，陈胜吴广在大泽乡揭竿而起——“王侯将相宁有种乎！”中国历史上第一次农民大起义。起义虽败，项羽巨鹿之战破釜沉舟歼秦军主力，刘邦前207年入咸阳，秦仅十几年而亡；楚汉之争四年，善用人才收揽民心的刘邦胜刚愎自用的项羽。

The First Emperor built at frenzy's pace on the backs of the taxed and conscripted — two-thirds of harvests taken, three million a year on corvée, books burned and scholars buried alive — and his son was worse. In 209 BC conscripts delayed by rain and facing execution rose at Daze: 'are kings and nobles born to it?' — China's first great peasant revolt. It failed, but Xiang Yu burned his boats at Julu and destroyed the Qin army, Liu Bang took the capital in 207 BC, and the dynasty built for ten thousand generations died at fifteen. Four more years of Chu-Han war crowned the man who could use talent over the man who could only fight.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“焚书坑儒”= the burning of books and burying of scholars（英语史学固定语）。“王侯将相宁有种乎”是中国平等主义的原声——可与 [[english-revolution]] 时代平等派并读。象棋“楚河汉界”即楚汉之争的活化石。“暴政”= tyranny；“徭役”= corvée (labour)。
