丝绸之路与张骞通西域 Sīchóu zhī lùthe Silk Road and Zhang Qian's missions

⏳ 前138年–前60年

前138年张骞应募出使西域联络大月氏夹击匈奴，两度被匈奴扣留（前后十余年）“持汉节不失”，前119年再使乌孙等国——“凿空”之举使汉与西域相互了解往来。商人自长安经河西走廊、西域达中亚西亚转欧洲：丝绸漆器与开渠凿井铸铁技术西去，核桃葡萄石榴苜蓿良马乐器东来——沟通欧亚的“丝绸之路”，古代东西方往来的大动脉。汉又辟海上丝绸之路（经马六甲达印度锡兰）。前60年西汉设西域都护，标志西域（今新疆及巴尔喀什湖以东以南）正式归属中央政权。东汉班超经营西域30余年，遣甘英使大秦（罗马）至安息受阻。

In 138 BC Zhang Qian volunteered to cross Xiongnu country and recruit the Yuezhi — captured twice, held thirteen years, 'never letting fall the Han envoy's staff'. His 'road-opening' linked the Han to Central Asia: silk and ironworking went west from Chang'an through the Hexi corridor, grapes, walnuts, alfalfa, blood-horses and lutes came east — the Silk Road, Eurasia's great artery, with a maritime branch reaching India and Ceylon. In 60 BC the Protectorate of the Western Regions placed today's Xinjiang under central administration; under the Eastern Han, Ban Chao ran the Regions for thirty years and his envoy Gan Ying reached the Persian Gulf, one sea short of Rome.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

Silk Road 一词实为德国地理学家李希霍芬1877年所造（die Seidenstraße）——概念史彩蛋。“凿空”可译 the road-opening/piercing the void（需注释）。西域都护府是新疆自古属中国论述的关键史实——英语表述 the Protectorate of the Western Regions (60 BC)。“一带一路”官方英译 the Belt and Road Initiative (BRI)。
