《掌控习惯》（原子习惯） yuánzǐ xíguànAtomic Habits

🔊 habit /ˈhæbət/

⏳ 2018年

克利尔的行为改变框架：微小改进的复利（每天好1%，一年37倍）；结果滞后于系统（“你不会升到目标的高度，只会落到系统的水平”）；身份认同驱动（不是“我要跑步”而是“我是跑步的人”）；习惯回路四法则——提示（显而易见）、渴求（有吸引力）、反应（简便易行：两分钟法则）、奖赏（令人满足）；环境设计胜过意志力。

Clear's behaviour-change canon: the compound interest of 1% daily gains; 'you do not rise to the level of your goals, you fall to the level of your systems'; identity-based habits ('I am a runner' beats 'I want to run'); the four laws on the cue-craving-response-reward loop — obvious, attractive, easy (the two-minute rule), satisfying; and environment design over willpower. Self-help's current lingua franca — its vocabulary now appears in teacher PD and product design alike.

Etymology 词源

habit 源自拉丁 habere（持有）→ habitus（习性、衣袍）——习惯即“穿在身上的持有”。
