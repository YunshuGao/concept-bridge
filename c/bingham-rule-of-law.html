🔊 rule of law /rul əv lɔ/

⏳ 2010年

英国前首席大法官宾厄姆对“法治”的权威界定：核心是“所有人和机构皆受法律约束并享其保护，法律公开、面向未来、由法院公开施行”。八项原则：法律可及且可预测；依法而非裁量决定权利；法律面前平等；公权力须善意、合目的、不越权行使；保护基本人权；提供可负担的争端解决；程序公正；国家遵守国际法义务。“薄”法治（形式合法）与“厚”法治（含人权）之辨的标准文本——分析中国“依法治国”表述的最佳英方参照系。

The late Lord Bingham's authoritative statement: 'all persons and authorities within the state, whether public or private, should be bound by and entitled to the benefit of laws publicly made... and publicly administered in the courts', unpacked into eight ingredients — accessible law; rights decided by law not discretion; equality before the law; public power exercised in good faith, for proper purpose, within limits; human-rights protection; affordable dispute resolution; fair procedure; and state compliance with international law. The standard text for the thin/thick rule-of-law distinction — and the sharpest available lens for parsing 依法治国.
