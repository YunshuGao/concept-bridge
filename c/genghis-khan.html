成吉思汗统一蒙古 Chéngjísī HánGenghis Khan and the Mongol rise

⏳ 1162年–1227年

12世纪蒙古草原各部为人口草场频繁战争、生灵涂炭。铁木真幼年丧父历经磨难，深感唯统一能致和平；多次征讨败诸强部，1206年统一蒙古草原建蒙古政权，被尊“成吉思汗”（“大海”之意）。建军政生产合一制度、组建万人护卫、立司法机构、创制蒙古文字。此后蒙古1227年灭西夏（成吉思汗征西夏时逝于六盘山）、1234年联宋灭金；蒙宋战争40余年（蒙哥死于合州钓鱼城下）。

Orphaned into a steppe at war with itself, Temujin concluded that only unity could end the killing — and imposed it, breaking every rival confederation until the assembled tribes hailed him Genghis Khan, 'oceanic ruler', in 1206. He gave the steppe what it had never had: a decimal army-state, a guard corps, a law code and a script. The machine then devoured the neighbourhood — Western Xia by 1227 (he died on that campaign), Jin by 1234, and the Southern Song after four more decades in which one khan died besieging a single Sichuan fortress.
