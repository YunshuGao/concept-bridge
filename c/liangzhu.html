良渚古城与中华五千年文明 LiángzhǔLiangzhu and 5,000 years of Chinese civilisation

⏳ 约前3300年–前2300年

良渚古城（杭州余杭，距今5300–4300年）：宫殿区内城外城、同期世界最大的水利系统（影响面积100平方千米）、30万平方米人工高台，工程总量超1000万立方米——见证经济发展水平与统治者的组织调动能力；权贵墓随葬精美玉琮玉璧玉钺与普通墓对比鲜明，阶级分化明显。良渚证实距今约5000年长江下游已出现早期国家、进入文明社会；2019年列入《世界遗产名录》。（陶寺、大汶口、龙山等发现共同表明中华文明起源“多元一体”。）

Liangzhu, near Hangzhou (5,300–4,300 years ago): a walled city with palace precinct, the largest water-management system of its age anywhere, and monumental platforms whose earthworks exceed ten million cubic metres — evidence of a state that could command labour on that scale, its elite buried under heaps of ritual jades while commoners took almost nothing. It is the site that grounds the claim of '5,000 years of Chinese civilisation' in archaeology, UNESCO-listed in 2019; with Taosi, Dawenkou and Longshan it shows a civilisation born plural and converging — 'diverse in origin, one in body'.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“多元一体”官方译法 diverse in origin yet unified / pluralistic unity——中华文明叙事的关键词，英译不统一，引用时宜附中文。“早期国家”= early state；“阶级分化”= social stratification。玉琮玉璧英语直接作 cong、bi。
