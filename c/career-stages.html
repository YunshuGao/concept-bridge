🔊 the four career stages /ðə fɔr kərɪr ˈsteɪʤɪz/

APST把教师专业成长划为四阶:毕业（Graduate,完成职前培养、达标可任教）、熟练（Proficient,通过认证的合格在职教师,多数教师的目标）、高度精湛（Highly Accomplished）、引领（Lead,示范与引领同行）。GTPA对标毕业阶段;新教师入职后须在规定年限内从毕业晋升至熟练。

The APST maps teacher growth across four stages: Graduate (completed initial training, meeting the standard to teach), Proficient (an accredited, fully qualified practising teacher — most teachers' target), Highly Accomplished, and Lead (modelling and leading peers). The GTPA is pitched at the Graduate stage; new teachers must progress from Graduate to Proficient within a set timeframe after starting.
