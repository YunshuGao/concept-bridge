🔊 LBO returns bridge /ˌɛlbiːˈoʊ rɪˈtərnz brɪʤ/

拆解私募股权收益从何而来的三大来源:①去杠杆(用现金流还债,债务减少直接增加股权价值);②EBITDA增长(收入增长+利润率改善);③倍数扩张(退出倍数高于进入倍数,最不可控、最靠运气)。好的PE靠前两者创造价值,靠倍数扩张赚钱则更接近赌博。面试必考的价值创造拆解。

Decomposing where private-equity returns come from, into three sources: (1) deleveraging (paying down debt from cash flow directly grows equity value); (2) EBITDA growth (revenue growth plus margin improvement); (3) multiple expansion (exiting at a higher multiple than entry — the least controllable, most luck-dependent). Good PE creates value through the first two; relying on multiple expansion is closer to gambling. A must-know value-creation decomposition for interviews.

Etymology 词源

bridge（桥）——把入场股权到出场股权的价值变化'搭桥'拆解为若干来源;归因分析的标准工具。
