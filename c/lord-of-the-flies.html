🔊 conch /kɒŋk/

戈尔丁1954年小说：流落荒岛的男孩们从文明退化为部落暴力——“文明只是薄薄一层外壳”的标准英语引用；海螺（conch）成为议事规则与合法性的象征。

Golding's boys descend from choir to tribe: English's standard citation for the thin veneer of civilisation. The conch — speak only while holding it — is shorthand for procedural legitimacy and its fragility.
