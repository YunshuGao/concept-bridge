中国特色社会主义理论体系 Zhōngguó tèsè shèhuìzhǔyìsocialism with Chinese characteristics

🔊 ideology /ˌaɪdiˈɑləʤi/

⏳ 1982年至今

邓小平提出“走自己的道路，建设有中国特色的社会主义”（1982年十二大）；十三大系统阐述社会主义初级阶段理论、“一个中心两个基本点”基本路线；1992年南方谈话（“发展才是硬道理”）进一步解放思想，十四大确立邓小平理论在全党的指导地位（十五大写入党章）——改革开放和现代化建设的总设计师。此后：江泽民“三个代表”重要思想、胡锦涛科学发展观相继写入党章；2012年十八大后习近平新时代中国特色社会主义思想（2017年十九大写入党章），提出中国梦（中华民族伟大复兴）、“两个一百年”奋斗目标、“四个全面”战略布局、新发展理念，全面建成小康社会、开启全面建设社会主义现代化国家新征程。

Deng's 1982 formula — 'build socialism with Chinese characteristics' — grew into the party's evolving theoretical canon: the primary-stage doctrine and the basic line ('one centre, two basic points'), the 1992 southern tour ('development is the hard truth') that relaunched reform, then each leader's contribution written successively into the constitution — Deng Xiaoping Theory, Jiang's Three Represents, Hu's Scientific Outlook, and from 2017 Xi Jinping Thought for a New Era, with its Chinese Dream of national rejuvenation, centenary goals and new development concepts. To read a party congress communiqué is to read this chapter's vocabulary verbatim.

In the wild 语境例句

'Socialism with Chinese characteristics' earns a knowing smile in western commentary ('capitalism with Chinese characteristics' the standing joke) — but the phrase's official English is fixed, and decoding congress-speak requires exactly this glossary.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

官方定译集：中国特色社会主义 = socialism with Chinese characteristics；南方谈话 = the southern tour/talks；中国梦 = the Chinese Dream；中华民族伟大复兴 = the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation；“三个代表”= the Three Represents。政治文本英译的规范性（新华社定译不可自创）是本节点的元教训——Economist 引用时的括注风格值得模仿。
