横向一体化 héngxiàng yìtǐhuàhorizontal integration

🔊 horizontal integration /ˌhɔrəˈzɑntəl ˌɪnəˈgreɪʃən/

在价值链同一层级并购竞争者或相邻产品线，以扩大市场份额、消除竞争、获取规模经济与范围经济。与纵向一体化（沿链条上下）方向正交。

Acquiring competitors or adjacent product lines at the same level of the value chain to grow share, remove rivalry, and gain scale and scope economies. Orthogonal to vertical integration (up and down the chain).

Etymology 词源

horizontal 源自希腊 horizōn（分界的圆，horizein 划界）——横向即“同一水平线上”的扩张。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ consolidation /kənˌsɑləˈdeɪʃən/
    Consolidation is horizontal integration told from the industry's point of view.
  • ▸ roll-up /ˈroʊlʌp/
    A roll-up buys a fragmented sector one small rival at a time.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ consolidate the market
  • ▸ bolt-on acquisitions
  • ▸ industry roll-up
  • ▸ eliminate a competitor

In the wild 语境例句

When a market fragments into many subscale players, a roll-up strategy uses horizontal integration to manufacture scale economics.
