孔子与儒家 Kǒngzǐ / RújiāConfucius and Confucianism

⏳ 前551年–前479年

春秋后期鲁国人，儒家创始人。核心思想“仁”：仁者爱人、“己所不欲勿施于人”，为处理人际关系的最高准则；政治上推崇西周制度、主张以德治国（“为政以德，譬如北辰”）、反对苛政。创办私学打破贵族垄断教育，主张“有教无类”，弟子三千；总结教学规律（因材施教、温故知新），晚年整理文化典籍。弟子辑其言为《论语》。战国孟子倡“仁政”、提出“民为贵，社稷次之，君为轻”；荀子主“礼治”明尊卑。

A minor Lu aristocrat turned teacher, the founder of Confucianism. Its core is ren, humaneness — 'do not impose on others what you would not wish for yourself' — as the supreme rule of human relations; in politics, rule by virtue and example ('like the pole star'), never by harshness. He opened teaching to all comers ('education without class distinction'), broke the aristocratic monopoly on learning with three thousand disciples, and edited the classics; his sayings became the Analects. Mencius later preached benevolent government — 'the people first, the altars second, the ruler last' — while Xunzi answered with ritual and rank.

'Confucian values' explains everything from Asian growth to Asian authoritarianism, depending on the columnist — the sage does more op-ed work than any thinker alive.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

Confucius 为拉丁化名（Kong Fuzi→Confucius，耶稣会士手笔）；“仁”译 humaneness/benevolence（无定译，学术常直接用 ren）。“己所不欲勿施于人”即西方伦理的 the (negative) Golden Rule——先于基督教版本五百年，是跨文明伦理对话的标准引证。“有教无类”= education for all without class distinction。
