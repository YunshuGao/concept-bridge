民族区域自治制度 mínzú qūyù zìzhìregional ethnic autonomy

⏳ 1947年至今

我国民族格局“大杂居、小聚居、相互交错”；党把民族平等、民族团结、各民族共同繁荣作为处理民族关系的基本原则，实行民族区域自治制度（基本政治制度）：在国家统一领导下，各少数民族聚居地方实行区域自治、设自治机关行使自治权——内蒙古（1947，第一个）、新疆、广西、宁夏、西藏五大自治区及自治州县。国家采取优惠政策（西部大开发、兴边富民；对口支援西藏；保护少数民族文化如整理《格萨尔王传》等）促进各民族共同繁荣发展，铸牢中华民族共同体意识。

For a population 'mixed at large, clustered in pockets', the PRC's answer is regional ethnic autonomy: self-governing organs in minority-concentrated areas under unified state leadership — five autonomous regions from Inner Mongolia (1947) to Tibet, plus prefectures and counties — under the triple principle of equality, unity and common prosperity, with preferential development policies (the Western Development drive, paired-assistance to Tibet) and cultural preservation from the Gesar epic down. The current keyword caps the account: forging a shared consciousness of the Chinese nation as one community.
