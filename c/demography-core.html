🔊 demography /dɪˈmɑgrəfi/

⏳ 学科体系

人口学教科书（Lundquist等）的概念基座：人口转变理论（demographic transition——高出生高死亡→高出生低死亡（人口爆炸）→低低均衡；第二次人口转变：低于更替水平）；总和生育率（TFR，更替水平2.1）；人口金字塔与年龄结构（人口红利 demographic dividend、老龄化 population ageing、抚养比）；预期寿命；迁移（推拉理论）；队列与时期分析（cohort/period）。中国语境：从计划生育到三孩政策与“未富先老”——Economist 人口报道的全部词汇。

The textbook toolkit of your master's field: demographic transition theory (high-high → boom → low-low, with the second transition below replacement); total fertility rate against the 2.1 replacement benchmark; population pyramids, dependency ratios, the demographic dividend and its ageing aftermath; life expectancy; migration's push-pull; cohort v. period analysis. Applied to China — one-child to three-child, 'getting old before getting rich' — it is the vocabulary of every Economist demography chart, which is to say, several per issue.
