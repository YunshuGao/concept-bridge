🔊 normalisation adjustments /ˌnɔːməlaɪˈzeɪʃən əˈʤəstmənts/

建模前把历史财务'洗干净'的调整:剔除一次性项目、还原会计政策差异、调整非经常性损益、标准化营运资本、修正关联交易定价,得出可持续、可预测的历史基准,再据此预测未来。垃圾进垃圾出——不做正常化,再精巧的模型也建在被扭曲的历史上。

Cleaning historical financials before modelling: stripping out one-off items, undoing accounting-policy differences, adjusting non-recurring gains and losses, standardising working capital, and correcting related-party pricing, to arrive at a sustainable, predictable historical base from which to forecast. Garbage in, garbage out — without normalisation, even an elegant model rests on distorted history.
