🔊 stakeholder capitalism /ˈsteɪˌkhoʊldər ˈkæpɪtəˌlɪzəm/

企业应为谁服务的根本之争:股东至上论（弗里德曼:企业的社会责任就是增加利润）vs 利益相关者论（企业应平衡股东、员工、客户、社区、环境的利益）。ESG 浪潮把这场半世纪的辩论重新推上台前。

The fundamental dispute over whom a company serves: shareholder primacy (Friedman: the social responsibility of business is to increase profits) versus stakeholder capitalism (balancing shareholders, employees, customers, communities and the environment). The ESG wave has pushed this half-century debate back to centre stage.

Etymology 词源

stakeholder（利益相关方）——原指'持赌注者';1960年代引申为'在企业中有利害关系的各方',与 shareholder（股东）对举。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the Friedman doctrine /ðə ˈfridmən ˈdɔktərɪn/
    The Friedman doctrine says a manager spending shareholders' money on social causes is taxing without consent.
  • ▸ shareholder primacy /ˈʃɛˌrhoʊldər ˈpraɪməsi/
    Shareholder primacy makes one interest sovereign and calls it clarity.
