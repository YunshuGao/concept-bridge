显性教学 xiǎnxìng jiàoxuéexplicit (direct) teaching

🔊 explicit teaching /ɪkˈsplɪsət ˈtiʧɪŋ/

清晰、有序、循序渐进地直接教授知识与技能,而非期望学生自行发现:明确目标、分解步骤、示范、检查理解、引导练习、独立练习、回顾。大量证据（尤其对新手与弱势学生）表明其高效。它是罗森夏因教学原理与澳洲HITS的核心,与探究式教学互补而非对立。

Teaching knowledge and skills clearly, in a structured, step-by-step way, rather than expecting students to discover them: clear goals, broken-down steps, modelling, checking for understanding, guided practice, independent practice, review. Strong evidence (especially for novices and disadvantaged students) shows it works. It is central to Rosenshine's principles and Australia's HITS, complementary to inquiry rather than opposed.

Etymology 词源

explicit 源自拉丁 explicare（展开、说明,ex- + plicare 折叠）——显性即'把内容明白展开'教,不留学生猜。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ direct instruction /dɪˈrɛkt ˌɪnˈstrəkʃən/
    Direct instruction shows and tells before it asks students to try alone.
  • ▸ Rosenshine's principles /ˈroʊzənʃaɪnz ˈprɪnsəpəlz/
    Rosenshine's principles distil explicit teaching into small steps and frequent checks.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ explicit instruction
  • ▸ check for understanding
  • ▸ small steps
  • ▸ model and guide

In the wild 语境例句

For novices learning to code, explicit teaching — model the syntax, check understanding, guide practice — beats leaving them to discover it and fail.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

显性教学≠满堂灌:它含大量检查理解与引导练习,并非教师独白。它与探究式学习是'何时用'的问题——新手打基础宜显性,已有基础做迁移宜探究。
