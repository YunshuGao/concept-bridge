《荷马史诗》 Hémǎ shǐshīthe Homeric epics

also: the Iliad and the Odyssey

⏳ 约前8世纪

希腊宝贵的文学遗产，也是了解早期希腊社会的主要文献；希腊神话“神人同形同性”的特点在其中充分体现。

Greece's foundational literature — the Iliad and the Odyssey — and the chief window onto early Greek society; their gods, made in man's image with man's tempers, embody the Greek habit of humanising the divine.

In the wild 语境例句

Tech writing is stitched from Homer: malware arrives as a Trojan horse, a start-up's decade of pivots is an odyssey, and every giant has an Achilles' heel.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“神人同形同性”= anthropomorphic gods（神具人形人性）。荷马衍生的活英语：odyssey（漫长历程）、Achilles' heel（致命弱点）、Trojan horse（内藏之患）、siren call（诱惑）——Economist 每期都在用。
