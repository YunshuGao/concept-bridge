市场与政府（决定性作用与宏观调控） shìchǎng yǔ zhèngfǔmarket and state: decisive role and macro regulation

⏳ 1992年至今

市场通过价格、供求、竞争机制配置资源——“使市场在资源配置中起决定性作用”（十八届三中全会由“基础性”升格）；但市场调节存在自发性、盲目性、滞后性，会失灵（垄断、外部性、公共物品缺位、分配不公），故须“更好发挥政府作用”：科学的宏观调控（经济政策：财政政策与货币政策；经济规划）、市场监管、公共服务、社会保障——构建市场机制有效、微观主体有活力、宏观调控有度的经济体制。

The 2013 formula that moved markets worldwide: resources allocated by the market's 'decisive role' (upgraded from 'basic'), paired inseparably with 'giving better play to the role of government'. The textbook teaches market failure by the book — monopoly, externalities, public goods, inequality — and the state's answering toolkit: macro regulation through fiscal and monetary policy, planning, regulation and public services. The Chinese economic-policy debate, in other words, is conducted in mainstream economics vocabulary with one extra term of art: 宏观调控.

In the wild 语境例句

'Decisive role' v. 'better play to government' is the tightrope every Third Plenum communiqué walks — analysts count which phrase comes first the way Kremlinologists once counted podium positions.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

宏观调控 = macro regulation/macroeconomic regulation（中国特色政策词，直译通行）；市场失灵 = market failure；外部性 = externalities；公共物品 = public goods——本课直接教授经济学英语基本盘。“看得见的手与看不见的手”回链 [[adam-smith]]、[[new-deal]]。
