自由现金流 zìyóu xiànjīnliúfree cash flow (FCF)

🔊 free cash flow /fri kæʃ floʊ/

企业在维持与扩张经营所需投资之后，真正可自由分配给资本提供者（股东、债权人）的现金:经营现金流减资本开支。“利润是观点，现金是事实”——FCF 是估值与偿债能力的硬通货。

The cash a firm truly has left for its capital providers (shareholders, lenders) after the investment needed to maintain and grow the business: operating cash flow minus capex. 'Profit is an opinion, cash is a fact' — FCF is the hard currency of valuation and solvency.

Etymology 词源

free 指“自由支配”——扣除必要投资后剩下的、可自由分给资本方的现金，故名。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ cash generation /kæʃ ˌʤɛnərˈeɪʃən/
    Cash generation is the reality check accrual profit sometimes fails.
  • ▸ owner earnings /ˈoʊnər ˈərnɪŋz/
    Buffett's 'owner earnings' is free cash flow seen through a shareholder's eyes.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ generate free cash flow
  • ▸ FCF conversion
  • ▸ operating cash flow minus capex
  • ▸ cash-generative business

In the wild 语境例句

A firm can report rising profits while free cash flow bleeds — inventory and receivables swallowing the earnings before they become cash.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

FCFF（对全体资本方）与 FCFE（仅对股东）须区分:前者贴现用 WACC 得企业价值，后者贴现用股权成本得股权价值。混用会算错。
