范围经济 fànwéi jīngjìeconomies of scope

🔊 economies of scope /ɪˈkɑnəmiz əv skoʊp/

用同一套资源生产多种产品比分别生产更便宜——共享品牌、渠道、研发、平台。范围经济解释了多元化与平台扩张的成本逻辑，区别于靠“更多同一产品”的规模经济。

Producing several products from shared resources costs less than making each separately — shared brand, channels, R&D, platform. Scope economies explain diversification and platform expansion, as distinct from scale (more of the same).

Etymology 词源

scope 源自希腊 skopos（目标、观察者，skopein 看）——范围即“视野所及”；范围经济即一套资源覆盖更宽的产品视野。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ cross-selling /krɔs-ˈsɛlɪŋ/
    Cross-selling is scope economics realised at the point of sale.
