即兴剧场与地位游戏 jíxìng jùchǎngImpro: status transactions

🔊 improvisation /ˌɪmprəvaɪˈzeɪʃən/

约翰斯通《即兴》的核心发现:一切对话都是地位交易（status transaction）——人无时不在微调高位/低位姿态;“Yes, and”原则:接受并叠加。多内兰《演员与目标》、林克莱特《释放天性的声音》补全表演三角。

Johnstone's Impro: every conversation is a status transaction — we play ourselves up or down moment to moment. 'Yes, and' accepts the offer and builds. Donnellan's Actor and the Target and Linklater's voice work complete the triangle.

Etymology 词源

improvise 源自拉丁 improvisus（未预见的:in-否定+pro-先+videre看）——即兴即“未先看过”;status 拉丁语“站立的姿态”:地位本义是站姿,约翰斯通照字面用。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ status transaction /ˈsteɪtəs trænˈzækʃən/
    Watch any staff meeting as status transactions and the agenda becomes subtitles.
  • ▸ yes-and /jɛs ænd/
    'Yes, and' is acceptance weaponised into momentum.

  • ▸ play high/low status

In the wild 语境例句

Classroom management is applied Johnstone: the teacher who can drop status voluntarily owns the room's see-saw.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

status 此处非社会阶层而是须臾之间的姿态博弈——与布迪厄的结构性地位互补:一个论毫秒,一个论世代。教师、演讲者、面试者的实操心理学。
