雅尔塔会议与波茨坦会议 Yǎ'ěrtǎ huìyìthe Yalta and Potsdam conferences

⏳ 1945年

1945年2月罗斯福、丘吉尔、斯大林在雅尔塔开会：决定彻底消灭德国法西斯主义、战后分区占领德国、成立联合国；苏联承诺欧战结束后3个月内对日作战（美苏就苏联参战背着中国商定外蒙古独立、租旅顺港等条件，严重侵犯中国主权）。7月波茨坦会议重申雅尔塔精神，以中美英名义发表《波茨坦公告》敦促日本投降，重申《开罗宣言》（1943：日本窃取的中国领土如东北、台湾及澎湖列岛归还中国）必须实施。

At Yalta (February 1945) Roosevelt, Churchill and Stalin settled the endgame: Germany to be destroyed as a fascist power and jointly occupied, a United Nations to be founded, Soviet entry against Japan within three months of victory in Europe — with concessions over Mongolia and Port Arthur made at China's expense and behind its back. Potsdam in July reaffirmed the design and demanded Japan's surrender, restating the Cairo Declaration: the stolen territories, Taiwan included, to be returned to China.
