可比公司倍数 kěbǐ gōngsī bèishùcomparable company (trading) multiples

🔊 trading multiples /ˈtreɪdɪŋ ˈməltəpəlz/

相对估值法:用一组同业上市公司的市场倍数（如 EV/EBITDA、P/E）给目标公司定价——“市场愿为类似公司的每单位盈利付多少”。快、贴市场，但会把市场的错误一并继承。

Relative valuation: pricing a target using the market multiples of a peer group of listed companies (EV/EBITDA, P/E) — 'what the market pays per unit of earnings for similar firms'. Fast and market-anchored, but it inherits the market's mistakes too.

Etymology 词源

multiple（倍数）——价格是某财务指标的“若干倍”；trading 指来自公开交易市场的实时价格。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ comps /kɑmpz/
    Comps tell you the going rate; a DCF tells you the fair one.
  • ▸ relative valuation /ˈrɛlətɪv væljuˈeɪʃən/
    Relative valuation is a beauty contest priced by the crowd.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ a peer comp set
  • ▸ trade at 10x EBITDA
  • ▸ the comparable universe
  • ▸ apply the multiple

In the wild 语境例句

If peers trade at 12x EBITDA and the target earns 100 of EBITDA, comps imply roughly 1,200 of enterprise value — before adjusting for its lower growth.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

倍数法的陷阱:可比公司永不完全可比（增长、风险、资本结构各异），且整个板块可能被市场同时高估或低估——须与 DCF 三角验证。
