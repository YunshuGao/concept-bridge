春秋争霸与王室衰微 Chūnqiū zhēngbàthe Spring and Autumn hegemons

⏳ 前770年–前476年

前770年平王东迁，东周前期为春秋时期（前770–前476，因孔子《春秋》得名）。铁农具与牛耕出现促进深耕细作，工商业发展、金属货币流通。周王室统治力大减、仅辖洛邑一带，诸侯各自为政不再纳贡；一些诸侯国设县郡、委派官员，分封制逐步瓦解。强大诸侯以“尊王攘夷”名义征战争霸：齐桓公（任管仲改革内政）首霸，晋文公、秦穆公、楚庄王等继起；中原“诸夏”与周边民族在长期交往斗争中大规模交融。

After the court fled east in 770 BC, the Zhou king reigned over little more than Luoyi while his vassals ruled themselves — the Spring and Autumn era (named for Confucius's chronicle). Iron tools and ox-ploughing lifted farming, trade and coinage spread, and ambitious states began replacing fiefs with appointed counties. The strong contended for hegemony 'honouring the king, expelling the barbarians': Duke Huan of Qi (with Guan Zhong's reforms) first among the Five Hegemons — while constant contact fused the central 'Xia' peoples with their neighbours.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“霸主”= hegemon（希腊语词恰好精准对应“霸”——中西概念巧合）；“尊王攘夷”可译 honour the king and expel the barbarians（此口号在幕末日本复活，回链 [[meiji-restoration]]）。天子虚位而霸主执政的结构可与 [[shogunate]]、[[charlemagne]] 对读。
