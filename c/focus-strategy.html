专注战略 zhuānzhù zhànlüèfocus (niche) strategy

🔊 focus strategy /ˈfoʊkɪs ˈstrætəʤi/

基本战略之一:集中服务某一细分市场（地域、客群或产品线），在窄范围内做到成本领先或差异化——以深度换广度，避开全市场巨头的正面碾压。

A generic strategy: serve one narrow segment (a geography, buyer group or product line) and win there on cost or differentiation — trading breadth for depth to avoid meeting the giants head-on.

Etymology 词源

focus 拉丁语原义“炉膛、焦点”——聚焦即把火力集中于一点；战略上即把资源压向一个细分。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ niche player /nɪʧ pleɪər/
    A niche player owns a pond too small for whales to bother draining.
  • ▸ segment specialist /ˌsɛgˈmɛnt ˈspɛʃəlɪst/
    The segment specialist knows one customer better than any generalist can.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ serve a defined niche
  • ▸ a beachhead segment
  • ▸ narrow but deep
  • ▸ dominate a segment

In the wild 语境例句

A B2B firm that serves only regional hospitals can out-focus a global generalist on exactly the features those buyers value.
