🔊 GMV and take rate /ˌdʒiːɛmˈviː ənd teɪk reɪt/

平台/市场的两个核心指标:GMV(商品交易总额)是平台上所有交易的总金额,衡量平台规模;take rate(抽成率)是平台从每笔交易中抽取的比例,决定平台自身收入。GMV×take rate≈平台营收。二者的权衡是平台货币化的核心。

Two core metrics for a platform or marketplace: GMV (gross merchandise value) is the total value of all transactions on the platform, measuring its scale; the take rate is the share the platform extracts from each transaction, determining its own revenue. GMV × take rate ≈ platform revenue. Their trade-off is the heart of platform monetisation.

Etymology 词源

GMV = Gross Merchandise Value（商品交易总额）；take rate 即平台'拿走'的比例——电商与市场平台的关键指标。

  • ▸ the platform's cut /ðə ˈplætˌfɔrmz kət/

In the wild 语境例句

A marketplace can boast huge GMV yet earn little if its take rate is 2% — raising the rate lifts revenue but risks driving sellers to cheaper rivals.
