市民阶层（早期资产阶级） shìmín jiēcéngthe burghers (early bourgeoisie)

also: townspeople · the urban middle class

⏳ 11世纪起

手工工匠与商人构成城市基本居民，多由农村农民转化而来；随城市工商业繁荣形成市民阶层，其中分化出富裕的大作坊主、商人和银行家——成为早期的资产阶级。

Craftsmen and merchants, mostly ex-peasants, formed the towns' core population; as commerce swelled, this burgher class stratified, its richest workshop masters, merchants and bankers becoming the embryo of the bourgeoisie.

In the wild 语境例句

Every account of capitalism's origins passes through the same door: the town gate, behind which burghers quietly accumulated the money that would one day outvote the land.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

词族梳理：burgher（历史语境的市民）→ bourgeois/bourgeoisie（法语借词，经马克思著作成为“资产阶级”标准术语，日常英语亦作“中产趣味的”微讽）→ 现代中性说法 the middle class。城市 burg/borough/burgh 词根同源（Edinburgh、Hamburg）。
