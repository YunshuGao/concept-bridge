东汉兴衰与黄巾起义 Dōnghàn xīngshuāithe Eastern Han: revival, eunuchs and the Yellow Turbans

⏳ 25年–220年

公元9年外戚王莽篡汉建新朝，施政加剧动荡被农民起义推翻；25年宗室刘秀称帝都洛阳（光武帝），释奴婢、轻负担、并郡县、惩贪吏、允许少数民族内迁——“光武中兴”。中期后皇帝多年幼，太后外戚专权，皇帝长成又依宦官除外戚——外戚宦官交替专权恶性循环，政治腐朽（“直如弦，死道边；曲如钩，反封侯”）。184年张角创太平道十余年、信徒数十万，发动有组织有准备的黄巾起义；虽被镇压，东汉一蹶不振，改刺史为州牧又酿军阀割据。

The usurper Wang Mang's 'New Dynasty' (AD 9) collapsed in rebellion; Liu Xiu restored the Han at Luoyang in 25 and his frugal, emancipating reign earned the name 'Guangwu revival'. Then the dynasty aged into its disease: child emperors, regent in-law clans, and the eunuchs each grown emperor used to destroy them — a rotation of parasites ('straight as a bowstring, die by the road; bent as a hook, be made a marquis'). In 184 the Daoist healer Zhang Jue launched the organised, yellow-scarfed rising of millions; it was crushed, but the throne never recovered, and the warlord governors who suppressed it kept their armies.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“外戚”= consort clans/imperial in-laws；“宦官”= eunuchs；“专权”= monopolise power。黄巾起义 = the Yellow Turban Rebellion（turban 约定俗成，实为黄巾裹头）。“军阀”= warlord——20世纪中国史高频词在此发源。
