经济体制改革 jīngjì tǐzhì gǎigéthe economic system reforms

⏳ 1978年–1992年

改革先从农村开始：1978年安徽凤阳小岗村农民实行分田包干到户，家庭联产承包责任制逐步在全国推开——激发农民劳动热情，农业大发展；乡镇企业迅速发展，为农民致富和实现现代化开辟新路。1984年起城市改革全面展开、重点是国有企业改革：政企分开、扩大企业自主权、按劳分配为主多种分配方式并存，增强企业活力。1992年中共十四大明确提出建立社会主义市场经济体制——把社会主义基本制度和市场经济结合起来，对现代化建设有巨大推动作用，使中国经济实力明显增强。

Reform began in a farmhouse: eighteen Xiaogang villagers secretly contracted collective land to households in 1978, and their 'household responsibility system' — output above quota kept by the family — swept the country, detonating agricultural growth and the township enterprises that industrialised the countryside sideways. Cities followed from 1984 with state-enterprise reform — autonomy, profit retention, pay by work — and in 1992 the Fourteenth Congress named the destination: a 'socialist market economy', the oxymoron that worked, and the institutional core of the growth miracle.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

家庭联产承包责任制 = the household (contract) responsibility system；乡镇企业 = township and village enterprises (TVEs——发展经济学专名)；社会主义市场经济 = the socialist market economy。与 [[new-economic-policy]]、[[wenjing]]（休养生息）的政策家族树在此结果——本知识库经济改革线的终点站。
