🔊 mentoring /ˈmɛntərɪŋ/

经验丰富的教师(mentor/supervising teacher)通过观课、反馈、示范、协同备课与情感支持,引导新手教师成长。对职前教师而言,指导教师既是实习的评估者也是最重要的学习资源。好的指导平衡'支持'与'挑战',提供具体可行的反馈而非泛泛评价——它本身就是一种教学。

An experienced teacher (mentor / supervising teacher) guiding a novice's growth through observation, feedback, modelling, co-planning and emotional support. For a preservice teacher, the supervising teacher is both the assessor of the placement and its most important learning resource. Good mentoring balances support with challenge and gives specific, actionable feedback rather than vague appraisal — it is itself a form of teaching.

Etymology 词源

mentor 源自《奥德赛》中特勒马科斯的导师门托尔(实为雅典娜化身)——'导师'即以智慧引路者。
