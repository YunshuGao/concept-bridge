可见学习与反馈 kějiàn xuéxí yǔ fǎnkuìfeedback (Hattie's visible learning)

🔊 feedback /ˈfidˌbæk/

哈蒂对数千项研究的综合分析发现,反馈是对学习影响最大的因素之一——但前提是有效反馈。有效反馈回答三问:我要去哪（目标）?我现在在哪（现状）?下一步怎么走（改进策略）?针对任务与过程、而非针对个人的反馈最有效;'做得好'式空泛表扬几乎无效。

Hattie's synthesis of thousands of studies found feedback among the largest influences on learning — but only when it is effective. Effective feedback answers three questions: where am I going (the goal)? where am I now (current state)? where to next (improvement strategy)? Feedback about the task and the process, not the person, works best; vague praise like 'well done' does almost nothing.

Etymology 词源

feedback 源自工程学（输出'回馈'为输入以自我调节）——教学反馈即让学习'回馈'以修正;Hattie《Visible Learning》普及其效应量研究。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ effective feedback /ˈifɛktɪv ˈfidˌbæk/
    Effective feedback tells a student where to go next, not just how they did.
  • ▸ feed-forward /fid-ˈfɔrwərd/
    Feed-forward turns a mark into a next step the student can actually take.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ where to next
  • ▸ task, process, self-regulation
  • ▸ feed-forward
  • ▸ effect size

In the wild 语境例句

'Your loop repeats one time too many — check the boundary condition' is feedback that feeds forward; 'good effort, 6/10' is a grade that teaches nothing.
