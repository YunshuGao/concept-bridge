西晋短暂统一与八王之乱 Xījìn / Bāwáng zhī luànthe brief Jin unification and the War of the Eight Princes

⏳ 266年–316年

263年魏灭蜀；266年司马炎代魏建晋（西晋），280年灭吴统一全国。但晋室出身大地主大贵族，处处维护家族权势、以奢侈为荣（石崇王恺斗富）；武帝大封同姓诸王、遣王据守重镇，惠帝昏庸时八王为争中央政权混战十几年（“八王之乱”），西晋从此衰落，中原人口大量南逃——中国古代第一次大规模人口迁徙高潮。同期匈奴鲜卑羯氐羌等游牧民族不断内迁（山西陕西内迁各族已占人口一半），受西晋暴政压迫而起兵；316年匈奴人灭西晋，此后北方陷入十六国混战（前秦苻坚一度统一北方）。

The Sima clan absorbed Wei (266) and finished Wu (280) — then squandered the reunification within a generation: an aristocracy that competed in extravagance, princes of the blood given armies and provinces, and, under an incapable emperor, the Eight Princes' decade of civil war that bled the dynasty white. Millions fled south in China's first great migration; the Xiongnu, Xianbei and other borderers who had settled the depopulated north — half its people in Shanxi and Shaanxi — rose against Jin misrule, extinguished the dynasty in 316, and the north dissolved into the Sixteen Kingdoms.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析
