季风气候 jìfēng qìhòuthe monsoon

🔊 monsoon /mɒnˈsuːn/

中国地理核心:海陆热力差异导致的季节性盛行风向反转——夏季风带来降水(雨热同期),冬季风干冷。东亚季风塑造了中国农业格局与人口分布。

The seasonal reversal of prevailing winds driven by land-sea temperature contrast: the summer monsoon brings rain when it is hot (rain and heat together), the winter monsoon is dry and cold. The East Asian monsoon shaped China's农业 and where its people live.

Etymology 词源

monsoon 源自阿拉伯 mawsim(季节)——经葡萄牙语 monção 入英;一个阿拉伯航海词描述了印度洋与东亚的定期风,词源即贸易史。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ prevailing wind /prɪˈveɪlɪŋ wɪnd/
    The prevailing wind decides which coast gets the rain and which gets the desert.
  • ▸ rain-heat concurrence /reɪn hiːt kənˈkʌrəns/
    Rain-heat concurrence lets one growing season do the work of two.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ the summer/winter monsoon
  • ▸ monsoon Asia
  • ▸ rain belt
  • ▸ the retreat of the monsoon

In the wild 语境例句

The same monsoon that floods the south feeds a fifth of humanity — geography as demography's silent author.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

monsoon 词根是“季节”而非“雨”,故 monsoon 可指风也可指雨季——英语中 the monsoon 常直接指雨季,须按语境判断。对接教材人口分布(胡焕庸线)与季风区关系。
