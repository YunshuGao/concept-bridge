S 曲线 S qūxiànthe technology S-curve

🔊 S-curve /ɛs-kərv/

技术性能随投入呈 S 形演进:早期缓慢、中期爆发、后期趋于饱和。当一条 S 曲线接近平台期，颠覆常来自跳上一条新的 S 曲线。管理者的难题是在旧曲线仍赚钱时押注新曲线。

A technology's performance follows an S-shape as investment rises: slow start, explosive middle, saturating end. As one S-curve plateaus, disruption usually comes from jumping to a new curve. The manager's hard problem is betting on the new curve while the old one still pays.

Etymology 词源

曲线形似字母 S 而得名——生物学（种群增长）、社会学（创新扩散）与技术管理共用同一形状。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ technology life cycle /tɛkˈnɑləʤi laɪf ˈsaɪkəl/
    The technology life cycle rewards those who leave the plateau before it collapses.
  • ▸ curve-jumping /kərv-ˈʤəmpɪŋ/
    Curve-jumping is cannibalising your best product before a rival does it for you.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ ride the S-curve
  • ▸ approaching the plateau
  • ▸ jump to a new curve
  • ▸ the inflection point

In the wild 语境例句

Combustion engines are high on their S-curve — improving slowly at great cost — while electric drivetrains are still on the steep middle of theirs.
