中华民国的创建 Zhōnghuá Mínguóthe founding of the Republic of China

🔊 provisional constitution /prəˈvɪʒənəl ˌkɑnstəˈtuʃən/

⏳ 1912年

1912年1月1日孙中山在南京就任临时大总统，中华民国临时政府成立（民国纪年）。袁世凯借北洋军力与列强支持逼宫；1912年2月12日宣统帝下诏退位——清朝260多年统治结束；孙中山依约辞职，袁世凯在北京就任临时大总统。1912年3月颁布《中华民国临时约法》：主权属于全体国民、国民一律平等、有人身居住言论出版集会结社宗教信仰及请愿考试选举参政等自由权利；参议院行使立法权、司法独立、责任内阁制——中国历史上第一部具有资产阶级共和国宪法性质的重要文件。辛亥革命果实被袁世凯窃取。

On New Year's Day 1912 Sun Yat-sen was sworn in at Nanjing as provisional president of Asia's first republic; six weeks later the boy emperor Puyi abdicated, closing 268 years of Qing and the imperial system entire. The price was the presidency itself: Yuan Shikai, commanding the only real army and the powers' confidence, took office in Beijing. The revolutionaries' insurance policy was the Provisional Constitution of March 1912 — popular sovereignty, equal citizenship, the full bill of liberties, separated powers, cabinet government: China's first republican constitutional document, drafted, pointedly, to cage the man about to inherit it.
