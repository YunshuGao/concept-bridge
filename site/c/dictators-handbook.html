《独裁者手册》（胜利联盟理论） dúcáizhě shǒucèThe Dictator's Handbook: selectorate theory

🔊 selectorate /sɪˈlɛktərət/

⏳ 2011年

布鲁诺·德·梅斯奎塔与史密斯把政治简化为一条规则：统治者只需取悦“胜利联盟”（winning coalition——决定其去留的关键支持者）。联盟小（独裁）则用私人收买（腐败、特权）最有效；联盟大（民主）则须供给公共品。由此解释外援养独裁、资源诅咒、民主国家打仗更谨慎等——“坏行为几乎总是好政治”。冷酷的理性选择框架，与制度学派互补，是政治评论的常用透镜。

Politics reduced to one law: rulers serve the winning coalition — the minimal set of backers who keep them in power. Small coalition (autocracy) → pay them privately (graft, privilege); large coalition (democracy) → pay in public goods. The framework churns out uncomfortable corollaries — aid props up dictators, resources curse, democracies fight more carefully — under the subtitle's thesis that bad behaviour is almost always good politics. The cynic's field guide that political columnists reach for weekly.
