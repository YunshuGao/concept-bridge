竞争对抗 jìngzhēng duìkàngcompetitive rivalry

🔊 competitive rivalry /kəmˈpɛtɪtɪv ˈraɪvəlri/

五力的核心:现有企业间的对抗强度。高固定成本、产品同质、增长停滞、退出壁垒高时，价格战与产能过剩会吞噬全行业利润。

The centre of the five forces: how fiercely incumbents fight. High fixed costs, undifferentiated products, stalled growth and high exit barriers breed price wars and overcapacity that devour industry profit.

Etymology 词源

rival 源自拉丁 rivalis（共用同一条河 rivus 的人）——竞争者本义“争水者”；对岸取水，冲突自来。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ price war /praɪs wɔr/
    A price war is rivalry that has run out of other ideas.
  • ▸ overcapacity /ˌoʊvərkəˈpæsɪti/
    Overcapacity turns a good industry into a charity for customers.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ intense rivalry
  • ▸ race to the bottom
  • ▸ exit barriers
  • ▸ industry consolidation

In the wild 语境例句

Airlines combine high fixed costs, perishable inventory and no differentiation — a structural recipe for rivalry that torches returns.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

rival 词源“争同一条河”——竞争的隐喻从来是稀缺资源之争；与教材 involution（内卷）同构:过度对抗，无人得利。
