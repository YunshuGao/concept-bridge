精英过剩 jīngyīng guòshèngelite overproduction

🔊 overproduction /ˌoʊvərprəˈdʌkʃən/

特纳(Peter Turchin)历史动力学的核心概念:当社会培养的精英候选人超过精英职位数量,落选精英成为动荡的引擎——从罗马内战到当代学历通胀。蒋教授文明史讲座反复援引以解释周期性崩溃。

Peter Turchin's cliodynamics term: when a society produces more elite aspirants than there are elite positions, the surplus becomes the engine of instability — from Rome's civil wars to today's credential inflation. A recurring lens in the Civilization lectures.

Etymology 词源

elite 源自拉丁 eligere(选出,e-出+legere 拣)——精英即“被选出者”;“精英过剩”即被选出者供过于求,词义自带反讽。
