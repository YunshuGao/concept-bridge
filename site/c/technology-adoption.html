技术采用生命周期 jìshù cǎiyòng shēngmìng zhōuqīthe technology adoption lifecycle

🔊 technology adoption lifecycle /tɛkˈnɑləʤi əˈdɑpʃən ˈlaɪfsaɪkəl/

新技术在人群中扩散的钟形曲线:创新者→早期采用者→早期大众→晚期大众→落后者。每一群人的心理与购买动机不同,营销须逐段调整。它是理解创新扩散、时机与规模化的基础模型。

The bell-curve by which a new technology spreads through a population: innovators → early adopters → early majority → late majority → laggards. Each group differs in psychology and buying motive, so marketing must adapt stage by stage. A foundational model for understanding diffusion, timing and scaling.

Etymology 词源

由 Everett Rogers《创新的扩散》(1962) 奠基——把创新在社会中的传播画成一条钟形曲线。
