雅典民主政治 Yǎdiǎn mínzhǔ zhèngzhìAthenian democracy

⏳ 前594年–前4世纪

雅典经数次改革建立的民主政体，伯里克利主政时达到高峰：公职几乎全由抽签产生，公民大会为最高权力机构，设津贴保障贫穷公民参政；但外邦人、奴隶和妇女——占人口绝大多数——毫无政治权利。

The direct democracy Athens built through successive reforms, peaking under Pericles: offices filled by lot, a sovereign popular Assembly, and stipends so the poor could serve — while metics, slaves and women, the great majority, had no political rights at all.

In the wild 语境例句

Critics of referendums reach instinctively for Athens: direct democracy, they warn, is one demagogue away from mob rule.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

关键区分：雅典是直接民主 direct democracy，现代多为代议民主 representative democracy。“抽签”= sortition/selection by lot（近年治理讨论中复兴的热词）。教材“暴民政治”= mob rule/ochlocracy；“蛊惑民众的野心家”= demagogue（Economist 高频词）。
