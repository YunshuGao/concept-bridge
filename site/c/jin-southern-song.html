金与南宋的对峙（岳飞抗金） Jīn yǔ Nánsòngthe Jin conquest and the Southern Song

🔊 rump state /rəmp steɪt/

⏳ 1115年–1234年

女真完颜阿骨打统一各部，1115年建金（模仿中原制度、颁女真文字）。宋金联盟夹攻辽，1125年金灭辽；金见北宋腐败无能，1127年攻破开封灭北宋（徽钦二帝被掳）。赵构建南宋都临安。岳飞“岳家军”纪律严明（“冻死不拆屋，饿死不掳掠”），郾城大败金军主力，有望收复中原之际，高宗秦桧恐抗金力量壮大危及统治，令岳飞班师、以“莫须有”罪名杀害之；宋金和议：南宋称臣纳岁币，以淮水—大散关为界对峙，南宋偏安江南（“暖风熏得游人醉，直把杭州作汴州”）。金朝统治者提倡儒学、译儒经、以儒典命科举题。

The Jurchen chief Aguda founded the Jin in 1115, destroyed Liao in a decade, then turned on the ally that had revealed its own weakness: Kaifeng fell in 1127, two emperors were carried into captivity, and a Song prince rebuilt the dynasty at Hangzhou. Yue Fei's incorruptible army ('freeze before seizing a house, starve before looting') broke the Jin cavalry at Yancheng — whereupon his own court, fearing victorious generals more than the enemy, recalled and judicially murdered him on a charge history remembers by its confession: 'perhaps there was something' (moxuyou). The peace made the Song a tribute-paying 'subject' south of the Huai, content — as the era's bitterest poem has it — to mistake Hangzhou for the lost capital; the Jin, meanwhile, translated the Confucian classics and examined its officials on them.

rump（残躯）+ state——“偏安政权”的英语惯用形象；loyalty 源自拉丁 legalis（合法）→ 忠义。

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“莫须有”= 'perhaps there was' — trumped-up/fabricated charges 的中文原型语。“偏安”= a rump regime content with partial rule（rump state 是准确对译）。岳飞“民族英雄”称号的当代辨析（宋金皆为今中华民族内部）是教材问题思考的深层背景——英语表述宜说 a hero of loyalty 而非简单 national hero。靖康之变 = the Jingkang Incident。
