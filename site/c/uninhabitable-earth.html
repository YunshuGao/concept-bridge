《不宜居的地球》 bùyíjū de dìqiúThe Uninhabitable Earth: climate cascades

🔊 cascade /kæˈskeɪd/

华莱士-威尔斯的气候警世书:“比你想的更糟,糟得多”——级联效应（cascades）、热死亡、气候难民、碳预算;新词汇:climate doomism（气候末日论）与其批评者。

Wallace-Wells's opening line — it is worse, much worse, than you think — introduced cascade thinking to lay readers: compounding feedbacks, heat death, climate refugees, carbon budgets; and the backlash coined climate doomism.

Etymology 词源

cascade 源自意大利 cascare（跌落）——瀑布转喻连锁反应;气候语境指反馈回路互相触发的雪崩式恶化。
