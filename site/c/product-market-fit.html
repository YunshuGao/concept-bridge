产品市场契合 chǎnpǐn shìchǎng qìhéproduct-market fit (PMF)

🔊 product-market fit /ˈprɑdəkt-ˈmɑrkɪt fɪt/

产品恰好满足了一个强需求市场，以致增长开始自我拉动——用户留存、口碑、拉动式需求。达到 PMF 前，唯一要务是寻找它；达到后，重心转向增长。创业的分水岭。

The point where a product so well satisfies a strong-demand market that growth starts pulling itself — retention holds, word of mouth spreads, demand pulls. Before PMF the only job is to find it; after PMF the job becomes growth. The watershed of a startup.

Etymology 词源

由投资人 Marc Andreessen 普及——“市场拉着产品从你手里跑”的那一刻，是他给创业者的唯一北极星。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the pull /ðə pʊl/
    You feel the pull of product-market fit; you don't have to argue for it.
  • ▸ retention curve /riˈtɛnʃən kərv/
    A retention curve that flattens above zero is PMF's clearest fingerprint.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ reach product-market fit
  • ▸ the market pulls the product
  • ▸ retention as the proof
  • ▸ before and after PMF

In the wild 语境例句

Founders chasing growth before product-market fit pour fuel on a fire that isn't lit — the retention curve tells the truth the pitch deck won't.
