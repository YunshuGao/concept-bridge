义和团与《辛丑条约》 Yìhétuán / Xīnchǒu tiáoyuēthe Boxers and the Boxer Protocol

⏳ 1899年–1901年

19世纪末反洋教斗争中义和团兴起山东直隶，提出“扶清灭洋”口号（具有爱国性质，亦有盲目排外落后性；“扶清”使其放松对清政府警惕）。1900年八国联军以镇压义和团为名侵华：廊坊阻击、天津保卫战（聂士成殉国）；联军攻陷北京，慈禧携光绪西逃并令剿杀义和团。1901年《辛丑条约》：赔款白银4.5亿两、分39年还清本息共9.8亿两；划东交民巷为使馆界不准中国人居住；拆炮台、允许各国驻兵北京至山海关铁路沿线；清政府保证严禁人民参加各种形式的反帝活动——清政府沦为帝国主义列强统治中国的工具，中国完全陷入半殖民地半封建社会的深渊。

Anti-missionary fury bred the Boxers — 'support the Qing, destroy the foreign' — whose siege of the legations brought eight armies to Beijing in 1900; the court fled west, then turned on the movement it had blessed. The Boxer Protocol of 1901 was the century of humiliation's price list: 450 million taels (one per Chinese subject, 980 million with interest over 39 years), a legation quarter barred to Chinese, foreign garrisons down the railway to the sea, and — the clause the textbook lands on — a standing Qing guarantee to suppress its own people's anti-foreign movements: the dynasty now, in the set phrase, 'the court of the foreigners', and China at the bottom of the semi-colonial abyss.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

义和团英语旧称 the Boxers（拳民直译）——the Boxer Rebellion/Uprising 视角差再现。“扶清灭洋”= support the Qing, exterminate the foreigners。《辛丑条约》= the Boxer Protocol（1901）。“洋人的朝廷”= the court of the foreigners。人均一两白银的赔款设计是羞辱的算术——细节即修辞。
