⏳ 2023年

《我看见的世界》：李飞飞自传——成都出生、16岁移民美国、洗衣店打工到斯坦福AI实验室主任。核心科学叙事：ImageNet（1500万标注图像数据集）押注“数据比算法更稀缺”，2012年AlexNet在其竞赛上突破，点燃深度学习革命；提出“AI的北极星”（North Star）研究观与以人为本AI（human-centered AI）。移民叙事+科学史+AI伦理三线——兼为励志读物与AI入门。

The Worlds I See braids memoir and science: Chengdu to New Jersey at sixteen, laundromat shifts to Stanford's AI lab — and the bet that made the deep-learning era: ImageNet, fifteen million labelled images, wagering that data, not algorithms, was the bottleneck, vindicated when AlexNet's 2012 win lit the fuse. Her framings — research 'North Stars', human-centered AI — now anchor the governance debate. As a bilingual immigrant-scientist narrative it reads like a companion volume to your own project.
