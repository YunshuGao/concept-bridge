🔊 theory of constraints /ˈθɪri əv kənˈstreɪnts/

高德拉特《目标》:任何系统的产出都受制于其最弱环节（瓶颈/约束）。改进非瓶颈环节毫无意义,唯有识别并突破瓶颈才能提升整体产出。'一条链的强度取决于最弱的一环'——聚焦约束,是运营优化的杠杆点。

Goldratt's The Goal: a system's output is limited by its weakest link (the bottleneck or constraint). Improving non-bottlenecks is pointless; only identifying and breaking the constraint raises total throughput. 'A chain is only as strong as its weakest link' — focusing on the constraint is the leverage point of operations.

Etymology 词源

constraint 源自拉丁 constringere（束紧）——约束即'捆住系统产出的那一环';Goldratt 以小说《目标》普及此理论。
