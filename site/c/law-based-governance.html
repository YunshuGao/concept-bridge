全面依法治国 quánmiàn yīfǎ zhìguócomprehensive law-based governance

⏳ 2014年至今

法治是治国理政的基本方式。全面依法治国总目标：建设中国特色社会主义法治体系、建设社会主义法治国家——坚持党的领导、人民当家作主、依法治国有机统一。建设法治国家、法治政府（职能科学、权责法定、执法严明、公开公正……）、法治社会一体推进；基本要求：科学立法、严格执法、公正司法、全民守法。宪法是国家的根本法，具有最高法律效力；依法治国首先要坚持依宪治国。

The governing method codified since 2014: 'comprehensive law-based governance', whose stated unity — Party leadership, popular sovereignty and rule by law 'organically combined' — is precisely what outside analysis probes. The programme runs on four requirements (scientific legislation, strict enforcement, impartial justice, universal observance) across state, government and society, with the Constitution supreme ('governance by law begins with governance by the Constitution'). The perennial translation battle — rule OF law v. rule BY law — is not pedantry but the entire analytical question, and this chapter supplies the official side's full vocabulary.

In the wild 语境例句

Every foreign-investment law explainer and every dissident-trial report negotiates the same phrase pair — 依法治国 rendered 'rule of law' by Xinhua and 'rule by law' by critics; the choice of preposition is the editorial.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

官方译 law-based governance（回避 of/by 之争的第三条路）；合宪性审查 = constitutional review（无违宪审查法院——与 [[us-constitution]] 的 judicial review 对照）。链 [[chinese-legal-tradition]]（礼法传统）、[[legalism]]（rule BY law 的古典原型）、[[magna-carta]]（rule OF law 的西方谱系）——本知识库法治概念群的当代收口。
