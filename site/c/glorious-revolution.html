光荣革命 Guāngróng gémìngthe Glorious Revolution

⏳ 1688年

詹姆士二世在英国恢复天主教与专制、倒行逆施；1688年议会废黜之，迎其女玛丽与女婿威廉入主英国——不流血的政变，史称“光荣革命”。

When James II set about restoring Catholicism and absolutism, Parliament in 1688 simply replaced him — inviting his daughter Mary and her husband William to the throne in a bloodless coup remembered as 'glorious' precisely because no one died for it.

In the wild 语境例句

Boardroom transitions get the same adjective: a 'glorious revolution' is any regime change accomplished by lawyers and letters rather than blood.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“政变”一般译 coup (d'état)，但英语史学从不称1688为 coup——命名本身是辉格史观的胜利。经济学中1688年是制度经济学的明星案例（North & Weingast：credible commitment 可信承诺——议会主权使英国国债利率骤降），Economist 讨论制度与增长常引。
