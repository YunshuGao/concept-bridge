从食物采集到现代粮食安全 shíwù cǎijí dào liángshi ānquánfrom foraging to food security

🔊 food security /fud sɪˈkjʊrəti/

⏳ 1万年前至今

远古人类采集渔猎；约1万年前农业出现（原始农耕畜牧革命——人类经济和社会生活的第一次革命：从食物采集者变成食物生产者），东亚稻粟、西亚麦豆、美洲玉米甘薯各成体系并随交流传播（新航路后食物物种大交流）。工业革命后食物生产现代化：机械化、化肥良种，冷链储运、罐头加工。现代问题：粮食安全（人口激增耕地减少；中国以占世界7%的耕地养活22%的人口，杂交水稻贡献巨大；《中华人民共和国粮食安全保障法》）与食品安全（化肥农药残留、添加剂滥用——各国立法监管）。消除饥饿仍是人类共同挑战。

Agriculture — humanity's first revolution, turning foragers into producers about 10,000 years ago — arose independently around rice and millet, wheat and barley, maize and potato, then cross-pollinated the planet after 1492. Industry mechanised it; chemistry fertilised it; refrigeration globalised it. The modern residue is the paired anxiety every government budget reflects: food security (China feeding 22% of the world on 7% of its arable land, hybrid rice doing heavy lifting) and food safety (residues, additives, the regulatory state's kitchen inspection) — with hunger, the textbook closes, still humanity's unfinished business.
