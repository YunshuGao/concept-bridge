🔊 photosynthesis /ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɪnθəsɪs/

初中生物核心:绿色植物利用叶绿素,把二氧化碳和水在光下转化为葡萄糖并释放氧气——地球几乎所有食物链与大气氧的源头。呼吸作用(respiration)是其逆过程。

Green plants use chlorophyll to turn carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen using light — the base of nearly every food chain and the source of atmospheric oxygen. Respiration is the reverse.

Etymology 词源

photo-(希腊 phōs 光)+ synthesis(syn 合+thesis 放置)——“以光合成”;字面即中文“光合”的希腊语版,两语构词完全对应。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ chlorophyll /ˈklɔːrəfɪl/
    Chlorophyll is the pigment that spends sunlight and pays in sugar.
  • ▸ carbon fixation /ˈkɑːrbən fɪkˈseɪʃən/
    Carbon fixation locks a gas into a leaf and, eventually, into you.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

中英构词完全同源(光合=photo+synthesis),是理科术语中希英-汉互译最透明的一类——记住词根 photo/syn 便解锁 photograph、synthesis、synthetic 全串。
