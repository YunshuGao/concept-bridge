青铜时代大崩溃 qīngtóng shídài bēngkuìthe Late Bronze Age collapse

🔊 collapse /kəˈlæps/

约公元前1200年,地中海东部互联文明群(迈锡尼、赫梯、埃及)在数十年内连锁崩溃——“海上民族”入侵、地震、气候变化、贸易断裂的系统性崩溃(systems collapse)。全球化脆弱性的最古老案例。

Around 1200 BC the interconnected eastern-Mediterranean civilisations (Mycenae, the Hittites, Egypt) fell in a chain within decades — Sea Peoples, earthquakes, climate change and severed trade: a systems collapse. History's oldest case study in globalisation's fragility.

Etymology 词源

collapse 源自拉丁 com-(一起)+ labi(滑落)——“崩溃”即“一起滑倒”;系统性崩溃的词源恰是连锁跌落的画面。

Synonyms 近义词
