三角贸易 sānjiǎo màoyìthe triangular trade

also: the Atlantic slave trade

⏳ 16世纪–19世纪

以贩卖黑奴为中心的大西洋贸易：欧洲商船载火器、机械制品与酒到非洲倾销并掳获黑奴，运往西印度群岛和美洲卖给种植园主，再购蔗糖烟草返欧——航线呈三角形。印第安人因压榨屠戮与传染病几乎灭绝后，西、英等国大规模贩奴补充美洲劳动力；无数黑人死于跋涉、瘟疫或被抛入大海。种植园与黑奴贸易为英国带来巨额利润。

The Atlantic circuit with slavery at its heart: European ships carried guns, wares and drink to Africa, exchanged them for captives, sold the survivors of the Middle Passage to New World planters, and sailed home loaded with sugar and tobacco. Launched after enslavement and epidemic had all but annihilated the Amerindians, the trade drowned, starved and worked millions of Africans to death — and made fortunes that helped bankroll Britain's rise.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析
