渠道战略 qúdào zhànlüèchannel strategy (route to market)

🔊 channel strategy /ˈʧænəl ˈstrætəʤi/

企业通过何种路径把产品送到客户手中:直销、经销、零售、电商、平台、加盟。渠道决定成本、控制力、客户关系与利润分配。直销掌控体验与数据但重资产,分销扩面快但让渡利润与客户关系——DTC 与平台正在重塑这场取舍。

The paths by which a firm gets its product to customers: direct sales, distributors, retail, e-commerce, platforms, franchising. Channel choice determines cost, control, customer relationship and margin split. Direct control owns experience and data but is asset-heavy; distribution scales reach but cedes margin and the customer relationship — a trade-off DTC and platforms are reshaping.

Etymology 词源

channel 源自拉丁 canalis（水道）——渠道即'产品流向客户的水道';route to market 是其同义表达。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ route to market /rut tɪ ˈmɑrkɪt/
    The route to market decides who owns the customer relationship at the end of it.
  • ▸ direct-to-consumer /dɪˈrɛkt-tɪ-kənˈsumər/
    Direct-to-consumer trades the retailer's reach for the brand's own data and margin.
