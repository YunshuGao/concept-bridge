三张报表的勾稽 sānzhāng bàobiǎo de gōujīthe three-statement linkage

🔊 three-statement linkage /θri-ˈsteɪtmənt ˈlɪŋkɪʤ/

投行分析师的第一课:利润表、资产负债表、现金流量表并非孤立,而是环环相扣。净利润流入留存收益(连接利润表与资产负债表);净利润加回非现金项、调整营运资本得出经营现金流;期末现金回到资产负债表。看懂这套勾稽,才能判断一家公司的数字是否自洽、是否被操纵。

An analyst's first lesson: the income statement, balance sheet and cash flow statement are not separate but interlock. Net income flows into retained earnings (linking P&L to balance sheet); net income plus non-cash add-backs and working-capital changes gives operating cash flow; ending cash returns to the balance sheet. Master this articulation and you can judge whether a company's numbers are internally consistent — or manipulated.

Etymology 词源

勾稽（gōujī）为中文会计术语,指数字间的相互稽核关系;英文 articulation 源自拉丁 articulare（分节连接）——报表'关节相连'。

In the wild 语境例句

An analyst tests a model by breaking one link — raising depreciation — and checking it flows correctly through all three statements; if cash doesn't balance, the model is wrong.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

面试高频考题:'折旧增加100(税率0)会如何影响三张报表?'——利润表净利润−100,现金流量表加回折旧使经营现金流+0,资产负债表现金不变但累计折旧+100、留存收益−100。答不出即出局。
