财富管理 cáifù guǎnlǐwealth management

🔊 wealth management /wɛlθ ˈmænɪʤmənt/

为个人与家庭(尤其高净值人群)提供的综合性、目标导向的财务服务:不止投资,还涵盖退休、税务、遗产、保险与人生目标规划。核心是理解客户的目标与风险承受度、构建合适组合、并把建议讲清楚。它是CFA三级最贴近'为真实的人做决策'的领域,重在信任与沟通。

Comprehensive, goals-based financial service for individuals and families (especially the high-net-worth): not just investing, but retirement, tax, estate, insurance and life-goal planning. Its core is understanding a client's goals and risk tolerance, building a suitable portfolio, and explaining the recommendation clearly. It is the CFA Level III arena closest to 'making decisions for a real person' — built on trust and communication.
