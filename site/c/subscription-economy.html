订阅经济 dìngyuè jīngjìthe subscription economy

🔊 subscription economy /səbˈskrɪpʃən ɪˈkɑnəmi/

商业模式从'一次性卖产品'转向'持续卖服务与关系':软件、媒体、消费品乃至汽车都在订阅化。它把不稳定的一次性交易变成可预测的经常性收入,但也把竞争焦点从'成交'永久转向'留存'——每月都要重新赢得客户。

The shift from selling a product once to selling an ongoing service and relationship: software, media, consumer goods, even cars are becoming subscriptions. It converts lumpy one-off sales into predictable recurring revenue — but permanently shifts the competitive focus from closing to retaining, re-winning the customer every month.

Etymology 词源

subscribe 源自拉丁 sub（在下）+ scribere（写）——'在下方签名'订约;订阅经济即'以持续订约取代一次买断'。
