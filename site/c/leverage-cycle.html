杠杆周期 gànggǎn zhōuqīthe leverage cycle

🔊 leverage cycle /ˈlɛvərɪdʒ ˈsaɪkəl/

经济繁荣时,乐观情绪与宽松信贷推动债务与资产价格螺旋上升(加杠杆);一旦逆转,抛售还债引发资产价格暴跌、进一步逼迫去杠杆,酿成危机。明斯基:'稳定本身孕育不稳定'——繁荣的种子里长出崩溃。

In a boom, optimism and easy credit spiral debt and asset prices upward (leveraging up); once it reverses, selling to repay debt crashes asset prices, forcing further deleveraging into crisis. Minsky: 'stability itself is destabilising' — the crash grows from the seeds of the boom.

Etymology 词源

leverage（杠杆）+ cycle——债务的扩张与收缩周期;Minsky 与 Geanakoplos 的理论核心。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the Minsky moment /ðə ˈmɪnski ˈmoʊmənt/
    The Minsky moment is when the debt built in good times suddenly demands repayment.
  • ▸ the debt supercycle /ðə dɛt ˈsuːpərsaɪkəl/
    The debt supercycle is the leverage cycle stretched across decades.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ leveraging up and deleveraging
  • ▸ the Minsky moment
  • ▸ the credit cycle
  • ▸ a debt-fuelled boom

In the wild 语境例句

The 2008 crisis was a leverage cycle in the housing market: cheap credit inflated prices until a small fall forced deleveraging that crashed everything.
