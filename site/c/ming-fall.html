明朝的灭亡与清兵入关 Míngcháo mièwángthe fall of the Ming

🔊 dynastic collapse /daɪˈnæstɪk kəˈlæps/

⏳ 1616年–1644年

明中后期政治日益腐败：神宗20年不上朝、熹宗委政宦官魏忠贤（“九千岁”），结党营私、兼并土地、农民流离失所。末年财政危机加派“三饷”，陕北连年大旱官府催征如故，农民纷起。李自成提出“均田免赋”（“迎闯王，不纳粮”）、军纪严明，队伍百万；1644年建大顺政权，4月攻入北京，崇祯自缢，276年明朝亡于农民起义。东北女真努尔哈赤1616年建后金（八旗制度兵民合一），皇太极改族名满洲、1636年改国号清；山海关守将吴三桂降清引清军入关，联合夹击李自成，李败退西行终至失败。

Late Ming government rotted from the head — one emperor skipped court for twenty years, another whittled furniture while the eunuch 'Nine Thousand Years' ran the state — while surtaxes multiplied and drought scoured the northwest. Li Zicheng's rebels marched on the promise 'equal land, no grain tax' ('open the gates for the Dashing Prince'); Beijing fell in April 1644 and the last emperor hanged himself on Coal Hill. Then the northeast collected: Nurhaci's Jurchen banner-state (Later Jin, 1616; renamed Manchu and Qing under his son) was waiting at the Wall when the Ming border general Wu Sangui opened Shanhai Pass — and the rebels who had toppled a dynasty lost the empire to the newcomers within the year.

Wu Sangui is Chinese for Quisling with better cavalry — 'opening Shanhai Pass' remains the standing metaphor for the insider who lets the outsider in to spite a domestic enemy.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

八旗 = the (Eight) Banners / banner system；旗人 bannermen——“八旗子弟”今喻坐吃特权的纨绔（教材知识拓展点明）。李自成“均田免赋”与 [[chen-sheng-uprising]]、[[huang-chao]]（在 an-lushan 内）同谱系——农民起义 the peasant rebellion 作为中国史动力机制，是英语汉学的经典议题（裴宜理等）。
