西汉建立与文景之治 Wén-Jǐng zhī zhìthe Han founding and the Wenjing era

⏳ 前202年–前141年

前202年刘邦建汉都长安（汉高祖）。秦末战乱使社会残破（“自天子不能具醇驷，将相或乘牛车”），高祖吸取秦亡教训行休养生息：兵皆罢归家、释奴婢为平民、轻徭薄赋（什五税一）。文帝景帝继之：以农为本、田赋降至三十税一、废肉刑“以德化民”、勤俭治国（文帝在位二十余年宫室车骑无所增）——政治清明、经济发展、库藏充盈，史称“文景之治”。

Liu Bang founded the Han in 202 BC over a wasteland — the emperor could not match four horses of one colour, his ministers rode ox-carts. Learning from Qin's death-by-exertion, the early Han governed by 'rest and recuperation': soldiers sent home to farm, slaves freed, taxes cut to a fifteenth and then a thirtieth of the crop, mutilating punishments abolished, the palace kept frugal. Two generations of deliberate under-government filled the granaries — the Wenjing golden age, China's founding case for small-state economics.

In the wild 语境例句

Reform-era economists reached straight for 休养生息 to explain the 1980s: after political exhaustion, govern less, tax less, and let the peasants make the country rich again.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“休养生息”= rest and recuperation（政策名，直译能懂）；“轻徭薄赋”= light corvée and low taxation。文景之治是“无为而治”的实践版——链 [[laozi]] 与 [[adam-smith]]：黄老政治与自由放任的比较是思想史常题。“之治”体例（golden age of X）后有贞观、开元。
