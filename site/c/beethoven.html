贝多芬 BèiduōfēnLudwig van Beethoven

⏳ 1770年–1827年

德国天才作曲家：音乐充满想象力与激情。《英雄交响曲》（1804）反映重大社会题材，气势磅礴，表达对自由平等博爱的渴望——原为拿破仑而作，闻其称帝愤而抹去其名。28岁起患耳疾至全聋，咬木棒抵钢琴辨音坚持作曲。

The composer who made music heroic: the Eroica (1804), blazing with liberty, equality and fraternity, bore Napoleon's name until news of the coronation made Beethoven scratch it out. Deaf from 28 — composing with a stick clenched in his teeth against the piano — he became art's byword for genius against fate.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析
