十一届三中全会（伟大的历史转折） shíyījiè sānzhōng quánhuìthe Third Plenum of 1978

🔊 pivot /ˈpɪvət/

⏳ 1978年

“文革”后“两个凡是”方针引起不满，1978年思想理论界展开真理标准问题大讨论——“实践是检验真理的唯一标准”，解放思想。1978年12月中共十一届三中全会（北京）：冲破长期“左”的错误的严重束缚，确定解放思想、开动脑筋、实事求是、团结一致向前看的指导方针；果断结束“以阶级斗争为纲”，作出把党和国家工作中心转移到经济建设上来、实行改革开放的历史性决策；实际上形成以邓小平为核心的党的第二代中央领导集体。是新中国成立以来党的历史上具有深远意义的伟大转折，开启了改革开放和社会主义现代化建设新时期。（此后平反冤假错案、1981年《关于建国以来党的若干历史问题的决议》。）

Out of the truth-criterion debate — 'practice is the sole criterion of truth', the slogan that broke the 'two whatevers' — came the meeting that redirected the country: the Third Plenum of the Eleventh Central Committee, December 1978. Class struggle was retired as the key link; economic construction became the party's central task; 'reform and opening up' was decided; and Deng Xiaoping emerged as core of the second leadership generation. The textbook's superlative — 'a great turning point of far-reaching significance' — is for once undersold: this is the pivot on which China's, and arguably the world economy's, past half-century turns.

In the wild 语境例句

Every December anniversary and every 'deepen reform' communiqué positions itself as heir to 1978 — 'since reform and opening up' rivals 'since New China' as official history's other epoch-marker.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

改革开放 = reform and opening up（官方定译，Economist 直接使用）。“实事求是”= seek truth from facts（毛语被邓征用——语义史）。三中全会 = the Third Plenum（因1978而使 'Third Plenum' 在英语报道中永久带有“重大改革窗口”的联想——2013年三中全会报道即消费此联想）。
