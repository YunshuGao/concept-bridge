专业实习 zhuānyè shíxíprofessional experience (the practicum)

🔊 professional experience /prəˈfɛʃənəl ɪkˈspɪriəns/

职前教师在真实学校、由指导教师（supervising teacher）督导下进行的驻校教学实践,是教师培养的核心与GTPA证据的来源。它把大学的理论置于课堂的真实复杂性中检验,新教师在此从'观察—协同—独立'逐步接过教学责任。

The in-school teaching practice a preservice teacher undertakes in a real school under a supervising teacher, the core of teacher preparation and the source of GTPA evidence. It tests university theory against the real complexity of classrooms, as the new teacher progressively takes over responsibility from observing to co-teaching to teaching solo.

Etymology 词源

practicum 源自拉丁 practicus（实践的,希腊 praktikos）——'实践课',区别于理论课。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the practicum /ðə ˈpræktɪkəm/

  • ▸ the teaching placement /ðə ˈtiʧɪŋ ˈpleɪsmənt/
    A teaching placement hands you a real class and a mentor watching from the back.
