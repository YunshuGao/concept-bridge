股利政策 gǔlì zhèngcèdividend policy

🔊 dividend policy /ˈdɪvɪˌdɛnd ˈpɑləsi/

公司决定把多少利润以现金分红派发给股东、留存多少再投资。稳定的分红传递信心与纪律，但也意味着管理层认为再投资机会有限。分红与回购、再投资之间的取舍，是资本配置的核心决策。

How a company decides how much profit to pay out to shareholders as cash dividends versus retain for reinvestment. A steady dividend signals confidence and discipline, but also that management sees limited reinvestment opportunities. The trade-off among dividends, buybacks and reinvestment is a core capital-allocation decision.

Etymology 词源

dividend 源自拉丁 dividendum（应分之物，dividere 分）——股利即“应分给股东的那份”。

  • ▸ the payout ratio /ðə peɪaʊt ˈreɪʃiˌoʊ/

  • ▸ dividend signalling /ˈdɪvɪˌdɛnd ˈsɪgnəlɪŋ/
    Dividend signalling means a cut screams distress even when the cash is fine.
