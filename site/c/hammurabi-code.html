《汉谟拉比法典》 Hànmólābǐ fǎdiǎnthe Code of Hammurabi

⏳ 前18世纪

迄今已知世界上第一部较为完整的成文法典，刻于黑色石柱（现藏卢浮宫），正文282条；反映古巴比伦森严的社会等级（有公民权自由民、无公民权自由民、奴隶）与活跃的商品经济，严格保护奴隶主利益。

The earliest substantially complete written law code known, its 282 clauses carved on a black stela now in the Louvre; it reveals Old Babylon's rigid hierarchy of full citizens, free non-citizens and slaves, its lively commerce — and its ruthless protection of slave-owners' interests.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“成文法”= written/statute law（与习惯法 customary law 相对）。“以眼还眼”原则学名 lex talionis。注意教材强调其阶级性——英语表述可用 justice was graded by status。
