Kroeber's China's Economy and Keyu Jin's New China Playbook supply English's standard vocabulary for the model: investment-led growth, land finance, financial repression, the local-official tournament, rebalancing, the mayor economy.

Etymology 词源

repression（抑制）源自拉丁 reprimere（压回）——financial repression 指人为压低存款利率以补贴投资,术语自带批评立场。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ financial repression /faɪˈnænʃəl rɪˈprɛʃən/
    Financial repression taxes savers invisibly and lends the proceeds to concrete.
  • ▸ investment-led growth /ɪnˈvɛstmənt lɛd ɡroʊθ/
    Investment-led growth builds tomorrow's capacity with yesterday's deposits.
