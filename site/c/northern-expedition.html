国共合作与北伐战争 Běifá zhànzhēngthe First United Front and the Northern Expedition

🔊 united front /juˈnaɪtɪd frənt/

⏳ 1924年–1928年

1923年中共三大决定与国民党合作；1924年国民党一大（确立联俄联共扶助农工政策、新三民主义）标志国共第一次合作正式建立，创办黄埔军校（孙中山任命蒋介石任校长、周恩来任政治部主任）培养军事政治人才。1925年孙中山逝世。1926年广州国民政府北伐：对象吴佩孚孙传芳张作霖；叶挺独立团贺胜桥汀泗桥屡破吴部（“铁军”），北伐军从珠江流域打到长江流域，工农革命运动蓬勃发展。1927年蒋介石发动四一二反革命政变（南京国民政府）、汪精卫七一五“分共”——国民革命失败，第一次国共合作破裂；1928年张学良东北易帜，南京政府名义上统一全国。

Taught by defeat that it needed allies, the young CCP joined Sun Yat-sen's reorganised Nationalists (KMT) in 1924 — Soviet advisers, the Whampoa Military Academy (Chiang Kai-shek commandant, Zhou Enlai in the political department), and a shared enemy in the warlords. The Northern Expedition (1926) swept from Canton to the Yangzi, Ye Ting's 'Iron Army' spearheading, mass movements rising in its wake — until Chiang turned the guns inward in the Shanghai coup of 12 April 1927, Wang Jingwei followed, and the first united front died in the purge. By 1928, with the Manchurian warlord's allegiance, Chiang's Nanjing government ruled a nominally reunified China.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

国民党 = the Kuomintang (KMT)/Nationalists；黄埔军校 = the Whampoa Military Academy。四一二政变英语多作 the Shanghai massacre/coup of 1927。“国民革命”= the National Revolution。统一战线 united front 是中共政治学关键概念的初版——今日 united front work 仍是外媒热词。
