纵向一体化 zòngxiàng yìtǐhuàvertical integration

🔊 vertical integration /ˈvərtɪkəl ˌɪnəˈgreɪʃən/

企业向上游（供应）或下游（分销/客户）扩展，把原本外购的环节纳入内部。可控制关键投入、攫取更多价值链利润、保护质量，但牺牲灵活性并增加固定成本。

Extending the firm upstream (supply) or downstream (distribution/customer), bringing bought-in activities in-house. It can secure key inputs, capture more of the value chain and protect quality — at the cost of flexibility and higher fixed costs.

Etymology 词源

integrate 源自拉丁 integrare（使完整，integer 完整之数）——一体化即“把分散环节合为整体”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ backward integration /ˈbækwərd ˌɪnəˈgreɪʃən/
    Backward integration buys your supplier before it can raise your prices.
  • ▸ forward integration /ˈfɔrwərd ˌɪnəˈgreɪʃən/
    Forward integration puts you closer to the customer — and to the margin.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ integrate up/down the chain
  • ▸ own the supply chain
  • ▸ in-house vs outsource
  • ▸ control critical inputs

In the wild 语境例句

Tesla vertically integrated batteries and software when suppliers couldn't deliver — owning the chokepoints its rivals must rent.
