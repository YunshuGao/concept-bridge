《金榜题名之后》（出路分化与文化资本） jīnbǎng tímíng zhīhòuAfter the Exam Lists: how graduates' paths diverge

🔊 cultural capital /ˈkəlʧərəl ˈkæpɪtəl/

⏳ 2023年

郑雅君对精英大学生的研究：同进名校，出路为何分化？关键在文化资本差异塑造的两种“上大学的方式”——目标掌控模式（多为优势家庭：入学即知规则，绩点实习留学步步为营）与直觉依赖模式（多为寒门：延续高考惯性埋头读书，临毕业才发现游戏规则已变）。“懂游戏规则”本身是隐性课程——布迪厄文化资本理论的中国校园实证。接续科举以来“上榜之后”的古老命题。

Zheng Yajun's study of elite-university students asks why equal admission yields unequal exits: cultural capital splits them into two modes of 'doing university' — the target-mastery mode (advantaged students who arrive knowing the meta-game: GPA, internships, exchange, each move sequenced) and the intuition-reliance mode (first-generation students who keep gaokao-grinding until graduation reveals the rules changed). Bourdieu's hidden curriculum, documented on Chinese campuses — and the modern instalment of a question as old as the examination lists themselves.

capital（资本）转喻+cultural——布迪厄术语：可继承、可增值的文化资产。
