人民解放战争 Jiěfàng zhànzhēngthe Chinese Civil War (War of Liberation)

⏳ 1946年–1949年

抗战胜利后全国人民渴望和平民主；1945年毛泽东赴重庆谈判（“弥天大勇”）签《双十协定》；1946年政治协商会议。但1946年6月蒋介石撕毁协定全面进攻中原解放区，内战爆发；国民党进攻陕北山东，中共以运动战粉碎（毛泽东转战陕北、彭德怀青化砭沙家店，粟裕孟良崮）。解放区土地改革（《中国土地法大纲》耕者有其田）使农民踊跃参军支前。1947年刘邓大军千里跃进大别山揭开战略进攻序幕；1948–49年辽沈、淮海、平津三大战役（歼灭和改编国民党军150多万、北平和平解放）基本消灭国民党军主力；1949年4月渡江战役解放南京，国民党在大陆的统治结束。（国统区经济崩溃、通货膨胀，第二条战线反饥饿反内战运动。）

Peace talks at Chongqing produced an accord Chiang tore up within a year: full civil war from June 1946. The script inverted expectations — the Nationalists' 4.3 million men and American equipment against a party whose land reform ('land to the tiller') turned the northern peasantry into its rear, whose generals traded cities for annihilation battles, and whose enemy's currency was dying of hyperinflation. Liu Bocheng and Deng Xiaoping's 1947 lunge to the Dabie mountains turned the tide; the three great campaigns of 1948–49 — Liaoshen, Huaihai, Pingjin — devoured 1.5 million Nationalist troops (Beiping surrendering intact); and in April 1949 the PLA crossed the Yangzi and took Nanjing: Nationalist rule on the mainland was over.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“解放战争”= the War of Liberation（中方名）/the Chinese Civil War（通名）——again, naming is narrating。淮海战役国际文献亦作 the Huaihai Campaign。“耕者有其田”= land to the tiller（孙中山口号由中共兑现——继承政治的修辞学）。金圆券恶性通胀链 [[versailles]] 魏玛、[[jiaozi]]。
