地理即命运 dìlǐ jí mìngyùngeography is destiny

🔊 destiny /ˈdɛstɪni/

地理决定论的通俗表述:一地的地形、气候、资源塑造其经济、文化与政制——斯巴达的平原造就农业奴隶制军国,雅典的港湾造就贸易民主。蒋教授反复用以对比城邦,亦是戴蒙德《枪炮病菌与钢铁》的主线。

The popular form of geographic determinism: a place's terrain, climate and resources shape its economy, culture and politics — Sparta's plains bred an agrarian slave-militarism, Athens's harbours bred trading democracy. Diamond's Guns, Germs and Steel is the book-length version.

Etymology 词源

destiny 源自拉丁 destinare(固定、注定)——“命运”即“被固定之事”;“地理即命运”把命运的锚点从星辰移到地形。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ geographic determinism /ˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪk dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪzəm/
    Geographic determinism loads the dice; it does not throw them.
  • ▸ environmental possibilism /ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈpɒsɪbɪlɪzəm/
    Possibilism replies: geography deals the hand, culture plays it.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

Why did Athens sail and Sparta march? Geography is destiny — one had harbours, the other had plains and helots.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

学界警惕其滑向决定论——possibilism(或然论)是标准修正:地理设约束不设剧本。对接用户地理/GIS背景与教材“人地关系”,是人文地理的元命题;段义孚(topophilia)恰是对纯地理决定论的人文主义反拨。
