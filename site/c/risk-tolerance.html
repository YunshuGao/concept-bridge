风险承受度 fēngxiǎn chéngshòudùrisk tolerance (ability vs willingness)

🔊 risk tolerance /rɪsk ˈtɑlərəns/

投资者能承受多大损失。须区分两面:承受能力(ability,客观——财力、时间跨度、收入稳定性)与承受意愿(willingness,主观——心理舒适度)。二者不一致时通常取更保守的一方。它决定组合的股债比例,是IPS与资产配置的关键输入。

How much loss an investor can bear. It has two sides that must be separated: ability (objective — wealth, time horizon, income stability) and willingness (subjective — psychological comfort). When they conflict, you generally take the more conservative. It sets the stock-bond balance and is a key input to the IPS and asset allocation.

Etymology 词源

tolerance 源自拉丁 tolerare（承受、忍耐）——风险'承受度'即能忍受多少波动与损失。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ ability vs willingness /əˈbɪləˌti ˈvərsəz ˈwɪlɪŋnəs/
    Ability vs willingness: a wealthy client may afford risk but not stomach it.
  • ▸ risk appetite /rɪsk ˈæpəˌtaɪt/
    Risk appetite that outruns risk capacity is how people sell at the bottom.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ ability vs willingness to take risk
  • ▸ risk capacity
  • ▸ the conservative of the two
  • ▸ stomach a 20% loss

In the wild 语境例句

A client says 'I want high returns but can't tolerate losing 20%' — ability and willingness conflict, so the IPS anchors to the lower, and the portfolio is built to a loss the client can actually endure.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

最经典的实务问题:客户嘴上要高收益、心里怕大跌。行为金融(见 loss-aversion)解释为何'意愿'常低于'能力';好顾问管理的是能被坚持下去的组合,不是理论最优。
