造纸术 zàozhǐshùpapermaking

⏳ 东汉（105年）

纸问世前文字书于甲骨简帛：简重帛贵，使用受限。西汉已懂造纸基本方法（考古发现西汉麻纸）；东汉蔡伦总结前人经验改进工艺，以树皮麻头破布旧渔网等植物纤维造纸（“蔡侯纸”），质量大增、原料易得价廉——纸逐渐取代简帛成为广泛书写材料，便利典籍流传。世界各国造纸术大都自中国辗转流传，是中国对世界文明的伟大贡献之一。

Before paper, China wrote on bone, bamboo and silk — the one heavy, the other dear. Western Han workshops already made rough paper; in the Eastern Han the palace officer Cai Lun perfected it from bark, hemp, rags and old fishing nets — cheap, light, everywhere copyable. Paper displaced bamboo and silk, set texts free, and travelled (via Talas and the Arabs) to remake every literate civilisation: China's gift to the written world.
