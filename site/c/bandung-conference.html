万隆会议 Wànlóng huìyìthe Bandung Conference

🔊 non-alignment /nɑn-əˈlaɪnmənt/

⏳ 1955年

战后民族解放运动席卷全球，新独立国家面临巩固政权发展经济的共同问题、反对冷战。1955年4月29个亚非国家和地区代表在印尼万隆举行第一次亚非会议，通过和平相处友好合作十项原则；“万隆精神”即团结合作、反帝反殖、争取民族独立、保卫和平。发展中国家自此作为新兴政治力量登上国际舞台；周恩来临时演讲提出“求同存异”方针保证会议成功，中国的和平共处五项原则渐获国际认可。

In April 1955 twenty-nine newly or nearly independent Asian and African nations met at Bandung — the first such gathering without the colonial powers — adopting ten principles of coexistence and proclaiming what became the Bandung spirit: solidarity against imperialism and colonialism, independence and peace. The developing world stepped onto the stage as a force of its own; Zhou Enlai's improvised 'seek common ground while reserving differences' saved the meeting and made China's Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence international currency.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“求同存异”标准译法 seek common ground while reserving/shelving differences。“和平共处五项原则”= the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence。“第三世界”the Third World 一词今渐被 the Global South/developing countries 取代（原义本是冷战三分法）。不结盟运动 = the Non-Aligned Movement（1961）。
