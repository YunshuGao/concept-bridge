太平天国运动 Tàipíng Tiānguóthe Taiping Rebellion

🔊 millenarianism /ˌmɪlɪˈneəriənɪzəm/

⏳ 1851年–1864年

鸦片战争后赋税加重、灾荒频仍，1851年洪秀全（创拜上帝会）金田起义建号太平天国，1853年克南京改天京定都，颁《天朝田亩制度》（“有田同耕，有饭同食……无处不均匀，无人不饱暖”——因绝对平均主义无法实施）；北伐西征军事全盛。1856年天京事变由盛转衰；后期洪仁玕《资政新篇》提出向西方学习、发展资本主义（未能实行）。1864年在中外反动势力联合绞杀下天京陷落。历时14年、席卷大半个中国的规模宏大的农民战争，沉重打击了清朝统治和外国侵略势力，谱写了中国近代史上壮烈的一章。

Out of post-war misery and a failed exam candidate's visions rose history's bloodiest civil war: Hong Xiuquan's God-Worshipping Society proclaimed the Heavenly Kingdom of Great Peace in 1851, took Nanjing as its Heavenly Capital in 1853, and decreed a land system of perfect communal equality — 'land tilled together, rice eaten together... no one anywhere unfed'. Internal slaughter in 1856 broke its momentum; a late programme for Western-style development was never tried; and in 1864, strangled jointly by Qing armies and foreign guns, the Heavenly Capital fell. Fourteen years, half of China, tens of millions dead — a peasant war that shook the dynasty and the foreign powers alike.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

英语通称 the Taiping Rebellion（“叛乱”视角）；中文“运动/起义”视角——命名政治的又一案例。洪秀全的基督教渊源使西方研究兴趣浓厚（“上帝的中国儿子”）。《天朝田亩制度》= the Heavenly Land System；绝对平均主义 = absolute egalitarianism（教材点其空想性，链 [[communist-manifesto]] 的对照有深意）。
