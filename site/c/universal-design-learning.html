通用学习设计（UDL） tōngyòng xuéxí shèjìUniversal Design for Learning (UDL)

🔊 Universal Design for Learning /ˌjunəˈvərsəl dɪˈzaɪn fər ˈlərnɪŋ/

一种预先为所有学习者的多样性而设计的框架,而非事后为个别学生打补丁。三大原则:提供多元的呈现方式（表征）、多元的行动与表达方式、多元的参与投入方式。理念源自建筑无障碍设计（坡道方便所有人,不止轮椅使用者）——好的通用设计减少了事后调整的需要。

A framework for designing upfront for the diversity of all learners, rather than retrofitting for individuals afterwards. Its three principles: provide multiple means of representation, multiple means of action and expression, and multiple means of engagement. The idea comes from accessible architecture (a ramp helps everyone, not only wheelchair users) — good universal design reduces the need for later adjustments.

Etymology 词源

universal（普适的,拉丁 universus 整体）——为'所有人'预先设计;借自建筑学的 universal design。
