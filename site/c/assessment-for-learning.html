为了学习的评估（AfL） wèile xuéxí de pínggūassessment for learning (AfL)

🔊 assessment for learning /əˈsɛsmənt fər ˈlərnɪŋ/

以评估信息即时指导教与学的形成性实践:明确成功标准、有效提问、给出可操作的改进反馈、同伴与自我评估、据证据调整教学。它把评估从'事后判分'变为'即时导航'。布莱克与威廉的研究表明,它是提升学习成效最有力的手段之一。GTPA评估实践的核心。

The formative practice of using assessment information to steer teaching and learning in the moment: clarifying success criteria, effective questioning, giving actionable improvement feedback, peer and self-assessment, and adjusting teaching from the evidence. It turns assessment from after-the-fact grading into real-time navigation. Black and Wiliam's research shows it among the most powerful levers on learning. Central to the GTPA's assessing practice.

Etymology 词源

for（为了）——评估'为了'促进学习而非仅判定;由 Black 与 Wiliam《Inside the Black Box》普及。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ formative assessment /ˈfɔːrmətɪv əˈsɛsmənt/
    Formative assessment feeds back into teaching before the grade is ever fixed.
  • ▸ minute-by-minute assessment /ˈmɪnət-baɪ-ˈmɪnət əˈsɛsmənt/
    Minute-by-minute assessment reads the room and reteaches on the spot.

In the wild 语境例句
