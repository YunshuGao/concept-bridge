中国共产党的诞生 Zhōngguó Gòngchǎndǎng dànshēngthe founding of the Chinese Communist Party

⏳ 1921年

十月革命后李大钊率先在中国传播马克思主义（《庶民的胜利》《布尔什维主义的胜利》《我的马克思主义观》）；五四后马克思主义与中国工人运动结合，1920年陈独秀在上海、李大钊在北京等地建共产党早期组织（“南陈北李，相约建党”）。1921年7月中共一大在上海（后转嘉兴南湖红船）召开，代表毛泽东董必武等13人，确定党的名称、奋斗目标（推翻资产阶级政权建立无产阶级专政实现共产主义）、中心工作（领导组织工人运动），选举陈独秀为中央局书记——中国共产党诞生是开天辟地的大事变，中国革命的面貌焕然一新。1922年二大制定民主革命纲领；1923年京汉铁路工人大罢工（二七惨案）表明单枪匹马不能取胜。

The October Revolution 'delivered Marxism to China' (Mao's later phrase), Li Dazhao its first apostle; May Fourth wove it into the young labour movement, and in July 1921 thirteen delegates — Mao Zedong among them — convened in a Shanghai shikumen and finished aboard a pleasure boat on Jiaxing's South Lake: the First Congress of the Chinese Communist Party, goal proletarian revolution, method the organisation of labour, membership nationwide about fifty. The textbook's phrase for it is cosmological — 'an event that opened heaven and split the earth' — and the bloody failure of the 1923 railway strike taught the infant party its first strategic lesson: it could not win alone.

In the wild 语境例句

The 'red boat' of Jiaxing carries a hundred-plus years of iconography — party anniversaries are staged around it, and 'red boat spirit' is current official vocabulary; knowing the 1921 scene decodes the imagery instantly.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

中共 = the Chinese Communist Party (CCP；亦见 CPC——中国官方偏好后者，外媒用前者，用词即立场的微观案例)。“开天辟地”= earth-shaking/epoch-making（盘古开天神话的动用）。李大钊、陈独秀的后来命运（就义/开除）是党史叙事复杂性的入口。
