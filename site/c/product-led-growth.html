产品驱动增长 chǎnpǐn qūdòng zēngzhǎngproduct-led growth (PLG)

🔊 product-led growth /ˈprɑdəkt-lɛd groʊθ/

让产品本身成为获客、转化与扩张的主引擎——用户先免费试用、自助上手、亲身体验价值,再自然转为付费并向同事传播,销售团队退居其后。它降低获客成本、缩短销售周期,是现代 SaaS 的主流增长范式。

Making the product itself the main engine of acquisition, conversion and expansion — users try it free, onboard themselves, experience the value first-hand, then convert to paid and spread it to colleagues, with sales in a supporting role. It lowers acquisition cost and shortens the sales cycle — the dominant growth model of modern SaaS.

Etymology 词源

product-led（产品主导的）——增长由'产品'而非销售或营销'主导';缩写 PLG 是 SaaS 增长的核心词。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ self-serve growth /sɛlf-sərv groʊθ/
    Self-serve growth lets the product sell itself before a human ever calls.
  • ▸ the freemium funnel /ðə ˈfriːmiəm ˈfʌnəl/
    The freemium funnel converts free users into paid ones by letting value do the persuading.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ product-led growth
  • ▸ self-serve onboarding
  • ▸ land and expand
  • ▸ let the product sell

In the wild 语境例句

A tool that spreads through free use inside a company — colleague showing colleague — grows through product-led growth, its adoption arriving before any salesperson does.
