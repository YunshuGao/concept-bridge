引流亏损品 yǐnliú kuīsǔn pǐnthe loss leader

🔊 loss leader /lɔs ˈlidər/

以低于成本或极薄利润出售某商品,吸引顾客进店/上平台,再靠他们购买的其他高利润商品赚回来。超市的特价鸡蛋、打印机的低价机身(靠墨盒赚钱)、游戏机(靠游戏赚钱)皆是。它是跨产品定价与生态锁定的经典策略。

Selling an item below cost or at razor-thin margin to draw customers into the store or onto the platform, then profiting from the higher-margin items they also buy. The supermarket's cut-price eggs, the cheap printer (profit is in the ink), the games console (profit is in the games) are all examples. A classic strategy of cross-product pricing and ecosystem lock-in.

Etymology 词源

loss（亏损）+ leader（引领者）——用一件亏本商品'引领'顾客上门,故名。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the razor-and-blades model /ðə ˈreɪzər-ənd-bleɪdz ˈmɑdəl/
    The razor-and-blades model gives away the razor and charges forever for the blades.
  • ▸ a doorbuster /ə ˈdɔːrbʌstər/
    A doorbuster loses money on one item to sell a full cart of profitable ones.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ a loss leader
  • ▸ razor and blades
  • ▸ sell the printer, profit on ink
  • ▸ cross-subsidy

In the wild 语境例句

A console sold at a loss is a loss leader — the maker profits on years of game sales, an ecosystem where the cheap hardware locks in expensive software.
