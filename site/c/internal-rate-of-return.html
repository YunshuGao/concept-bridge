内部收益率 nèibù shōuyìlǜinternal rate of return (IRR)

🔊 internal rate of return /ˌɪnˈtərnəl reɪt əv rɪˈtərn/

使项目净现值恰好为零的贴现率——即项目自身的年化回报率。IRR 高于资本成本则值得投。直观易懂，但在非常规现金流或比较不同规模项目时会误导。

The discount rate at which a project's NPV equals zero — the project's own annualised return. If the IRR exceeds the cost of capital, the project is worth doing. Intuitive, but it misleads with unconventional cash flows or when comparing projects of different sizes.

Etymology 词源

internal 指“项目内部产生”的回报率，不依赖外部假设的贴现率——它是解方程解出来的那个率。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the hurdle rate /ðə ˈhɜːrdəl reɪt/
    A project clears the hurdle rate or it doesn't get funded.
  • ▸ money-weighted return /ˈməni-ˈweɪtɪd rɪˈtərn/
    IRR is a money-weighted return: it cares when the cash arrives, not just how much.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ beat the hurdle rate
  • ▸ the IRR of the deal
  • ▸ gross vs net IRR
  • ▸ clear the cost of capital

In the wild 语境例句

Private equity funds live and die by IRR — a 25% IRR means every dollar invested compounded at 25% a year until it came back.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

IRR 假设中间现金流按 IRR 再投资（常不现实），且忽略绝对规模——20%回报的小项目 IRR 高，却可能不如10%回报的大项目创造的价值多。
