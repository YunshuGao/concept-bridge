鸦片战争与《南京条约》 Yāpiàn zhànzhēngthe Opium War and the Treaty of Nanjing

⏳ 1839年–1842年

英国正当贸易中中国出超，遂大量走私鸦片（掠走三四亿银元），白银外流、军民体质与战力俱损。1839年林则徐虎门销烟——中国人民禁烟斗争的伟大胜利。1840年6月英国发动鸦片战争；清朝制度腐败、经济落后、武器陈旧，关天培陈化成等战死，1842年8月被迫签中国近代史上第一个丧权辱国的不平等条约中英《南京条约》：开放广州福州厦门宁波上海五口通商、割香港岛、赔款2100万银元、协定关税；续有《虎门条约》获领事裁判权与片面最惠国待遇，中美《望厦条约》中法《黄埔条约》。中国开始沦为半殖民地半封建社会，鸦片战争成为中国近代史的开端。魏源编《海国图志》提出“师夷长技以制夷”。

Britain, bleeding silver into a trade China dominated, balanced the books with smuggled opium — three to four hundred million dollars' worth — until Commissioner Lin Zexu destroyed 1.1m kg of it at Humen in 1839. London answered commerce's grievance with the fleet: the war of 1840–42 met brave garrisons and obsolete weapons, and ended aboard a British warship with the Treaty of Nanjing — five ports opened, Hong Kong island ceded, $21m indemnity, tariffs fixed by treaty — followed by extraterritoriality and most-favoured-nation clauses for Britain, America and France. China began its descent into what the textbook names 'semi-colonial, semi-feudal society'; modern Chinese history starts here, as does the search for answers — Wei Yuan's 'learn the barbarians' strengths to check the barbarians' first among them.

In the wild 语境例句

'Century of humiliation' — the frame every Chinese statement on sovereignty invokes — starts its clock in 1840; Western historiography counters with 'the opening of China', and the two phrases fight the same war forever.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“半殖民地半封建社会”= semi-colonial, semi-feudal society（中国马克思主义史学的总定性，英语文献引用时通常带引号说明其框架属性）。“出超/入超”= trade surplus/deficit——本课直接教授贸易术语！“协定关税”= treaty tariffs（关税自主 tariff autonomy 的丧失）；“领事裁判权”= extraterritoriality/consular jurisdiction。马克思论鸦片贸易的引文显示教材的经典谱系。
