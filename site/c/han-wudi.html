汉武帝巩固大一统 Hàn Wǔdì dàyītǒngHan Wudi's consolidation of the unified state

⏳ 前141年–前87年

汉武帝（前141–前87在位）多管齐下强化中央：政治上采主父偃议行“推恩令”，令诸侯分封子弟侯国、封地愈分愈小，又借故削爵除国，并立刺史监察地方、定察举制选官；思想上采董仲舒议罢黜百家尊崇儒术，长安兴太学以五经为教材——儒学自此居正统主导、为历代推崇；经济上收铸币权铸五铢钱、盐铁官营专卖、统一调配平抑物价；军事上卫青霍去病三击匈奴（前119年漠北战役重创之，部分匈奴西迁）。西汉进入鼎盛。

Wudi (r. 141–87 BC) fastened the empire together on every front: the 'grace-extending decree' obliged princes to subdivide their fiefs among all their sons until none could threaten the throne; inspectors policed the provinces and recommendation examinations recruited officials; Dong Zhongshu's synthesis made Confucianism state orthodoxy, taught from the new Imperial Academy — the ideology of empire for two millennia thereafter; coinage, salt and iron became state monopolies funding it all; and Wei Qing and Huo Qubing broke the Xiongnu in the steppe, sending part of them migrating west. The Han reached its zenith.

The 'salt and iron' debate of 81 BC — state monopoly v. free enterprise — reads like a modern SOE-reform panel; Chinese economic commentary still calls the topic 盐铁论 without needing a footnote.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“大一统”= the grand unification/unity（中国政治思想的元概念，直译 grand unification 需加注）。“推恩令”巧在以恩为名行削藩之实——可译 the decree of extended favour（分析性译法）。“罢黜百家，独尊儒术”= dismissing the hundred schools, honouring Confucianism alone。盐铁专卖 = the state salt and iron monopolies——国企议题的两千年前源头，链 [[monopoly-capitalism]]。
