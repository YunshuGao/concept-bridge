多元化折价 duōyuánhuà zhéjiàthe conglomerate discount

🔊 conglomerate discount /kənˈglɑmərət ˈdɪskaʊnt/

多元化集团的市值常低于其各业务分拆后的估值之和——因复杂性、资本错配、透明度低与总部成本。这是拆分、剥离与激进投资者要求“分家”的核心动因。

A diversified group often trades below the sum of its parts if broken up — owing to complexity, capital misallocation, poor transparency and head-office cost. This gap drives break-ups, spin-offs and activist demands to split the company.

Etymology 词源

conglomerate 源自拉丁 conglomerare（滚成一团，glomus 线球）——企业集团即“滚在一起的多元业务团”；折价即市场对这团“看不清”的惩罚。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ sum-of-the-parts /səm-əv-ðə-pɑrts/
    A sum-of-the-parts valuation reveals the discount the market is charging for complexity.
  • ▸ break-up value /breɪk-əp ˈvælju/
    Break-up value is what an activist waves at a sleepy board.
