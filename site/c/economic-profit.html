经济利润 jīngjì lìrùneconomic profit (EVA)

🔊 economic profit /ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk ˈprɑfɪt/

会计利润再减去股权资本的机会成本——只有当回报超过全部资本（含股权）的成本时才为正。它纠正了会计利润“免费使用股东资本”的假象:一家会计盈利的公司可能仍在摧毁经济价值。

Accounting profit minus the opportunity cost of equity capital — positive only when returns exceed the cost of all capital, equity included. It corrects accounting profit's illusion that shareholder capital is free: a firm can be accounting-profitable while destroying economic value.

Etymology 词源

由咨询公司 Stern Stewart 商标化为 EVA（Economic Value Added，经济增加值）——把经济学的“机会成本”正式搬进公司业绩考核。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ economic value added /ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk ˈvælju ˈædɪd/
    Economic value added asks whether you beat the cost of the money you used.
  • ▸ residual income /rɪˈzɪʤuəl ˈɪnˌkəm/
    Residual income is what's left after every provider of capital is paid their due.

In the wild 语境例句

A division reporting 50 of accounting profit but tying up capital that costs 60 has negative economic profit — it looks like a winner and is a drain.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

经济利润的关键洞见:股东资本不是免费的。会计报表把利息（债务成本）算进去，却把股权成本当零——经济利润补上这一课。
