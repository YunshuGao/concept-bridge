建构性对齐 jiàngòuxìng duìqíconstructive alignment

🔊 constructive alignment /kənˈstrəktɪv əˈlaɪnmənt/

比格斯的原则:学习成果、评估任务、教学活动三者必须相互对齐、彼此支撑——想让学生学会'评估算法',就得让评估任务要求评估算法、教学活动练习评估算法。对齐则高效,错位则空转（教A考B学C是最常见的失败）。是逆向设计的理论内核。

Biggs's principle: learning outcomes, assessment tasks and teaching activities must all align and reinforce one another — if you want students to learn to 'evaluate algorithms', the assessment must require evaluating algorithms and the activities must practise it. Aligned, it is efficient; misaligned, it spins its wheels (teaching A, testing B, having them practise C is the commonest failure). The theoretical core of backward design.

Etymology 词源

align 源自古法语 aligner（排成一线,拉丁 linea 线）——对齐即'把目标、评估、活动排在同一条线上'。

In the wild 语境例句

If the outcome is 'evaluate', but the test only asks students to 'recall', the unit lacks constructive alignment — and no amount of good teaching will fix the mismatch.
