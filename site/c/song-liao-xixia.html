辽、西夏与北宋的并立（澶渊之盟） Chányuān zhī méngSong, Liao and Xia: the Chanyuan settlement

⏳ 916年–1125年

10世纪初契丹耶律阿保机统一各部建辽（发展生产创制文字）；辽太宗得后晋石敬瑭所割燕云十六州，与中原冲突加剧。11世纪前期党项元昊称帝建西夏（仿唐宋制度、创西夏文字）。1004年辽大举攻宋至澶州，寇准力劝真宗亲征，宋胜后仍议和：宋给辽岁币——“澶渊之盟”，此后长期和平；宋夏和约同型（元昊称臣、宋给岁币）。边界设榷场互市，贸易量大，促进经济文化交流与民族了解。

The Song never ruled alone: the Khitan Liao (from 907, literate and farming as well as riding, holding the Sixteen Prefectures a Chinese usurper had ceded) and the Tangut Western Xia (from 1038, with its own script and Tang-style institutions) flanked it north and west. After beating back a Liao invasion at Chanyuan (1004–5), the Song bought permanence instead of pressing victory: annual payments of silver and silk — 'the Chanyuan Covenant' — and a matching deal with Xia. A century of peace followed, lubricated by official border markets; the tribute was cheaper than war, and both sides knew it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“岁币”= annual payments/indemnities（谁向谁纳贡的方向问题使 tribute 一词在此充满反讽——中原王朝是支付方）。契丹 Khitan 一词经俄语成为 Cathay（契丹=中国的旧称，国泰航空 Cathay Pacific 即此）——词源彩蛋。燕云十六州 = the Sixteen Prefectures。
