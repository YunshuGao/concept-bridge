私有制 sīyǒuzhìprivate property

also: private ownership

⏳ 约前3500年起

生产资料归私人占有的制度。教材叙述其随剩余产品出现而产生，导致原始社会解体、阶级社会形成。

Ownership of productive assets by individuals rather than the community; in the textbook's Marxist narrative it emerges with agricultural surplus and dissolves primitive communal life into class society.

In the wild 语境例句

Secure property rights, economists never tire of saying, are the difference between farming your land and merely squatting on it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“私有制”作制度讲，译 private ownership/the institution of private property；日常英语 private property 更多指“私人财产/私人领地”（Private property — keep out!）。经济学讨论中常用 property rights（产权）这一分析框架。
