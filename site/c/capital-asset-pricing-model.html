🔊 CAPM /ˈkæpəm/

金融理论基石:一项资产的预期回报 = 无风险利率 + β ×（市场回报−无风险利率）。核心思想是只有无法分散的系统性风险（β）才应获得补偿，可分散的个体风险不该被定价。

A cornerstone of finance: an asset's expected return = risk-free rate + beta × (market return − risk-free rate). Its core idea is that only undiversifiable, systematic risk (beta) deserves compensation; diversifiable idiosyncratic risk should not be priced.

Etymology 词源

Capital Asset Pricing Model 缩略（读作 'cap-em'）——Sharpe、Lintner 等1960年代创立，Sharpe 因此获诺奖。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the security market line /ðə sɪˈkjʊrəti ˈmɑrkɪt laɪn/
    The security market line plots required return against beta as a straight line.
  • ▸ systematic risk pricing /ˌsɪstəˈmætɪk rɪsk ˈpraɪsɪŋ/
    CAPM's bargain: bear market risk and be paid, bear specific risk for free.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ the CAPM formula
  • ▸ only systematic risk is rewarded
  • ▸ the market risk premium

In the wild 语境例句

CAPM says a stock twice as volatile as the market (beta 2) should offer twice the market's risk premium — no more, no less.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

CAPM 假设苛刻（有效市场、单期、可无限借贷），实证解释力有限——但作为思维框架（只有系统性风险该被补偿）仍是估值语言的基础。
