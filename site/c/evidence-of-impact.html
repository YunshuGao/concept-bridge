🔊 evidence of impact on student learning /ˈɛvədəns əv ˌɪmˈpækt ɔn ˈstudənt ˈlərnɪŋ/

GTPA与教师认证的核心要求:不只描述教了什么,而是用具体证据（学生作品、前后测数据、观察记录、反馈）证明教学'导致'了学习的发生与进步。它把教学从'活动'转向'成效',是循证教学（evidence-based practice）在教师评估中的落地。

The central demand of the GTPA and teacher accreditation: not describing what you taught but using concrete evidence (student work samples, pre/post data, observations, feedback) to show that your teaching caused learning to happen and improve. It shifts teaching from activity to outcome — evidence-based practice applied to the assessment of teachers.

Etymology 词源

evidence 源自拉丁 ex-（出）+ videre（看）——证据即'使人看得见'之物;教学影响须'可见'方能采信,呼应 Hattie 的 visible learning。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ impact on learning /ˌɪmˈpækt ɔn ˈlərnɪŋ/
    Impact on learning is the difference the teaching made, shown rather than claimed.
  • ▸ evidence-based practice /ˈɛvədəns-beɪst ˈpræktɪs/
    Evidence-based practice asks teachers to prove what works, not to defend what's familiar.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

Two work samples — one before instruction, one after — annotated to show the specific growth, are stronger evidence of impact than any lesson plan.
