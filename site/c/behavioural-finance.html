行为金融学 xíngwéi jīnróngxuébehavioural finance

🔊 behavioural finance /bɪˈheɪvjərəl ˈfaɪˌnæns/

用心理学解释市场为何偏离理性:投资者受损失厌恶、锚定、羊群效应、过度自信、可得性偏差驱动，制造泡沫与恐慌。它挑战有效市场假说——价格并非总是理性，情绪会系统性地扭曲定价。

Using psychology to explain why markets deviate from rationality: investors are driven by loss aversion, anchoring, herding, overconfidence and availability bias, creating bubbles and panics. It challenges the efficient market hypothesis — prices are not always rational, and emotion distorts them systematically.

Etymology 词源

behavioural（行为的）——研究真实投资者的'行为'而非理性人假设;Kahneman、Thaler 等因此获诺奖。
