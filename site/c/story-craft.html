故事技艺（编剧与叙事理论） gùshi jìyìthe craft of story

⏳ 2000年–2016年

三本互补的叙事方法论：克龙《故事天才》（Story Genius）——故事的本质是主角内在误信（misbelief）被现实逐步击碎的心理弧线，脑科学证明人靠故事模拟生存；特鲁比《故事的解剖》——欲望（desire）+对手（opponent）+道德抉择的22步结构，反对三幕式公式；金《写作这回事》（On Writing）——回忆录+工具箱：“门关着写初稿，门开着改”（write with the door closed）、副词是地狱之路、情境（situation）先于情节。对游戏课（9GAMZA）的叙事设计与英语写作教学皆为直接弹药。

Three complementary manuals: Cron's Story Genius — story is a protagonist's misbelief colliding with events, the brain running survival simulations (neuroscience as story theory); Truby's Anatomy of Story — desire, opponent and moral argument in 22 steps, against three-act formula; King's On Writing — memoir plus toolbox: first drafts with the door closed, 'the road to hell is paved with adverbs', situation before plot. Direct ammunition both for narrative design in your Unity course and for teaching writing itself.
