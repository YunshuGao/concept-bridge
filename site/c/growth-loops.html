增长循环 zēngzhǎng xúnhuángrowth loops

🔊 growth loops /groʊθ lups/

自我强化的增长机制:产出（新用户、内容、收入）被再投入为下一轮增长的输入，形成复利飞轮，而非线性的漏斗。病毒循环（用户带用户）、内容循环、付费再投资循环是主要类型。好的增长循环让增长内生、可复利。

Self-reinforcing growth mechanisms where the output (new users, content, revenue) is reinvested as the input to the next round of growth — a compounding flywheel rather than a linear funnel. Viral loops (users bring users), content loops and paid-reinvestment loops are the main types. A good growth loop makes growth endogenous and compounding.

Etymology 词源

loop（循环）——增长的产出'循环'回流为输入,与一次性的 funnel（漏斗）相对。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the flywheel /ðə ˈflaɪwiːl/
    A flywheel takes effort to start and then turns increasingly by its own momentum.
  • ▸ the viral loop /ðə ˈvaɪrəl lup/
    A viral loop makes each user a recruiter of the next.

In the wild 语境例句

A referral program is a growth loop: each new user invites others, whose sign-ups fund and fuel the next wave — growth that feeds itself.
