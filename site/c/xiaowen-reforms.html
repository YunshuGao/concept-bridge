淝水之战与北魏孝文帝改革 Xiàowéndì gǎigéthe Battle of Fei River and the Xiaowen sinicisation reforms

⏳ 383年–499年

383年前秦苻坚不顾反对强征各族87万大军南下欲灭东晋（“投鞭断流”），东晋8万精兵淝水以少胜多（“草木皆兵”“风声鹤唳”），前秦土崩瓦解、北方再乱。鲜卑拓跋部建北魏，439年统一北方。孝文帝（冯太后奠基）立志移风易俗：494年迁都洛阳，规定朝廷用汉语禁鲜卑语、改穿汉服、改汉姓（拓跋→元）、鼓励与汉人贵族联姻——促进民族交融、增强国力。北朝后期北方民族大交融：胡人农耕、汉人牧畜，胡床方凳入中原，汉语成通用语，“胡”“汉”观念淡薄——为中华民族注入新活力，为隋唐多民族国家的繁荣奠基。

In 383 Fu Jian hurled a conscript horde ('my army could dam the river with its whips') at the Eastern Jin and lost to 80,000 at the Fei River — panic phrases from that rout ('every bush a soldier', 'wind-cries and crane-calls') are still daily Chinese. From the wreckage the Xianbei Tuoba built Northern Wei, unified the north (439), and under Emperor Xiaowen chose assimilation over apartness: capital moved to Luoyang (494), Chinese mandated at court, Xianbei dress and surnames abolished (Tuoba became Yuan), intermarriage promoted. The great fusion followed — herders farming, farmers herding, 'Hu' and 'Han' fading as labels — the demographic and cultural base on which Sui-Tang greatness would be built.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“汉化”= sinicisation（当代政策语境同词，含义演变须自觉）；“民族交融”官方译 ethnic integration/fusion（区别于 assimilation 同化的单向义——教材强调双向）。淝水之战成语群：草木皆兵 every bush and tree looks like an enemy soldier；风声鹤唳 the sound of wind and crane alike spell pursuit——中文成语英译的展示柜。
