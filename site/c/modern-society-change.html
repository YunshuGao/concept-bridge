近代经济、社会与文教变化 jìndài jīngjì shèhuì biànhuàeconomic and social change in the Republican era

⏳ 1860年代–1949年

民族资本主义：19世纪六七十年代产生；甲午后张謇“实业救国”创办大生纱厂（状元办厂）；辛亥革命后与一战期间（列强忙于欧战放松掠夺）迎来“短暂的春天”；一战后再受挤压，在夹缝中顽强挣扎（荣氏兄弟等）——总体落后、集中于轻工业与沿海沿江。社会生活：剪辫易服劝禁缠足、改称呼（“先生”）废跪拜，火车轮船电车汽车、照相电影报纸（《申报》）出版（商务印书馆）改变生活；崇洋逐新与传统并存、新旧并呈多元发展、不平衡。教育文化：京师大学堂、1905年废科举兴学堂学制；鲁迅《狂人日记》《阿Q正传》、齐白石徐悲鸿（《愚公移山》）、聂耳《义勇军进行曲》冼星海《黄河大合唱》、茅盾巴金老舍曹禺等。

Beneath the wars, a society modernised in fragments: 'saving the nation through industry' built Zhang Jian's cotton mills (a top examination graduate turned manufacturer — the status revolution in one career), and the First World War's distraction gave native capitalism its 'brief spring' before foreign competition returned. Queues were cut, feet unbound, 'Mister' replaced kowtows; trains, trams, photographs, the Shen Bao press and the Commercial Press remade daily life — unevenly, coast before interior, city before village, new jostling old. The exams gave way to schools (1905), and a modern culture found its canon: Lu Xun's fiction, Xu Beihong's brushes, and the resistance anthems — 'March of the Volunteers' among them — that a later state would make its own.
