🔊 industry analysis /ˈɪndəstri æˈnælɪsɪs/

在给个股估值前先判断其所处行业的结构、生命周期与前景——因为一家好公司在坏行业里也难赚钱(五力模型)。CFA权益投资的第一步:自上而下从宏观→行业→公司,或识别行业驱动因素、周期性与竞争格局,再落到个股。

Judging the structure, life cycle and prospects of a company's industry before valuing the stock — because even a good company struggles in a bad industry (the five forces). The first step of CFA equity analysis: top-down from macro → industry → company, identifying industry drivers, cyclicality and competitive structure before reaching the individual stock.

Etymology 词源

industry 源自拉丁 industria（勤勉）——'行业'本义勤劳制造,后指同类企业的集合。
