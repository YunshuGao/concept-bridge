对学习的评估（AoL） duì xuéxí de pínggūassessment of learning (AoL)

🔊 assessment of learning /əˈsɛsmənt əv ˈlərnɪŋ/

终结性评估,在学习阶段结束时判定学生达成了什么、达到何种水平,用于评分、报告与认证。它须可靠、有效、公平,并对标课程成果与成绩标准。逆向设计正是从这里倒推:先定AoL任务,再设计通向它的教学。

Summative assessment, judging at the end of a phase what students have achieved and to what standard, for grading, reporting and certification. It must be reliable, valid and fair, and aligned to curriculum outcomes and grade standards. Backward design works from here: define the AoL task first, then design the teaching that leads to it.

Etymology 词源

of（对……的）——'对学习的'总结判定;与 for/as learning 构成 NSW 评估三分法。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ summative judgement /ˈsʌmətɪv ˈʤəʤmənt/
    A summative judgement certifies attainment against the standard, for the record.
  • ▸ the reporting grade /ðə rɪˈpɔrtɪŋ greɪd/
    The reporting grade is assessment of learning made official.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ assessment of learning

In the wild 语境例句

The end-of-unit project that earns a mark on the report is assessment of learning — designed first, under backward design, so all teaching aims at it.
