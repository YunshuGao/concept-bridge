卢云：负伤的治疗者 lúyúnNouwen: the wounded healer

🔊 Nouwen /ˈnaʊwən/

荷兰神父卢云的核心概念：助人者不是从高处施与,而是以自己的伤口服侍他人的伤口——“负伤的治疗者”。另有 hospitality（款待:为陌生人腾出自由空间）与 solitude（独处≠孤独）。

Henri Nouwen's signature idea: the helper serves from shared woundedness, not from above. His hospitality means creating free space for the stranger; solitude is aloneness chosen, not suffered.

Etymology 词源

hospitality 源自拉丁 hospes（既是主人也是客人/陌生人）——hospital、hotel、host、hostile 同根:款待与敌意仅一线之隔,词源自带神学。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ compassion /kəmˈpæʃən/
    Compassion — suffering-with — is sympathy that stays in the room.
  • ▸ belovedness /bɪˈlʌvɪdnəs/
    Belovedness, for Nouwen, precedes achievement — the voice before the résumé.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ the wounded healer
  • ▸ creating space for the stranger
  • ▸ from solitude to community
  • ▸ downward mobility of the gospel

In the wild 语境例句

Teacher-training now borrows the wounded healer without citation: the mentor who admits struggle reaches students the expert cannot.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

wounded healer 原型出自荣格（希腊神话喀戎 Chiron:负伤的医神半人马）,卢云使其成为牧养与助人职业的通用语——教育、心理咨询文献高频词。
