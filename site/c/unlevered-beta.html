去杠杆贝塔 qù gànggǎn bèitǎunlevered (asset) beta

🔊 unlevered beta /ʌnˈlɛvərd ˈbeɪtə/

剥离了财务杠杆影响的β,只反映业务本身的风险(资产β)。因为一家公司的股票β同时含经营风险与财务(杠杆)风险,比较不同资本结构的公司时须先'去杠杆'到资产β,再按目标结构'加杠杆'。哈马达方程是标准工具。WACC搭建的关键中间步。

Beta with the effect of financial leverage stripped out, reflecting only the risk of the business itself (asset beta). Because a company's equity beta contains both operating and financial (leverage) risk, comparing firms with different capital structures requires unlevering to the asset beta first, then relevering to the target's structure. The Hamada equation is the standard tool. A key intermediate step in building WACC.

Etymology 词源

unlevered（去杠杆的）——去掉债务放大效应后剩下的'资产'风险;Hamada 方程以物理学家 Robert Hamada 命名。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ asset beta /ˈæˌsɛt ˈbeɪtə/
    Asset beta measures business risk alone, before any debt amplifies it.
  • ▸ the Hamada equation /ðə hɑːˈmɑːdə ɪkˈweɪʒən/
    The Hamada equation is the bridge between a levered equity beta and its unlevered core.

In the wild 语境例句

A heavily indebted peer's equity beta of 2.0 unlevers to an asset beta of 1.1 — the business risk, before leverage inflated it, and the right basis for comparison.
