人地关系与区域认知 réndì guānxì / qūyù rènzhīhuman-environment relations and regional thinking

⏳ 教材体系

人文地理研究人类活动与地理环境的关系。社区与地图（方向、比例尺、图例——从地图和图表中获取信息）；聚落：乡村（农村、渔村、牧村——生产生活与自然环境相适应）与城市（功能分区、城乡差异与联系）。世界典型区域案例：稻作文化的印度/湄公河平原、用机械种庄稼的美国中部平原、牧场牧民（内蒙古/澳大利亚/安第斯）、耕海牧鱼（日本）、沙漠绿洲（波斯坎儿井）、山地之国（瑞士旅游）、水上都市（威尼斯）——因地制宜、人与自然和谐共生：自然环境为人类活动提供条件，人类活动应遵循自然规律；区域发展各具特色，无优劣之分。

Human geography's junior toolkit: reading maps (direction, scale, legend — information literacy avant la lettre), classifying settlements (village types matched to environments; urban functional zones; rural-urban links), and the world tour of adaptation case studies — monsoon rice bowls, Midwestern machine farms, Mongolian and Andean pastoralists, Japan's fishing coasts, qanat-fed oases, Alpine tourism, Venice on water — all teaching one thesis: development succeeds by suiting the land (因地制宜), and regions differ without ranking. The junior version, in effect, of geography's oldest debate — possibilism against determinism — resolved in favour of harmony.

因地制宜 — 'suit measures to local conditions' — is simultaneously a geography principle, a policy cliché in every provincial document, and a GIS analyst's job description; for a demography-and-GIS graduate this node is home turf renamed.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析
