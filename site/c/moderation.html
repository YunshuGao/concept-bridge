评分调节（会商） píngfēn tiáojiémoderation

🔊 moderation /ˌmɑdərˈeɪʃən/

多位评分者对同一批学生作品共同研讨、比对判断标准,以确保评分的一致性、公平性与可靠性的过程。它校准了不同教师'心中的尺子',是标准参照评估可信的保障。GTPA、HSC与校本评估都依赖社会化调节（social moderation）达成共识判断。

The process by which several assessors jointly discuss the same student work and compare their judgements against the standards, to ensure consistency, fairness and reliability of grading. It calibrates the different 'rulers in teachers' heads' and is what makes standards-referenced assessment trustworthy. The GTPA, the HSC and school-based assessment all rely on social moderation to reach agreed judgements.

Etymology 词源

moderate 源自拉丁 moderari（节制、调节,modus 尺度）——调节即'以共同尺度节制个人偏差'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ social moderation /ˈsoʊʃəl ˌmɑdərˈeɪʃən/
    Social moderation replaces one teacher's private judgement with a shared, defensible one.
  • ▸ calibration /ˌkæləˈbreɪʃən/
    Calibration lines up markers so the same work earns the same grade, whoever marks it.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ moderate the marking
  • ▸ reach consistency of judgement
  • ▸ calibrate against the standards
  • ▸ a moderation meeting

In the wild 语境例句

At a moderation meeting, two teachers grading the same coding project to different marks discuss the criteria until their judgements align — reliability, made social.
