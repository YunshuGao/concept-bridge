超级个体 chāojí gètǐthe super individual: a company of one

🔊 solopreneur /ˌsoʊloʊprəˈnɜːr/

《单干》等书界定的中文互联网概念:一人=一家公司——以工具、自动化与个人品牌获得团队级杠杆;认知→变现→流量→产品→组织(外包/AI)的单干路线图。英语对应词:solopreneur, company of one, one-person business。

The Chinese internet's 超级个体: one person operating with a team's leverage through tools, automation and personal brand. English equivalents: solopreneur, company of one, the one-person business.

Etymology 词源

individual 源自拉丁 individuus（不可分割:in-否定+dividere分）——“个体”本义“不可再分之物”;“超级个体”反用其义:一个不可分的人,分出一支队伍的产能。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ company of one /ˈkʌmpəni ʌv wʌn/
    A company of one asks 'how big is enough?' before 'how big is possible?'
  • ▸ leverage stack /ˈlɛvərɪdʒ stæk/
    The super individual's leverage stack: code, content, capital, and now agents.

In the wild 语境例句

This very knowledge base is a super-individual artefact: one teacher, one AI, a publishing pipeline that once needed a department.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

即用户的自我目标(超级个体):此节点是知识库的自我指涉锚点。中文概念偏宏大叙事,英文 company of one(Jarvis)反增长哲学更克制——两种气质,同一结构。
