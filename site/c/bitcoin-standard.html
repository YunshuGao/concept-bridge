《比特币本位》（健全货币论） Bǐtèbì běnwèiThe Bitcoin Standard and sound money

🔊 sound money /saʊnd ˈməni/

⏳ 2018年

阿莫斯从奥地利学派立场重述货币史：健全货币（sound money——难以增发：贵金属的存量/增量比 stock-to-flow）造就低时间偏好（time preference）与文明积累；金本位崩溃后的法币时代即“政府货币”通胀时代；比特币被论证为数字时代的健全货币（2100万枚硬上限）。作为立场鲜明的奥派宣言，与主流货币经济学（[[world-monetary-system]]）对读方见全景。

Ammous retells monetary history as an Austrian-school sermon: sound money (hard to inflate — high stock-to-flow, gold the historical champion) breeds low time preference and civilisation; the post-1971 fiat era is government money and chronic debasement; bitcoin, capped at 21 million, is nominated as the digital successor. Read as a manifesto against the mainstream account in [[world-monetary-system]] — the pairing is the whole crypto debate in two book spines.

Etymology 词源

sound（健全）源自拉丁 sanus（健康）经古法语——sound money 即“健康的钱”。

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

健全货币 = sound money（奥派术语）；时间偏好 = time preference。奥地利学派 the Austrian School（哈耶克米塞斯——链 [[serfdom]] 的《通往奴役之路》作者）。中国语境下比特币讨论的政策约束是另一层对照。
