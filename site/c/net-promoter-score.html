净推荐值（NPS） jìng tuījiàn zhíthe net promoter score (NPS)

🔊 net promoter score /nɛt prəˈmoʊtər skɔr/

用一个问题衡量客户忠诚:'你有多大可能把我们推荐给朋友?'（0–10分）。推荐者（9–10）占比减去贬损者（0–6）占比即 NPS。简单、可比、与增长相关，但也因过度简化、易被操纵而受批评。

Measuring loyalty with one question: 'how likely are you to recommend us to a friend?' (0–10). The share of promoters (9–10) minus the share of detractors (0–6) is the NPS. Simple, comparable and correlated with growth — though criticised for over-simplifying and being easy to game.

Etymology 词源

Fred Reichheld 2003年提出——'net'指推荐者减贬损者的净值;因其单一问题的简洁而风行企业界。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ promoters minus detractors /prəˈmoʊtərz ˈmaɪnəs dɪˈtræktərz/
    Promoters minus detractors reduces loyalty to one blunt, comparable number.
  • ▸ the ultimate question /ðə ˈəltəmət kˈwɛʃən/
    Reichheld called 'would you recommend us?' the ultimate question.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ measure NPS
  • ▸ promoters and detractors
  • ▸ a world-class NPS
  • ▸ close the loop

In the wild 语境例句

A rising NPS often precedes a rising customer base — happy customers become an unpaid, credible sales force through referrals.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

NPS 批评:单一指标掩盖复杂真相,且易被员工'求高分'操纵;宜作趋势而非绝对值,并追问'为什么'的定性反馈。
