公司层战略 gōngsī céng zhànlüècorporate strategy

🔊 corporate strategy /ˈkɔrpərət ˈstrætəʤi/

回答“我们应经营哪些业务、如何管理这组业务”——区别于业务层战略（“某业务如何在其市场取胜”）。核心问题:多元化是否创造价值、总部如何为各业务增值、如何配置资本。

Answers 'which businesses should we be in, and how do we manage the set' — as distinct from business strategy ('how does this unit win in its market'). Its core questions: does diversification create value, how does the centre add value to units, and how is capital allocated.

Etymology 词源

corporate 源自拉丁 corpus（身体）——公司层战略即管理“企业整体这具身体”，而非单个器官。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the corporate centre /ðə ˈkɔrpərət ˈsɛntər/
    The corporate centre must earn its cost by adding value units couldn't create alone.
  • ▸ portfolio logic /pɔrtˈfoʊliˌoʊ ˈlɑʤɪk/
    Portfolio logic asks whether the whole is worth more than the sold-off parts.

In the wild 语境例句

Corporate strategy asks the ruthless question: would each business be worth more owned by someone else? If yes, the group is destroying value.
