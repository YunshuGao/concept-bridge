分部报告 fēnbù bàogàosegment reporting

🔊 segment reporting /ˌsɛgˈmɛnt rɪˈpɔrtɪŋ/

多元化企业须按业务或地区分部披露收入、利润与资产,揭开合并数字掩盖的内部结构。它让分析师看清哪块业务真正赚钱、哪块在拖累,是分部估值(SOTP)与识别'隐藏冠军/隐藏亏损'的数据来源。附注里的宝藏。

Diversified companies must disclose revenue, profit and assets by business or geographic segment, opening up the internal structure that consolidated figures conceal. It lets analysts see which unit truly earns and which drags, and is the data source for sum-of-the-parts valuation and spotting a 'hidden gem' or a 'hidden loss-maker'. A treasure in the footnotes.
