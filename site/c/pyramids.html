金字塔 jīnzìtǎthe pyramids

🔊 monument /ˈmɑnjəmənt/

also: the Great Pyramid of Giza · the pyramid of Khufu (Cheops)

⏳ 约前2600年

古埃及法老的角锥体陵墓，因侧面形似汉字“金”而得中文名；是古埃及文明的象征，反映了王权的强大与社会经济发展水平。最大者为胡夫金字塔（约建于公元前2600年）。

The monumental royal tombs of Egypt's pharaohs, of which the Great Pyramid of Khufu (c. 2600 BC) is the largest; symbols at once of Egyptian civilisation and of the absolute power that could command such labour.

Etymology 词源

monument 源自拉丁 monere（提醒）——纪念碑即“提醒之物”。

Synonyms 近义词

    A mausoleum is ambition housed for eternity.
  • ▸ megaproject /ˈmɛɡəˌprɒdʒɛkt/
    The megaproject employed the flood season's idle hands.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ build/erect the pyramids

  • ▸ pyramid-building

In the wild 语境例句

Every age gets the megaprojects its rulers can extract: the pyramids were less an engineering feat than a statement about who commanded Egypt's surplus.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

中文名源于字形“金”，纯属汉字联想；英语 pyramid 来自希腊语 pyramis。注意 pyramid scheme（传销/金字塔骗局）是完全不同的固定用法。教材点出“金字塔越修越小，反映王权衰落”——用英语表达即 the shrinking pyramids track the waning of royal power。
