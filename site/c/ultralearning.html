《超学习》（自学方法论） chāoxuéxíUltralearning

🔊 metalearning /ˈmɛtəˌlɜːnɪŋ/

⏳ 2019年

斯科特·扬（MIT四年课程一年自学完成者）的高强度自学框架九原则：元学习（先画学习地图）、专注、直接性（directness——在目标场景中学：学语言就去说）、反复操练弱点（drill）、提取练习（retrieval——测试即学习）、反馈、保持（间隔重复）、直觉（费曼技巧）、实验。对本项目的直接意义：用概念网络+双语对读学英语正是 directness 与 retrieval 的实践。

Scott Young's nine-principle framework for aggressive self-education (he speed-ran MIT's CS degree in a year): metalearning first (map the subject), focus, directness (learn in the context of use — speak the language, don't just study it), drill the weak sub-skills, retrieval over review, feedback, retention by spacing, intuition via the Feynman technique, experimentation. This knowledge base is itself an ultralearning artefact — directness and retrieval applied to Economist English.
