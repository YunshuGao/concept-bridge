委托代理问题 wěituō dàilǐ wèntíthe principal-agent (agency) problem

🔊 principal-agent problem /ˈprɪnsəpəl-ˈeɪʤənt ˈprɑbləm/

当一方（代理人,如经理人）代表另一方（委托人,如股东）行事,但两者利益不一致、且委托人无法完全监督代理人时产生的冲突。经理人可能追求规模、薪酬或安逸而非股东价值。公司治理与激励设计的核心问题。

The conflict that arises when one party (the agent, e.g. a manager) acts for another (the principal, e.g. shareholders), but their interests diverge and the principal cannot fully monitor the agent. Managers may chase size, pay or a quiet life over shareholder value. It is the central problem of corporate governance and incentive design.

Etymology 词源

agent 源自拉丁 agere（做、行动）——代理人即'代人行动者';principal 源自 princeps（首要者），即委托人。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the agency cost /ðə ˈeɪʤənsi kɔst/
    The agency cost is the value lost when the agent serves himself before the owner.
  • ▸ skin in the game /skɪn ɪn ðə geɪm/
    Skin in the game aligns the agent by making him a principal too.

In the wild 语境例句

Stock options try to solve the agency problem by making managers part-owners — so building shareholder value becomes building their own wealth.
