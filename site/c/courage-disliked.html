《被讨厌的勇气》（阿德勒心理学） bèi tǎoyàn de yǒngqìThe Courage to Be Disliked: Adlerian psychology

🔊 teleology /ˌtɛliˈɒlədʒi/

⏳ 2013年

岸见一郎以哲人—青年对话体普及阿德勒：目的论反因果论（不是过去决定你，而是你为当下目的“选用”过去——与弗洛伊德创伤决定论相对）；一切烦恼皆源于人际关系；课题分离（separation of tasks——分清谁的课题，不干涉他人课题、不让他人干涉你的）；被讨厌的勇气即自由的代价；共同体感觉与横向关系（鼓励而非表扬）。东亚现象级畅销，其词汇已入中文日常。

Kishimi's philosopher-and-youth dialogues made Adler East Asia's therapist: teleology over trauma (you employ your past for present purposes — the anti-Freud); all problems are interpersonal problems; the separation of tasks (whose problem is it? intervene in no one's, surrender yours to no one); freedom is the willingness to be disliked; community feeling and horizontal relationships (encourage, don't praise). Its vocabulary — 课题分离 above all — is now everyday Chinese.

Etymology 词源

teleology 源自希腊 telos（目的）+ logos——目的论：行为由所欲之未来牵引。
