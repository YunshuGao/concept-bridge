瓦特与蒸汽机 Wǎtè / zhēngqìjīWatt and the steam engine

🔊 steam power /stim paʊər/

⏳ 1785年–19世纪

早期蒸汽机仅用于矿井抽水；18世纪中期瓦特系列改进，1785年瓦特蒸汽机投入使用提供便利动力，化工、冶金、采矿相继采用，19世纪30年代成为主要动力来源——生产领域意义重大的飞跃，使工厂摆脱水力、可设于远离河流处，规模更大。

Steam began as a mine pump; Watt's improvements (in use from 1785) turned it into general-purpose power, adopted across chemicals, metallurgy and mining until, by the 1830s, steam was the prime mover — freeing factories from the riverbank and letting them grow wherever coal could reach.

Etymology 词源

steam 古英语 stēam（蒸汽）；engine 拉丁 ingenium（天赋、巧智）——引擎即“巧思之物”。

Economists call electricity and AI 'general-purpose technologies' by analogy with steam: inventions whose real product is every other industry's productivity.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

horsepower（马力）即瓦特为推销蒸汽机发明的营销单位；功率单位 watt 以他命名——一个人名同时进入物理学与日常语。习语：run out of steam（后劲不足）、let off steam（发泄）、full steam ahead（全速前进）——蒸汽时代的语言化石。
