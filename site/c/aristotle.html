亚里士多德 YàlǐshìduōdéAristotle

⏳ 前384年–前322年

柏拉图的学生、“百科全书式的学者”：解剖动物奠定动物学基础，创立逻辑学，提出演绎法等，成为科学研究的方法论基础；名言“吾爱吾师，吾更爱真理”。

Plato's student and antiquity's great polymath: founder of logic and pioneer of deductive method, dissector of animals and father of zoology — famously declaring that dear as Plato was, truth was dearer still.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ a polymath
  • ▸ deductive/inductive reasoning
  • ▸ formal logic
  • ▸ Plato is dear to me, but dearer still is truth

In the wild 语境例句

Aristotle would recognise a modern syllabus instantly — logic, biology, politics, rhetoric: he wrote the founding textbook for most of it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“百科全书式学者”= polymath（高频褒义词）。“演绎”deduction 与“归纳”induction 是英语学术写作的基本对立词——教材只提演绎，注意配对记忆。德谟克利特的“原子论”= atomism，其 atom 源自希腊语“不可分割”。
