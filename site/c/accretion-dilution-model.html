增厚稀释模型 zēnghòu xīshì móxíngthe accretion/dilution model

🔊 accretion dilution model /əˈkriːʃən dɪˈluʃən ˈmɑdəl/

并购中判断交易对收购方每股收益(EPS)即时影响的模型:合并双方净利润、加上协同、减去融资成本(新债利息或新股稀释),除以交易后总股数,与收购方独立EPS比较。用现金/债务融资通常增厚(利率低于收益率),用股票融资是否稀释取决于双方市盈率高低。并购谈判的快速财务筛。

The model judging a deal's immediate effect on the acquirer's EPS: combine both companies' net income, add synergies, subtract financing costs (interest on new debt or dilution from new shares), divide by post-deal shares, and compare with the acquirer's standalone EPS. Cash/debt financing is usually accretive (rates below the earnings yield); whether stock financing dilutes depends on the two firms' relative P/E ratios. A quick financial screen in M&A negotiation.

Etymology 词源

见 accretion-dilution 节点;'model'指在Excel中搭建合并EPS并比较的完整测算。

  • ▸ the merger consequences /ðə ˈmərʤər ˈkɑnsəkˌwɛnsəz/
