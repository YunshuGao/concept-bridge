并购后整合 bìnggòu hòu zhěnghépost-merger integration (PMI)

🔊 post-merger integration /poʊst-ˈmərʤər ˌɪnəˈgreɪʃən/

交易完成后把两家企业的组织、系统、文化、流程真正融合以兑现协同——这是并购价值创造或毁灭的真正战场。多数并购失败不在估值，而在整合:文化冲突、人才流失、执行拖延。

After the deal closes, genuinely combining two firms' organisations, systems, cultures and processes to realise the synergies — the real battlefield where deal value is made or destroyed. Most M&A fails not on price but on integration: culture clash, talent flight, delayed execution.

Etymology 词源

integration 见 vertical-integration；PMI 即“把买来的公司真正并入自身运转”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the first hundred days /ðə fərst ˈhənərd deɪz/
    The first hundred days set whether the merger becomes one company or two under one roof.
  • ▸ culture clash /ˈkəlʧər klæʃ/
    Culture clash quietly voids synergies that the model treated as guaranteed.
