🔊 data-informed practice /ˈdætə-ˌɪnˈfɔrmd ˈpræktɪs/

系统收集、分析并运用学习证据（评估结果、作品、观察、进度数据）来诊断需求、调整教学、评判影响、规划下一步。GTPA的'研判'实践与教师认证都要求教师是'数据素养'的:不凭感觉,而凭证据决策。关键词是'informed'（支持）而非'driven'（驱动）——数据辅助专业判断,不取代它。

Systematically gathering, analysing and using evidence of learning (assessment results, work samples, observations, progress data) to diagnose needs, adjust teaching, appraise impact and plan next steps. The GTPA's appraising practice and teacher accreditation both demand data-literate teachers: deciding on evidence, not hunch. The word is 'informed', not 'driven' — data supports professional judgement, it does not replace it.

Etymology 词源

data 拉丁'被给予之物'(dare 给);informed 源自 informare（塑形、告知）——数据'告知'并塑造教学决策。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ data literacy /ˈdætə ˈlɪtərəsi/
    Data literacy lets a teacher read a spreadsheet of results as a class's needs.
  • ▸ evidence-informed teaching /ˈɛvədəns-ˌɪnˈfɔrmd ˈtiʧɪŋ/
    Evidence-informed teaching decides by what worked, tempered by professional judgement.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ data-informed vs data-driven

  • ▸ identify the trend
  • ▸ plan the next step

In the wild 语境例句

Analysing which quiz questions the class failed — data-informed practice — tells you exactly which concept to reteach, and the GTPA wants that reasoning shown.
