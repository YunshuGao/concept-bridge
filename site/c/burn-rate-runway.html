🔊 burn rate and runway /bərn reɪt ənd ˈrʌnweɪ/

创业公司的生死指标:烧钱率是每月净消耗的现金,跑道是现有现金按当前烧钱率还能撑多少个月(现金÷月烧钱)。跑道见底而未融到下一轮或未实现盈利,公司即死。管理跑道是创业 CFO 的头等大事——现金不是利润,是氧气。

A startup's life-and-death metrics: burn rate is the net cash consumed per month, and runway is how many months the remaining cash lasts at that burn (cash ÷ monthly burn). When runway ends before the next round is raised or profitability reached, the company dies. Managing runway is the startup CFO's first job — cash isn't profit, it's oxygen.

Etymology 词源

burn（燃烧）——现金像燃料一样被'烧掉';runway（跑道）——飞机起飞前剩余的距离,喻资金耗尽前的时间。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ cash runway /kæʃ ˈrʌnweɪ/
    Cash runway is the number every founder checks before they check anything else.
  • ▸ months of runway /mənθs əv ˈrʌnweɪ/
    Months of runway is a countdown clock that only new revenue or new funding can reset.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ default alive vs default dead
  • ▸ months of cash left

In the wild 语境例句

A startup with 12 months of runway and no path to a raise must reach 'default alive' — cutting burn until revenue covers it — before the runway hits zero.
