“澳大利亚定约”的终结 àodàlìyà dìngyuēThe Australian Settlement (Paul Kelly)

🔊 arbitration /ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃən/

凯利《确定性的终结》提出的著名框架:立国的五根支柱——白澳政策、产业保护、劳资仲裁、国家家长主义、帝国庇护;1980年代霍克-基廷改革（浮动汇率、减关税、金融开放）将其逐一拆除,故曰“确定性的终结”。

Kelly's famous frame in The End of Certainty: Federation Australia rested on five pillars — White Australia, industry protection, wage arbitration, state paternalism, imperial benevolence. The Hawke–Keating reforms of the 1980s dismantled them; hence the end of certainty.

Etymology 词源

settlement 此处非“定居”而是“政治定约”(如1689年英国 settlement)——一词多义正是理解本框架的门槛。
