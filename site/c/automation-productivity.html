🔊 automation and productivity /ɔtəˈmeɪʃən ənd ˌproʊdəkˈtɪvɪti/

用机器、软件与 AI 替代或增强人力,以更少投入产出更多——生产率提升是长期财富增长的根本来源。它引发'技术性失业'的忧虑,但历史上自动化多改变而非消灭工作总量,创造新岗位。核心问题是过渡的痛苦与分配。

Using machines, software and AI to replace or augment human labour, producing more from less — productivity growth is the ultimate source of long-run wealth. It stokes fears of 'technological unemployment', but historically automation has changed rather than eliminated the total quantity of work, creating new jobs. The real issues are the pain of transition and the distribution of gains.

Etymology 词源

automation 源自希腊 automatos（自行运作的）——自动化即'让机器自行完成';productivity 即'单位投入的产出'。
