资本成本 zīběn chéngběnthe cost of capital

🔊 cost of capital /kɔst əv ˈkæpɪtəl/

资本提供者要求的最低回报率——债权人要利息，股东要与风险相称的回报。它既是投资项目的“门槛率”，也是贴现率。低于资本成本的回报即在摧毁价值。

The minimum return capital providers demand — lenders want interest, shareholders want a return matching the risk. It is both the hurdle rate for investments and the discount rate for valuation. Earning below the cost of capital destroys value.

Etymology 词源

capital 源自拉丁 caput（头→本金）——资本成本即“使用这笔本金的代价”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the hurdle rate /ðə ˈhɜːrdəl reɪt/
    The hurdle rate is the cost of capital wearing its decision-making hat.
  • ▸ required return /rikˈwaɪərd rɪˈtərn/
    The required return is what investors could earn elsewhere at equal risk.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ the opportunity cost of funds

In the wild 语境例句

A business earning 6% on capital that costs 9% is quietly destroying value every year, no matter how profitable it looks.
