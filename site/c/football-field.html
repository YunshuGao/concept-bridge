足球场估值图 zúqiúchǎng gūzhí túthe football field chart

🔊 football field chart /ˈfʊtˌbɔl fild ʧɑrt/

投行展示估值结论的经典图表:把DCF、可比公司、先例交易、52周股价等多种估值方法各自的价值区间,画成一排水平横条(形似足球场的场地线),让客户/董事会一眼看清各方法的估值范围与重叠区。它体现'估值是区间而非精确数字'的专业态度。

The classic chart investment banks use to present a valuation conclusion: the value ranges from several methods — DCF, comparable companies, precedent transactions, 52-week share price — drawn as a row of horizontal bars (resembling a football field's yard lines), letting a client or board see each method's range and where they overlap. It embodies the professional stance that valuation is a range, not a precise number.

Etymology 词源

football field（美式橄榄球场）——横条排列形似球场码线,故名;投行 pitch book 的标志性图表。
