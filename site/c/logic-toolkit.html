逻辑与思维（形式逻辑与推理） luóji yǔ sīwéithe logic toolkit: laws and inference

🔊 inference /ˈɪnfərəns/

⏳ 教材体系

形式逻辑基本规律：同一律（思维的确定性——概念判断保持同一，防偷换概念/论题）、矛盾律（一致性——互相矛盾的判断不能同真，防自相矛盾）、排中律（明确性——互相矛盾的判断不能同假，防骑墙居中）。概念（内涵外延；定义与划分）与判断。推理：演绎推理（必然推理——三段论、假言/选言推理，前提真+形式有效则结论必然真）、归纳推理（不完全归纳为或然——求同求异共变等穆勒五法提高可靠性）、类比推理（或然——启发发现）。辩证思维（整体性动态性；分析与综合）；创新思维（联想、发散与聚合、逆向思维、超前思维）。

Critical thinking with Chinese textbook rigour: the three classical laws (identity — no equivocation; non-contradiction; excluded middle — no fence-sitting), concepts with intension and extension, and the inference family — deduction's guarantee (valid form + true premises = necessarily true conclusion, syllogisms and conditionals included), induction's probability (Mill's methods to strengthen it), analogy's suggestiveness. Capped with dialectical and creative thinking (analysis/synthesis, divergent/convergent, reverse and anticipatory thinking) — in effect, the argumentative English survival kit: every fallacy name and inference verb an essay or leader column runs on.

Etymology 词源

inference 源自拉丁 inferre（携入）——推理即“把结论带进来”。

IELTS and university writing rubrics grade exactly this chapter — 'develop a coherent argument, avoid logical fallacies'; the Chinese logic textbook and the western style guide turn out to be teaching the same skill.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

同一律/矛盾律/排中律 = the laws of identity / non-contradiction / excluded middle（亚里士多德——链 [[aristotle]] 收环）。“偷换概念”= equivocation；“自相矛盾”= self-contradiction（韩非子矛盾典故是中文“矛盾”一词本源——链 [[legalism]]、[[dialectics]] 的 contradiction 三义辨析：逻辑矛盾/辩证矛盾/成语矛盾——本知识库最精微的一组区分）。valid（有效）vs sound（可靠）为逻辑英语关键对。
