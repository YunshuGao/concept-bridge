井冈山道路（工农武装割据） Jǐnggāngshān dàolùthe Jinggangshan road: encircling the cities from the countryside

⏳ 1927年–1931年

国民革命失败后，1927年8月1日南昌起义（周恩来贺龙叶挺朱德刘伯承）打响武装反抗国民党反动统治第一枪；八七会议确定土地革命和武装反抗总方针、毛泽东提出“政权是由枪杆子中取得”；秋收起义受挫后毛泽东率部向敌人统治力量薄弱的农村进军，创建第一个农村革命根据地井冈山根据地；1928年朱毛会师成立工农革命军第四军。开展土地革命和游击战争，创造“工农武装割据”局面——农村包围城市、武装夺取政权的井冈山道路，是马克思主义基本原理同中国具体实际相结合的光辉典范。1931年成立中华苏维埃共和国临时中央政府（瑞金）。

After 1927's massacres the party learned Mao's aphorism the hard way — 'political power grows out of the barrel of a gun'. The Nanchang rising of 1 August (still the army's birthday) fired the first shot; when attacks on cities failed, Mao marched the survivors of the Autumn Harvest rising into the Jinggang mountains and invented the model instead: a rural base area, land revolution to win the peasants, guerrilla war to keep it — 'encircling the cities from the countryside', Marxism bent to Chinese reality, the textbook's exemplar of adapting theory to fact. Zhu De's forces joined in 1928; a Soviet Republic followed at Ruijin in 1931.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“枪杆子里面出政权”= political power grows out of the barrel of a gun（毛语录最全球化的一句）。“农村包围城市”= encircling the cities from the countryside。根据地 = base areas；游击战 = guerrilla warfare（西语借词，与毛派战略在英语中深度绑定）。马克思主义中国化 = the sinicisation of Marxism——与 [[xiaowen-reforms]] 的 sinicisation 同词异史。
