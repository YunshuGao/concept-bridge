🔊 Code of Ethics /koʊd əv ˈɛθɪks/

CFA协会的《道德准则与职业行为标准》——贯穿三级、权重最高的支柱。核心:客户利益至上、诚信、独立客观、勤勉、信息披露、不利用重大非公开信息(内幕交易)、公平对待客户。金融的信任基础比任何估值技巧都根本。

The CFA Institute's Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct — the pillar that runs through all three levels and carries the heaviest weight. Its core: client interests first, integrity, independence and objectivity, diligence, disclosure, no trading on material non-public information (insider trading), and fair dealing. The trust foundation of finance, more fundamental than any valuation technique.

ethics 源自希腊 ethos（品性、习俗）——职业道德即从业者的'品性规范';CFA把它列为第一科目,分量最重。

Nuance 陷阱与辨析
