🔊 the five practices of the GTPA /ðə faɪv ˈpræktɪsɪz əv ðə ˌdʒiːtiːpiːˈeɪ/

GTPA 教学循环的五个相互关联的阶段:①计划（planning,依据课程与学情设计对齐的教学）;②教学（teaching,实施并因应学生调整）;③评估（assessing,收集学习证据并作判断）;④反思（reflecting,批判性检视教学决策）;⑤研判（appraising,用数据分析教学对学习的影响并规划下一步）。第五项'研判'最能区分优秀与合格。

The five interrelated stages of the GTPA teaching cycle: (1) planning — designing aligned teaching from the curriculum and knowledge of students; (2) teaching — enacting and adjusting responsively; (3) assessing — gathering evidence of learning and making judgements; (4) reflecting — critically examining teaching decisions; (5) appraising — using data to analyse the impact of teaching on learning and plan next steps. The fifth, appraising, is what separates the strong from the merely adequate.

Etymology 词源

appraise 源自古法语 aprisier（定价、评估,拉丁 pretium 价格）——'研判'即为教学的成效'定价'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the teaching cycle /ðə ˈtiʧɪŋ ˈsaɪkəl/

  • ▸ appraising impact /əˈpreɪzɪŋ ˌɪmˈpækt/
    Appraising impact is where a teacher proves the learning happened, not just the teaching.
