审计意见 shěnjì yìjiànthe auditor's opinion

🔊 auditor's opinion /ˈɔdɪtərz əˈpɪnjən/

独立审计师对财报是否'公允反映'企业状况的正式判断:无保留意见(干净,标准)、保留意见、否定意见、无法表示意见。尤其关键的是'持续经营'警示(going concern)——审计师质疑公司能否活过一年。审计意见是财报可信度的第一道外部背书。

The independent auditor's formal judgement on whether the statements 'fairly present' the company's position: unqualified (clean, standard), qualified, adverse, or a disclaimer. Especially critical is a going-concern warning — the auditor doubting the company can survive the next year. The audit opinion is the first external endorsement of a report's credibility.

Etymology 词源

audit 源自拉丁 audire（听）——审计本义'听账'(古罗马查账靠朗读);opinion 即审计师的正式'意见'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ a going-concern warning /ə goʊɪŋ-kənˈsərn ˈwɔrnɪŋ/
    A going-concern warning is the auditor quietly questioning whether the company lives to next year.
  • ▸ an unqualified opinion /ən ənkˈwɑlɪˌfaɪd əˈpɪnjən/
    An unqualified opinion is clean; a qualified one is a caveat you must chase down.

In the wild 语境例句

Buried after strong results, a going-concern qualification told the real story: the auditor doubted the company could refinance its debt and survive the year.
