能力驱动战略 nénglì qūdòng zhànlüècapabilities-driven strategy

🔊 capabilities-driven strategy /ˌkeɪpəˈbɪlətiz-ˈdrɪvən ˈstrætəʤi/

战略应建立在少数几项相互强化、世界级的差异化能力之上，且所有业务都受益于同一套能力体系（“取胜之道”）。与其追逐市场机会，不如让机会匹配你独有的能力系统。

Strategy should rest on a few reinforcing, world-class differentiating capabilities that all the businesses draw on (a coherent 'way to play'). Rather than chasing market opportunities, fit opportunities to the distinctive capability system you already own.

Etymology 词源

capability 源自拉丁 capere（抓取、容纳）——能力即“能抓住做成之事”；能力驱动即让战略从“我们擅长什么”出发。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the way to play /ðə weɪ tɪ pleɪ/
    The way to play is how you create value; the capabilities make it real.
  • ▸ coherence premium /koʊˈhɪrəns ˈprimiəm/
    The coherence premium rewards firms whose businesses share one capability system.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ a coherent capability system
  • ▸ the right to win
  • ▸ fit vs stretch
  • ▸ the coherence premium

In the wild 语境例句

IKEA's low-cost design, flat-pack logistics and store experience are one reinforcing capability system — each business decision draws on the same source of advantage.
