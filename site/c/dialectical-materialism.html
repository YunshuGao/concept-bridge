辩证唯物主义（物质与意识） biànzhèng wéiwùzhǔyìdialectical materialism: matter and consciousness

🔊 materialism /məˈtɪriəˌlɪzəm/

⏳ 教材体系

哲学基本问题是思维和存在的关系问题（何者为本原——唯物/唯心；有无同一性——可知论/不可知论）。辩证唯物主义：世界的本质是物质（物质决定意识，意识是客观存在的反映、对物质具有能动的反作用）；运动是物质的固有属性，规律是客观的（不以人的意志为转移——尊重规律与发挥主观能动性相结合，一切从实际出发、实事求是）。实践是认识的基础（来源、动力、检验标准、目的），认识经实践—认识—再实践循环上升——“实践是检验真理的唯一标准”。

The official philosophy's foundations, taught as a system: philosophy's basic question (thinking v. being — materialism against idealism, knowability against agnosticism); matter as primary with consciousness its active reflection; law-governed motion independent of will ('proceed from reality, seek truth from facts'); and practice as knowledge's source, engine, test and purpose — the epistemology whose one-line version, 'practice is the sole criterion of truth', relaunched China in 1978. Marxist philosophical English (base, superstructure, negation) remains live currency in western theory too — this node is the shared dictionary.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

唯物/唯心主义 = materialism/idealism（哲学义，与日常英语 materialistic 拜金、idealistic 理想化的落差——高频误读警告）。主观能动性 = subjective initiative/agency。实事求是链 [[third-plenum]]。“物质”哲学义 = matter（非 material 财物）。
