🔊 the rule of forty /ðə rul əv ˈfɔrti/

评估软件/SaaS 公司健康度的经验法则:营收增长率加利润率(如自由现金流率)之和应达到或超过40%。它量化了增长与盈利的平衡——高增长可暂时容忍低利润,成熟公司则须靠利润补足。低于40%常预示商业模式或效率有问题。

A rule of thumb for gauging a software/SaaS company's health: revenue growth rate plus profit margin (e.g. free-cash-flow margin) should meet or exceed 40%. It quantifies the balance of growth and profitability — rapid growth can excuse thin margins for a while, while a mature firm must make it up in profit. Below 40% often signals a flawed model or poor efficiency.

Etymology 词源

'40'为经验阈值——增长率与利润率之和的黄金线;成长股投资者与 SaaS 高管的通用速判。
