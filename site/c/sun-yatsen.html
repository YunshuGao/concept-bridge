孙中山与三民主义 Sūn Zhōngshān / Sānmín zhǔyìSun Yat-sen and the Three Principles of the People

🔊 republicanism /rɪˈpʌblɪkənɪzəm/

⏳ 1894年–1905年

革命先行者孙中山早年上书李鸿章改革碰壁后走上革命道路：1894年檀香山创兴中会（“振兴中华”），1895年广州起义失败流亡海外。1905年在东京联合兴中会、华兴会、光复会成立中国同盟会——第一个全国规模的、统一的资产阶级革命政党，纲领“驱除鞑虏，恢复中华，创立民国，平均地权”，机关报《民报》发刊词将其阐发为“民族、民权、民生”三大主义——三民主义成为孙中山领导资产阶级革命的指导思想。

Sun Yat-sen — mission-schooled, medically trained, radicalised when reform petitions went unread — founded the Revive China Society in Honolulu (1894) and spent a decade in exile organising, funding and failing at risings. In Tokyo in 1905 he fused the revolutionary groups into the Chinese United League (Tongmenghui), China's first nationwide revolutionary party, whose programme he distilled into the Three Principles of the People: nationalism, democracy, and the people's livelihood — the ideological operating system of the coming republic, claimed to this day by parties on both sides of the strait.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

孙中山英语通行 Sun Yat-sen（粤拼“逸仙”）。三民主义 = the Three Principles of the People；“民生”译 the people's livelihood（孙自注可对应 socialism——思想史细节）。其名言“民有民治民享”即林肯句的移植——链 [[lincoln]] 的回路闭合。
