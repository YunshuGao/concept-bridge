🔊 time value of money /taɪm ˈvælju əv ˈməni/

金融第一原理:今天的一元比未来的一元更值钱——因可投资生息、有通胀与风险。一切估值都是把未来现金流按贴现率折回今天。掌握它，才理解利率、投资与估值的全部逻辑。

Finance's first principle: a dollar today is worth more than a dollar tomorrow — it can be invested to earn a return, and inflation and risk erode the future. All valuation is discounting future cash flows back to today. Grasp it and interest, investing and valuation all follow.

Etymology 词源

源自复利数学；present value（现值）与 future value（终值）是其两端，贴现率是连接二者的桥。

Synonyms 近义词

    Discounting is compounding run in reverse.

In the wild 语境例句

A promised million in ten years is worth far less today — the time value of money is why 'I'll pay you later' always costs something.
