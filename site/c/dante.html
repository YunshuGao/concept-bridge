但丁与《神曲》 Dàndīng / ShénqǔDante and the Divine Comedy

⏳ 1265年–1321年

文艺复兴的先驱，与彼特拉克、薄伽丘并称“文学三杰”。《神曲》分地狱、炼狱、天堂三部，借梦游三界描写现实人物，抨击教会贪婪腐化，把主教僧侣甚至教皇打入地狱，表达市民阶层的情感与理想。名言：“走自己的路，让别人说去吧！”

Forerunner of the Renaissance and first of its 'three crowns' of letters (with Petrarch and Boccaccio). His Divine Comedy tours Hell, Purgatory and Paradise, peopling them with his contemporaries — greedy prelates and even popes consigned to the Inferno — the ambitions and grievances of the urban classes in verse.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

the nine circles of hell（九层地狱）已是英语现成比喻；Dantesque 形容炼狱般的景象。“炼狱”= purgatory，亦常作比喻（辛苦煎熬的过渡状态：contract purgatory）。“文学三杰/美术三杰”可译 the three crowns / the trinity of Renaissance art（非固定名，需解释）。
