盛唐气象 shèng-Táng qìxiàngHigh Tang splendour

🔊 cosmopolitan /ˌkɑzməˈpɑlətən/

⏳ 7–8世纪

唐前期百余年经济迅速发展：曲辕犁筒车发明推广、广修水利；蜀锦冠全国，越窑青瓷邢窑白瓷唐三彩；商业繁荣水陆通达——长安规模宏伟布局严整（东西两市、百余坊、人口百万），国内各族与外国商人使者艺人云集，是国际性大都会。社会风气开放兼容并包：妇女受文学音乐教育、骑马打球射箭，衣食住行多受西北民族影响，尚武风气盛行。民族交往交融：太宗击败东西突厥、行开明民族政策，被尊“天可汗”；文成公主入藏嫁松赞干布（带去蚕种技术书籍，唐蕃“和同为一家”、会盟碑存大昭寺）；设安西北庭都护府辖天山南北。

A century of boom: the curved-shaft plough and waterwheel, brocades and tricolour ceramics, and Chang'an — a walled grid of a million people, two great markets and a hundred wards, the largest and most international city on earth, thronged with Turks, Persians, Koreans and Japanese. Society wore the openness: women schooled, riding and playing polo; fashion, food and music happily 'barbarian'. Abroad, Taizong crushed the Turkic khaganates yet was hailed by the steppe as 'Heavenly Khagan'; Princess Wencheng's marriage carried silk, seeds and books to Songtsen Gampo's Tibet ('one family', says the treaty stone still standing before the Jokhang); protectorates at Anxi and Beiting governed the Tarim.

Etymology 词源

cosmopolitan 源自希腊 kosmos（世界）+ politēs（公民）——“世界公民的”，长安即其城市形态。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ cosmopolitanism /ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtənɪzəm/
    Tang cosmopolitanism wore foreign fashion without anxiety.
  • ▸ openness /ˈoʊpənəs/
    Openness was the empire's confidence made visible.

In the wild 语境例句

'Tang openness' is modern China's favourite self-image when arguing that greatness and openness rise together — every expo speech about 开放包容 has Chang'an's two markets behind it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“天可汗”= the Heavenly Khagan（游牧与农耕双重合法性的独特称号——比较 [[charlemagne]] 的双重加冕）。“和亲”= marriage alliance/heqin diplomacy。文成公主与唐蕃会盟碑是汉藏关系论述的核心史料，英语表述 the Tang-Tibetan alliance stele。唐三彩 = Tang sancai (tricolour) ware。
