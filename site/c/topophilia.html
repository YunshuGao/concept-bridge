段义孚：恋地情结 liàndì qíngjiéYi-Fu Tuan: topophilia, space and place

🔊 topophilia /ˌtɒpəˈfɪliə/

华裔人文地理学宗师段义孚:恋地情结（topophilia,人与地方的情感纽带）;空间（space,自由与开放）与地方（place,安全与依附）的辩证——“空间+经验=地方”。人文主义地理学的奠基词汇。

Yi-Fu Tuan founded humanistic geography's vocabulary: topophilia, the affective bond between people and place; and the dialectic of space (freedom, openness) and place (security, attachment) — space becomes place as we know it and endow it with value.

Etymology 词源

topophilia = 希腊 topos（地方）+ philia（爱）——段义孚自造词;topic、topology、utopia 同根:话题本义“处所”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ place attachment /pleɪs əˈtætʃmənt/
    Place attachment is why demolition notices read like bereavements.
