经济学十大原理（曼昆） jīngjìxué shídà yuánlǐMankiw's ten principles of economics

🔊 opportunity cost /ˌɑpərˈtunəti kɔst/

⏳ 1998年（教材）

全球最流行经济学入门教材的开篇框架：人们面临权衡取舍（trade-offs）；机会成本（某物的成本是为得到它所放弃的东西）；理性人考虑边际量（marginal thinking）；人们会对激励作出反应（incentives）；贸易能使每个人状况更好（比较优势）；市场通常是组织经济活动的好方法（看不见的手）；政府有时能改善市场结果（市场失灵：外部性/市场势力）；生产率决定生活水平；货币超发引起通胀；社会面临通胀与失业的短期权衡（菲利普斯曲线）。

The opening framework of the world's bestselling econ textbook — ten principles that double as the Economist's house grammar: trade-offs everywhere; opportunity cost ('what you give up to get it'); thinking at the margin; people respond to incentives; trade makes everyone better off (comparative advantage); markets usually organise activity well; governments can sometimes improve outcomes (market failure); productivity determines living standards; printing money causes inflation; and the short-run inflation-unemployment trade-off. Learn these ten and half of financial journalism parses itself.

opportunity 源自拉丁 ob portum（顺风向港）——机会本义“顺风”；cost 拉丁 constare（合计为）。

In the wild 语境例句

TANSTAAFL — 'there ain't no such thing as a free lunch' — is principle one in slang form; 天下没有免费的午餐 crossed into Chinese so completely that both languages now cite it at each other.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

机会成本 = opportunity cost；边际 = marginal（边际革命 the marginal revolution 为经济思想史节点）；比较优势 = comparative advantage（李嘉图——[[adam-smith]] 的续章）。本节点为 Economist 阅读的最小经济学装备。
