🔊 syllabus outcomes /ˈsɪləbəs ˈaʊtˌkəmz/

NESA课程大纲以'学习成果'（outcomes,常带编码如CT5-DAT-01）陈述学生学完某内容后应能做到什么。它是所有计划、教学与评估的法定起点与对标点——澳洲教学要求'成果导向',每份教案、每项评估都须明确对应具体大纲成果编码。

NESA syllabuses state what students should be able to do after learning, as outcomes (often coded, e.g. CT5-DAT-01). They are the statutory starting point and reference for all planning, teaching and assessment — Australian teaching is outcomes-based, and every lesson plan and assessment must map to specific syllabus outcome codes.

Etymology 词源

syllabus 源自希腊误抄的拉丁 sittybas（书标签）——大纲即'内容的标签清单';outcome 即'学习产出的结果'。

  • ▸ the outcome codes /ðə ˈaʊtˌkəm koʊdz/

A Year 8 coding lesson names its outcome — 'designs, produces and evaluates algorithms' — so the plan, the task and the feedback all aim at the same coded target.
