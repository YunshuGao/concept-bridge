SCQA 叙事结构 SCQA xùshì jiégòuthe SCQA framework

🔊 SCQA /ˌɛssiːkjuːˈeɪ/

明托金字塔原理中的开场结构:情境(Situation,共识背景)→冲突(Complication,打破平衡的变化)→问题(Question,由此自然引出的疑问)→答案(Answer,即你的核心建议)。它把任何汇报的开头变成一个抓人的故事,让听众自己'问出'你要回答的问题。

The opening structure within Minto's pyramid principle: Situation (agreed context) → Complication (a change that upsets the balance) → Question (the query it naturally raises) → Answer (your core recommendation). It turns any presentation's opening into a gripping story, making the audience themselves 'ask' the question you came to answer.

SCQA opens a deck with agreement ('we lead the market'), tension ('but growth is stalling'), the question that raises ('so how do we reignite it?'), then your answer.
