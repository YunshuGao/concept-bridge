安·兰德与客观主义 Ān LándéAyn Rand and Objectivism

🔊 objectivism /əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪzəm/

⏳ 1943年/1957年

《阿特拉斯耸耸肩》《源泉》：兰德的客观主义哲学小说——理性自利（rational self-interest）为最高道德，创造者/生产者是文明的阿特拉斯（“耸肩”即罢工——约翰·高尔特带走头脑），利他主义被斥为道德勒索，资本主义是唯一道德的制度。美国右翼与硅谷的地下圣经（格林斯潘出身兰德圈），学院哲学多不认，文化影响极巨——“Who is John Galt?”是英语政治梗。

Atlas Shrugged and The Fountainhead are philosophy delivered as blockbuster: Objectivism's rational self-interest as the moral summit, creators as civilisation's Atlas (the 'shrug' is their strike — John Galt withdraws the minds), altruism unmasked as moral extortion, capitalism the only moral system. Academia shrugs back, but the cultural footprint is enormous — Greenspan came from Rand's circle, Silicon Valley quotes her sideways, and 'Who is John Galt?' remains a live political meme.
