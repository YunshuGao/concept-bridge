中华优秀传统文化的内涵与特点 Zhōnghuá yōuxiù chuántǒng wénhuàthe core ideas of Chinese traditional culture

🔊 harmony /ˈhɑrməni/

⏳ 先秦至今

内涵：重视以人为本（周公“敬天保民”、孔子“仁者爱人”），政治伦理上即民本思想（管子顺民意、孟子“民为贵，社稷次之，君为轻”）；崇尚天人合一、道法自然（老子；荀子“制天命而用之”——人与自然和谐）；提倡爱国、追求家国情怀（“修身齐家治国平天下”；张载四句；范仲淹先忧后乐；顾炎武“天下兴亡，匹夫有责”）；崇德尚贤、天下为公（“为政以德”“选贤与能”）；崇尚自强不息、厚德载物（《周易》）；主张和而不同（“和实生物，同则不继”“和为贵”）。特点：本土性、多样性、包容性（兼收并蓄）、凝聚性、连续性。发展脉络：先秦奠基—汉尊儒术—儒佛道交融—宋明理学（朱熹格物致知；陆王心学知行合一）—明清批判（黄宗羲顾炎武王夫之经世致用）—近代救亡转型。取其精华去其糟粕、古为今用推陈出新。

The official anatomy of the tradition, formulation by formulation: humanity as the root (ren, 'the people are noblest, the ruler lightest'); heaven-and-humanity as one (harmony with nature's way, not conquest of it); family-state devotion ('cultivate self, order family, govern state, pacify all under heaven' — down to 'every commoner shares responsibility for the world's rise and fall'); virtue and worthiness exalted, 'all under heaven as public trust'; ceaseless self-strengthening bearing all things (the Zhouyi's twinned mottoes); and harmony-without-uniformity as the method of difference. Its listed characters — indigenous origin, diversity, inclusiveness, cohesion, continuity — and its long arc from pre-Qin foundation through Neo-Confucian synthesis to modern reinvention are the frame within which 'cultural confidence' speaks today.

Etymology 词源

harmony 源自希腊 harmonia（接合、协和——木工与音乐同词）——“和”的西方对应亦是工匠词。

Synonyms 近义词

    Chinese humanism centres the people without dethroning heaven.

    A syncretic tradition digests its rivals into chapters.

In the wild 语境例句

When 天人合一 appears in a climate speech or 和而不同 at a diplomatic forum, this chapter is the source code — official English renders them 'harmony between humanity and nature' and 'harmony in diversity', and the citations are doing policy work.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

官方译语集：以人为本 = people-centred/people first；民本 = people-as-foundation（区别于 democracy——民本 vs 民主的概念史是中西政治思想比较的枢纽，链 [[tang-golden-age]] 舟水之喻、[[declaration-of-independence]]）；天人合一 = the unity of heaven and humanity；家国情怀 = family-state sentiment/patriotic devotion；和而不同 = harmony without uniformity；文化自信 = cultural confidence。理学 = Neo-Confucianism（朱熹 Zhu Xi；心学 the School of Mind，王阳明 Wang Yangming——日韩及当代管理学的活传统）。
