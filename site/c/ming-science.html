明代科技名著与西学东渐 Míngdài kējì míngzhùMing science: the great compendia and the Jesuit exchange

🔊 compendium /kəmˈpɛndiəm/

⏳ 明代中后期

李时珍27年编成《本草纲目》：药物1800多种、药方11000多个，总结古代药物学成就，17世纪初传日朝、后译拉法俄德英文。宋应星《天工开物》分18类涵盖农工各部门、插图120余幅（“贵五谷而贱金玉”），被誉“中国17世纪的工艺百科全书”。徐光启《农政全书》60卷总结古代农学。徐光启与意大利传教士利玛窦合译古希腊《几何原本》——“几何”“点线三角形”等术语由此定名。徐霞客30余年游历成《徐霞客游记》，丹霞地貌考察居世界先进水平。

Late Ming scholarship built monuments: Li Shizhen's Compendium of Materia Medica (27 years, 1,800 drugs, 11,000 prescriptions — translated eventually into Latin and five European tongues), Song Yingxing's Exploitation of the Works of Nature ('grain before jade' — the illustrated encyclopaedia of every Chinese craft, dubbed China's 17th-century technical encyclopaedia), Xu Guangqi's Complete Treatise on Agriculture. And the traffic ran both ways: Xu and the Jesuit Matteo Ricci translated Euclid, coining the Chinese words for geometry, point, line and triangle that every student still uses; the wandering Xu Xiake, meanwhile, described karst landforms centuries ahead of the field.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

《本草纲目》= Compendium of Materia Medica；《天工开物》= The Exploitation of the Works of Nature（任以都译名）。“西学东渐”= the eastward spread of Western learning。利玛窦 = Matteo Ricci——中西交流史的头号人名，Economist 谈中西相遇必引。这批总结性巨著与 [[song-yuan-inventions]] 的原创性对照，是李约瑟问题的又一素材。
