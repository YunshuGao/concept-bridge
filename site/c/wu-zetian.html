武则天 Wǔ ZétiānWu Zetian

🔊 empress regnant /ˈɛmprɛs ˈrɛɡnənt/

⏳ 690年–705年

唐高宗皇后，多谋善断，共掌朝政；高宗死后废两子自立，改国号为周——中国历史上唯一的女皇帝。打击敌对官僚贵族；大力发展科举、创殿试亲自面试考生、不拘一格选才（读骆宾王讨己檄文而惜其才）；继续减轻民负重视生产。在位期间社会经济持续发展、边疆巩固开拓，为“开元盛世”奠基。

Gaozong's empress and co-ruler, then — having deposed two of her own sons — emperor in her own right and her own dynasty's name: the only woman ever to rule China as sovereign. She broke the old aristocratic families, expanded the examinations and invented the palace test (admiring even the author of a manifesto against her: 'that such talent went unused is the ministers' fault'), and kept the economy growing and the frontiers firm — the bridge between Taizong's and Xuanzong's golden ages.

Etymology 词源

empress 为 emperor 的阴性（拉丁 imperatrix）；regnant 拉丁 regnare（在位统治）——“在位女皇”非皇后。

Wu Zetian is the case study in gendered historiography: a competent reign vilified for a millennium as usurpation — every modern reassessment of a powerful woman cites her file.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

英语须区分 empress consort（皇后）与 empress regnant（女皇）——武则天是中国唯一的后者。“垂帘听政”= ruling from behind the curtain（后世慈禧亦用此词）。她的无字碑（the wordless stele）是“让后人评说”的著名意象。
