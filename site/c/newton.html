牛顿 NiúdùnIsaac Newton

⏳ 1643年–1727年

近代自然科学奠基人之一：万有引力定律、光学分析和微积分学为三大成就；1687年《自然哲学的数学原理》出版，物理学成为独立学科，人类对客观世界的探索向前迈进一大步。

Co-founder of modern science: universal gravitation, the analysis of light and the calculus are his triple crown, and the Principia (1687) made physics a discipline — nature reduced, for the first time, to law.
