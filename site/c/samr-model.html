🔊 the SAMR model /ðə ˈsæmər ˈmɑdəl/

评估课堂技术整合深度的四级模型:替代（Substitution,技术只替代旧工具无功能改变,如用平板读PDF代替纸)、增强（Augmentation,有功能改进)、修改（Modification,技术使任务被显著重新设计)、重塑（Redefinition,技术使原本不可能的任务成为可能)。前两级'增强'现有教学,后两级'转化'教学。目标是走向转化而非停留在替代。

A four-level model for gauging the depth of classroom technology integration: Substitution (tech replaces an old tool with no functional change, e.g. reading a PDF on a tablet instead of paper), Augmentation (a functional improvement), Modification (tech significantly redesigns the task), Redefinition (tech makes possible what was previously impossible). The first two enhance existing teaching, the latter two transform it. The aim is to move toward transformation, not linger at substitution.

Etymology 词源

SAMR = Substitution/Augmentation/Modification/Redefinition 首字母缩略——Ruben Puentedura 提出。
