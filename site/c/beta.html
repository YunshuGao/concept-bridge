🔊 beta /ˈbeɪtə/

衡量个股回报相对整体市场波动的敏感度:β=1 随市场同步，β>1 波动更大（更激进），β<1 更稳。它是 CAPM 中唯一被定价的风险——代表无法通过分散消除的系统性风险。

A measure of a stock's return sensitivity to the overall market: beta 1 moves with the market, above 1 swings more (aggressive), below 1 is steadier. It is the only risk CAPM prices — representing the systematic risk that diversification cannot remove.

Etymology 词源

希腊字母 β——统计回归中市场因子的斜率系数，金融借用以名“市场敏感度”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ market sensitivity /ˈmɑrkɪt ˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/
    Market sensitivity tells you how a stock trembles when the index shakes.
  • ▸ systematic risk /ˌsɪstəˈmætɪk rɪsk/
    Systematic risk is the part of volatility no diversification can launder away.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

In a downturn, high-beta cyclicals fall hardest while low-beta utilities hold up — beta is the market's amplification factor.
