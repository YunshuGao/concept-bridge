🔊 war for talent /wɔr fər ˈtælənt/

麦肯锡1997年提出:在知识经济中,顶尖人才是最稀缺、最具决定性的资源,吸引、发展与留住他们是核心竞争优势的来源。少数关键岗位上的卓越者与平庸者产出可差数倍——人才不是成本,是资本。

A McKinsey coinage (1997): in the knowledge economy, top talent is the scarcest and most decisive resource, and attracting, developing and retaining it is a source of core competitive advantage. In a few pivotal roles, an outstanding performer can out-produce an average one many times over — talent is not a cost but capital.
