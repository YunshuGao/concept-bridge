资产负债表 zīchǎn fùzhài biǎothe balance sheet

🔊 balance sheet /ˈbæləns ʃit/

三大报表之一:某一时点的存量快照，恒等式为资产=负债+所有者权益。左边是企业拥有什么，右边是这些资产由谁出资（债权人与股东）。它揭示财务实力、杠杆与偿债能力。

One of the three statements: a point-in-time snapshot governed by the identity assets = liabilities + equity. The left shows what the firm owns; the right shows who financed it (lenders and shareholders). It reveals financial strength, leverage and solvency.

Etymology 词源

balance（平衡）——两边必然相等（资产=负债+权益），故名“平衡表”；sheet 即一张表。

  • ▸ assets equals liabilities plus equity /ˈæˌsɛts ˈikwəlz ˌlaɪəˈbɪlɪtiz pləs ˈɛkwəti/

A fortress balance sheet — low debt, ample cash — let the firm buy rivals cheaply in the downturn while over-leveraged competitors folded.
