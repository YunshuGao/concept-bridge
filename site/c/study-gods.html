《学神》 xuéshénStudy Gods: the elite student taxonomy

🔊 ethnography /ɛθˈnɒɡrəfi/

姜以琳对北京顶尖中学的民族志:学神（轻松高分）、学霸（苦读高分）、学渣（不学低分）、学弱（苦读低分）——地位由“成绩÷努力”决定,毫不费力是最高贵。精英再生产的中国剧本,英文学界的关键新词。

Yi-Lin Chiang's ethnography of elite Beijing schools: study gods (effortless top scores), studyholics (effortful top scores), underachievers and losers — status equals results divided by visible effort; ease is nobility. China's script of elite reproduction, now anglophone sociology's vocabulary.
