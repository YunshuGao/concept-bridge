🔊 synergies /ˈsɪnərʤiz/

并购后合并实体创造的、超过两家独立之和的额外价值:成本协同（削减重复、规模采购，较可靠）与收入协同（交叉销售、扩大市场，较虚幻）。收购溢价的正当性全系于协同能否真正兑现。

The extra value the combined entity creates beyond the two firms standalone: cost synergies (cutting duplication, scale procurement — more reliable) and revenue synergies (cross-selling, wider reach — more speculative). The entire justification for a takeover premium hinges on whether synergies actually materialise.

Etymology 词源

synergy 源自希腊 synergos（共同工作，syn 一起 + ergon 功）——协同即“合力大于分力之和”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ cost synergies /kɔst ˈsɪnərʤiz/
    Cost synergies are bankable; revenue synergies are a hope with a spreadsheet.
  • ▸ one plus one equals three /wən pləs wən ˈikwəlz θri/
    'One plus one equals three' is the pitch; integration decides whether it's really 1.8.
