阿拉伯帝国 Ālābó dìguóthe Arab caliphate

also: the Arab Empire · the Caliphate

⏳ 632年–1258年

穆罕默德逝世后，继承者“哈里发”继续扩张：东灭波斯、751年败唐将高仙芝控制中亚，西克埃及横扫北非、占西班牙，至8世纪中期成为地跨亚欧非、当时世界最大的帝国，伊斯兰教随之广泛传播。哈里发集政军神权于一身。9世纪中叶起内讧分裂，1258年蒙古人陷巴格达，帝国灭亡。

Under the caliphs — Muhammad's successors — Arab arms destroyed Persia, checked Tang China at Talas (751), swept North Africa and took Spain, making the caliphate by 750 the largest empire on earth and carrying Islam with it; the caliph fused political, military and religious supremacy. Riven by dissension from the 9th century, it ended when the Mongols sacked Baghdad in 1258.
