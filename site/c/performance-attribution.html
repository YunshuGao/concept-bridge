业绩归因 yèjì guīyīnperformance attribution

🔊 performance attribution /pərˈfɔrməns ˌætrɪˈbjuʃən/

拆解投资组合相对基准的超额收益从何而来:配置效应(在对的资产类别上超配/低配)、选择效应(在类别内选对了证券)、以及交互与择时效应。它回答'我的回报是靠运气、择时、选股还是配置?',是评估基金经理真实技能、区分阿尔法与贝塔的核心工具。

Decomposing a portfolio's excess return over its benchmark into its sources: the allocation effect (over/under-weighting the right asset classes), the selection effect (picking the right securities within a class), plus interaction and timing effects. It answers 'did my return come from luck, timing, stock-picking or allocation?' — the core tool for judging a manager's real skill and separating alpha from beta.

Etymology 词源

attribute 源自拉丁 attribuere（归于、分派）——归因即'把回报分派给各来源'。

In the wild 语境例句

Attribution reveals a fund beat its benchmark not by stock-picking (selection) but by luckily over-weighting energy (allocation) — a very different verdict on the manager's skill.
