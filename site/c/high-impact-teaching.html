高影响教学策略（HITS） gāo yǐngxiǎng jiàoxué cèlüèHigh Impact Teaching Strategies (HITS)

🔊 High Impact Teaching Strategies /haɪ ˌɪmˈpækt ˈtiʧɪŋ ˈstrætəʤiz/

由教育部门综合循证研究提炼的一组已被证明对学习影响最大的教学策略:设定目标、结构化课程、显性教学、示范、协作学习、多元曝光、提问、反馈、元认知策略、差异化教学。它为教师提供了'什么最管用'的循证清单,是备课与专业发展的实用锚。

A set of strategies distilled from evidence research as having the largest proven impact on learning: setting goals, structuring lessons, explicit teaching, worked examples, collaborative learning, multiple exposures, questioning, feedback, metacognitive strategies, and differentiated teaching. It gives teachers an evidence-based shortlist of what works best — a practical anchor for planning and professional development.

Etymology 词源

high impact（高影响）——借自 Hattie 的效应量研究,指对学习'影响量大'的策略;HITS 为其缩写。
