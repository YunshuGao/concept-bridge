🔊 circularity and the balancing plug /ˌsɜːrkjʊˈlærɪti ənd ðə ˈbælənsɪŋ pləg/

三表模型的技术难点:利息费用取决于债务余额,债务偿还取决于可用现金,现金又取决于利息——形成循环引用。须在Excel开启'迭代计算'求解,或用断路开关(circuit breaker)避免报错。此外,资产负债表须靠'现金/循环贷'作为平衡插头(plug)自动配平。掌握它是建出可运行模型的门槛。

The technical crux of a three-statement model: interest expense depends on the debt balance, debt repayment depends on available cash, and cash depends on interest — a circular reference. It is solved by enabling Excel's iterative calculation, or a circuit breaker switch to avoid errors. The balance sheet is also auto-balanced by a cash/revolver 'plug'. Mastering this is the threshold to building a model that actually runs.

Etymology 词源

circular reference（循环引用）——公式互相依赖成环;plug（插头/填塞）指用来'配平'资产负债表的科目。

    Iterative calculation lets Excel converge on the answer the circularity would otherwise refuse.
