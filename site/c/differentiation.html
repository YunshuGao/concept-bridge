差异化教学 chāyìhuà jiàoxuédifferentiation

🔊 differentiation /ˌdɪfərɛnʃiˈeɪʃən/

根据学生不同的准备度、兴趣与学习方式,调整教学的内容、过程、成果或环境,使每个学生都能在其能力边缘获得适切挑战。常见做法:分层任务（支持/核心/拓展）、多样化支架、弹性分组、开放式任务。它是APST标准1.5的直接要求,也是你三层备课的理论根据。

Adjusting the content, process, product or environment of teaching to students' differing readiness, interests and learning profiles, so each is appropriately challenged at the edge of their ability. Common moves: tiered tasks (support / core / extension), varied scaffolds, flexible grouping, open-ended tasks. It is the direct demand of APST Standard 1.5 and the rationale for your three-tier planning.

Etymology 词源

differentiate 源自拉丁 differre（分开携带、区别）——差异化即'按学生差异区别对待'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ tiered tasks /tird tæsks/
    Tiered tasks meet the same outcome at three levels of support and challenge.
  • ▸ teaching to the edges /ˈtiʧɪŋ tɪ ðə ˈɛʤɪz/
    Teaching to the edges stretches the able while catching the struggling.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ differentiate the task
  • ▸ support, core and extension
  • ▸ flexible grouping
  • ▸ content, process, product

In the wild 语境例句

Differentiating a coding lesson: the same outcome, but sentence-started code for support, a standard brief for core, and an open challenge for extension.
