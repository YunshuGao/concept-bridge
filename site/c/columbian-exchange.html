物种与商品的洲际交流 wùzhǒng jiāoliúthe Columbian Exchange

⏳ 16世纪起

殖民贸易将美洲的马铃薯、玉米、番茄、烟草、可可及亚洲茶叶等运回欧洲，欧洲的葡萄酒、啤酒、毛纺织品销往殖民地；欧洲文化亦传入殖民地，对当地社会发展影响深远。（教材描述的这一双向流动，英语史学称“哥伦布大交换”。）

The two-way traffic colonisation opened: potatoes, maize, tomatoes, tobacco and cocoa flowing to Europe, European goods, people, culture — and, historians add, diseases — flowing back; the process Alfred Crosby named the Columbian Exchange.

Ireland's famine, Sichuan's chillies and Italy's tomatoes are all footnotes to the same event: the Columbian Exchange rewired every cuisine and population curve on earth.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

the Columbian Exchange 是英语史学标准术语（Crosby 1972），中文教材描述其内容而未命名——典型的“概念在场、术语缺位”案例，正是本知识库要补的那类映射。相关词：cash crop（经济作物）、staple crop（主粮）。
