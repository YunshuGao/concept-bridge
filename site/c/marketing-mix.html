营销组合（4P） yíngxiāo zǔhéthe marketing mix (4Ps)

🔊 the four Ps /ðə fɔr piz/

营销执行的四大可控杠杆:产品（Product）、价格（Price）、渠道（Place）、促销（Promotion）。它们必须相互一致地支撑同一定位——高端产品配低价或劣质渠道，四要素打架，定位就崩了。服务业常扩展为7P。

The four controllable levers of marketing execution: Product, Price, Place, Promotion. They must reinforce one and the same positioning — a premium product with a cut price or a shabby channel is a mix at war with itself, and the positioning collapses. Services often extend it to 7Ps.

Etymology 词源

McCarthy 1960年提出的 Product/Price/Place/Promotion 四要素——'4P'成为营销入门第一课。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ the four Ps /ðə fɔr piz/
    The four Ps are only as strong as their agreement with one another.
  • ▸ the go-to-market mix /ðə goʊ-tɪ-ˈmɑrkɪt mɪks/
    The go-to-market mix is where positioning either comes true or falls apart.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ the 4Ps
  • ▸ the marketing mix
  • ▸ align the mix
  • ▸ product, price, place, promotion

In the wild 语境例句

A luxury brand's marketing mix must cohere — premium product, high price, exclusive place, aspirational promotion; discount any one and the whole positioning cheapens.
