🔊 customer acquisition cost /ˈkəstəmər ˌækwəˈzɪʃən kɔst/

获取一个新客户的全部平均成本:营销、销售、渠道、补贴总支出除以新增客户数。CAC 与客户终身价值（LTV）的关系是增长可持续性的核心检验;CAC 回收期越短，增长越健康、越不依赖持续输血。

The full average cost of winning one new customer: total marketing, sales, channel and subsidy spend divided by customers gained. Its relationship to lifetime value (LTV) is the core test of sustainable growth; the shorter the CAC payback period, the healthier the growth and the less it depends on constant funding.

Etymology 词源

acquisition 源自拉丁 acquirere（获取）——获客成本即'把一个陌生人变成付费客户的代价'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ cost per acquisition /kɔst pər ˌækwəˈzɪʃən/
    Cost per acquisition rising while LTV stalls is a growth model quietly breaking.
  • ▸ the CAC payback /ðə kæk ˈpeɪˌbæk/
    The CAC payback period is how many months a customer takes to earn back what you spent to win them.
