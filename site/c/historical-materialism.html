历史唯物主义（社会存在与人民群众） lìshǐ wéiwùzhǔyìhistorical materialism: social being and the people

⏳ 教材体系

社会存在决定社会意识，社会意识具有相对独立性（先进社会意识可推动社会发展）。生产力与生产关系、经济基础与上层建筑的矛盾运动是社会发展的基本规律（生产关系一定要适合生产力状况——改革即社会主义制度的自我完善和发展）。社会历史的主体是人民群众：人民群众是历史的创造者（物质财富、精神财富的创造者，社会变革的决定力量）——坚持群众观点和群众路线（一切为了群众、一切依靠群众，从群众中来、到群众中去）。价值观的导向作用；在劳动和奉献、个人与社会的统一中创造和实现人生价值。

The social half of the doctrine: being determines consciousness; productive forces and relations, base and superstructure, drive history through their friction (reform, in this frame, is socialism 'perfecting itself' when relations lag forces); and the masses — not heroes — make history, grounding the 'mass line' method ('from the masses, to the masses') that the party names as its lifeline. This is the theory referenced by every 'people-centred' formulation, and the analytical frame [[marxism]] built and this book operationalises for civic instruction.

In the wild 语境例句

'Base and superstructure' escaped the seminar long ago — Economist writers use it half-ironically for everything from crypto to culture wars; the source vocabulary is this chapter's.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

经济基础/上层建筑 = base/superstructure（英语理论界通用）；群众路线 = the mass line（官方译，治理研究专名）。“自我完善和发展”是改革的经典正当化语式——链 [[economic-reform]]、[[third-plenum]]。人民群众史观 vs 英雄史观（great man theory）为史学理论对轴——链 [[shiji]]。
