哥伦布“发现”美洲 GēlúnbùColumbus and the 'discovery' of America

⏳ 1492年–1504年

意大利热那亚航海家，坚信地圆说、向往《马可·波罗行纪》中的东方；1492年受西班牙王室资助率3船87人横渡大西洋，抵巴哈马群岛的圣萨尔瓦多岛，后又“发现”古巴、海地。他至死认为所到即印度，称当地人“印第安人”。美洲实以确认新大陆的亚美利哥（Amerigo）命名。

A Genoese who, sold on the round earth and Marco Polo's Cathay, crossed the Atlantic in 1492 with Spanish backing, three ships and 87 men, making landfall in the Bahamas and then Cuba and Hispaniola — convinced to his death he had reached the Indies, which is why the people he met are still called 'Indians'. The continent took its name instead from Amerigo Vespucci, who recognised it as a new world.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“发现”必须打引号的原因，英语讨论用 Eurocentric perspective 表述；“印第安人”现规范称 Native Americans / Indigenous peoples（加拿大用 First Nations），American Indian 在美国法律语境仍存在。pre-Columbian（前哥伦布时代的）是考古艺术领域标准词。
