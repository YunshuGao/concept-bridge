数字化转型 shùzìhuà zhuǎnxíngdigital transformation

🔊 digital transformation /ˈdɪʤɪtəl ˌtrænsfərˈmeɪʃən/

用数字技术（云、数据、AI、自动化）从根本上重塑商业模式、流程与客户体验,而非仅把线下环节搬到线上。真正的转型改变'公司如何创造与捕获价值',其最大障碍往往不是技术而是组织文化与既得利益。

Using digital technology (cloud, data, AI, automation) to fundamentally reshape business models, processes and customer experience — not merely moving offline steps online. Real transformation changes how a company creates and captures value, and its biggest obstacle is usually not technology but culture and vested interests.

Etymology 词源

digital 源自拉丁 digitus（手指——计数始于掰指头）；转型即'根本的形态改变',非表层数字化。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ digitalisation /ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
    Digitalisation done right rewires the business, not just its front end.
  • ▸ technology-enabled reinvention /tɛkˈnɑləʤi-ɪˈneɪbəld ˌriɪnˈvɛnʃən/
    Technology-enabled reinvention is transformation; putting a PDF online is not.
