澳大利亚教学与学校领导协会（AITSL） àodàlìyà jiàoxué yǔ xuéxiào lǐngdǎo xiéhuìAITSL

🔊 AITSL /ˈaɪtsəl/

制定并维护全国教师专业标准（APST）、校长标准与教师教育认证框架的国家机构。它是澳洲教师专业化的'标准制定者',提供大量循证教学资源（如高影响教学策略HITS）。理解AITSL与NESA的分工——前者定全国标准,后者在本州执行——是读懂澳洲教师治理的关键。

The national body that develops and maintains the Australian Professional Standards for Teachers (APST), the Principal Standard and the accreditation framework for teacher education. It is the standard-setter of Australian teacher professionalism, providing evidence-based resources (such as the High Impact Teaching Strategies, HITS). Understanding the division between AITSL and NESA — the former sets national standards, the latter enforces them in the state — is key to reading Australian teacher governance.
