什一税 shíyīshuìthe tithe

🔊 tithe /taɪð/

⏳ 8世纪起

查理曼命各教区人民将每年收入的十分之一贡献给教会的税项，使教会日益富有、势力膨胀。

The tenth of annual income Charlemagne ordered every parish to render to the Church — the levy that made the medieval Church rich and mighty.

Etymology 词源

tithe 古英语 teogotha（第十）——“什一”即“第十份”，词即税率。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ levy /ˈlɛvi/
    A levy on faith funded the spire.
  • ▸ church tax /ʧərʧ tæks/
    The church tax built rich abbeys and resentments.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

The word survives its abolition: pundits describe app-store commissions as the 30% tithe developers pay their platform church.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

tithe 本义即“十分之一”（古英语 teogotha）；现代英语作动词/名词仍活跃，尤指教会捐献或比喻性的“上贡”。区分 tax（国家税）与 tithe（教会税）。
