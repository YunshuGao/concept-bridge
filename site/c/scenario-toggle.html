情景切换开关 qíngjǐng qiēhuàn kāiguānscenario toggles (bull/base/bear)

🔊 scenario toggles /sɪˈnɛrioʊ ˈtɑgəlz/

在模型中用一个开关(下拉菜单或数字选择)一键切换牛市/基准/熊市三套假设,让同一模型瞬间呈现乐观、中性、悲观三种结果。它把敏感性分析升级为完整情景分析,是稳健建模与向管理层展示不确定性的标配。配合CHOOSE或INDEX函数实现。

A switch in the model (a dropdown or number) that flips between bull/base/bear assumption sets with one click, letting the same model instantly show optimistic, neutral and pessimistic outcomes. It upgrades sensitivity analysis to full scenario analysis and is standard in robust modelling and in presenting uncertainty to management. Implemented with CHOOSE or INDEX functions.

Etymology 词源

toggle（拨动开关）——在几套假设间'拨动'切换;bull（牛,涨）/bear（熊,跌）为市场情绪的传统隐喻。
