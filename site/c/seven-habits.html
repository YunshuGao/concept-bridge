🔊 proactive /proʊˈæktɪv/

柯维的管理学圣经:主动积极（proactive,刺激与回应之间存在选择自由）、以终为始、要事第一、双赢思维、知彼解己、统合综效、不断更新。关键区分:关注圈 vs 影响圈——把能量花在影响圈。

Covey's canon: be proactive (between stimulus and response lies the freedom to choose), begin with the end in mind, first things first, think win-win, seek first to understand, synergise, sharpen the saw. Its sharpest tool: circle of concern vs circle of influence.

Etymology 词源

proactive 为20世纪新造词:pro-（先）+ active——先于刺激而动;由弗兰克尔集中营心理学转入管理学,柯维使其成日常英语。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ circle of influence /ˈsɜːrkəl ʌv ˈɪnfluəns/
    Work the circle of influence and the circle of concern shrinks by itself.
  • ▸ win-win /wɪn wɪn/
    Win-win became boardroom English; Covey supplied the grammar.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ be proactive
  • ▸ begin with the end in mind

  • ▸ emotional bank account

In the wild 语境例句

Half of corporate English is Covey unattributed: win-win, proactive, synergy — the book dissolved into the language.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

proactive/win-win/synergy 已因滥用而近陈词——写作中直接用会显平庸,识别它们的出处反而是修养。“主动积极”的哲学根在弗兰克尔《活出意义来》,比鸡汤深一层。
