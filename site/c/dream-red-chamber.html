《红楼梦》与京剧 Hónglóumèng / JīngjùDream of the Red Chamber and Peking opera

⏳ 18世纪–19世纪

曹雪芹《红楼梦》（后40回一般认为高鹗续）以贾府盛衰为线索，描写贾宝玉林黛玉爱情悲剧，深刻反映封建社会末期的社会现实与走向衰亡的历史命运；人物众多性格鲜明、语言精练生动，是中国古典小说的巅峰，已译成多种文字成为世界文化宝库的珍品。戏剧方面，乾隆时四大徽班进京，徽调吸收昆曲秦腔等曲调唱法，道光年间形成新剧种“皮黄戏”，后称“京戏”“京剧”，深受广大群众喜爱、流传四方。

Cao Xueqin's Dream of the Red Chamber — the fall of a great house, the doomed love of Baoyu and Daiyu, and in them the autumn of the whole imperial order — stands as the summit of the Chinese novel, so bottomless that 'Redology' is an academic discipline. On stage, the Anhui troupes summoned to Qianlong's birthday in 1790 stayed, blended styles, and by the 1840s had crystallised into Peking opera — the painted-face, falsetto-and-acrobatics art that became imperial China's last great gift to its own popular culture.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

《红楼梦》两大英译并立：Hawkes 的 The Story of the Stone 与杨宪益夫妇的 A Dream of Red Mansions——译名之争本身是翻译学名案。京剧 = Peking opera（约定俗成保留 Peking）。“红学”= Redology。与 [[ming-novels]] 合为四大名著；与 [[shakespeare]]、[[realist-literature]] 构成文学巅峰三角。
