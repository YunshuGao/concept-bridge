股本：基本与稀释 gǔběn: jīběn yǔ xīshìshares outstanding (basic vs diluted)

🔊 shares outstanding /ʃɛrz ˌaʊtˈstændɪŋ/

每股指标的分母有两种口径:基本股数(实际发行在外)与稀释股数(计入期权、可转债、限制性股票等潜在稀释,常用库存股法计算)。估值与每股收益须用稀释、全面摊薄口径,否则会高估每股价值。分析师算股权价值时必须用完全稀释股数。

The denominator of per-share metrics comes two ways: basic shares (actually issued and outstanding) and diluted shares (including potential dilution from options, convertibles and restricted stock, usually via the treasury stock method). Valuation and EPS use the diluted, fully-loaded count, or per-share value is overstated. Analysts must use fully diluted shares when computing equity value.

Etymology 词源

outstanding（在外流通的）——已发行、'在外'由投资者持有的股份;treasury stock method 即库存股法。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ fully diluted shares /ˈfʊli dɪˈlutəd ʃɛrz/
    Fully diluted shares count every option and convertible as if already exercised.
  • ▸ the treasury stock method /ðə ˈtrɛʒəri stɑk ˈmɛθəd/
    The treasury stock method estimates the net new shares options would actually add.

In the wild 语境例句

Using basic instead of diluted shares understated the count by 12% — inflating per-share value until the analyst applied the treasury stock method to the option pool.
