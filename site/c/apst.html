澳大利亚教师专业标准（APST） àodàlìyà jiàoshī zhuānyè biāozhǔnthe Australian Professional Standards for Teachers (APST)

🔊 Australian Professional Standards for Teachers /ɔˈstreɪljən prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈstændərdz fər ˈtiʧərz/

全国统一的教师能力框架,由AITSL制定,含7项标准、归为3大领域（专业知识、专业实践、专业投入）,并划分4个职业阶段（毕业Graduate→熟练Proficient→高度精湛Highly Accomplished→引领Lead）。它是教师认证、GTPA评判、专业发展与绩效的共同语言与标尺。

A national framework of teacher capability, developed by AITSL, with 7 standards grouped into 3 domains (Professional Knowledge, Professional Practice, Professional Engagement) and 4 career stages (Graduate → Proficient → Highly Accomplished → Lead). It is the common language and yardstick for teacher accreditation, GTPA judgement, professional development and performance.

Etymology 词源

standard 源自古法语 estandart（军旗、集结点）——标准即'共同集结、衡量的旗帜';APST 是全澳教师的共同旗帜。
