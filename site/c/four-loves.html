路易斯《四种爱》 lùyìsīC.S. Lewis: the four loves

🔊 agape /ˈæɡəpeɪ/

C.S.路易斯借希腊语四分英语的 love：storge（亲情/熟悉之爱）、philia（友爱）、eros（爱情）、agape/charity（无条件的圣爱）。《返璞归真》（Mere Christianity）为其神学入门书。

Lewis borrows Greek to split the English word love four ways: storge (affection), philia (friendship), eros (romantic love), agape or charity (unconditional love). Mere Christianity is his companion apologetics.

Etymology 词源

agape 希腊语“圣爱”借入英语后与 agape（张口惊愕,a-gape）拼写撞车——重音分义:/ˈæɡəpeɪ/ 是爱,/əˈɡeɪp/ 是张着嘴。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ charity /ˈtʃærɪti/
    In older English, charity meant agape — love, before it meant donations.
  • ▸ affection /əˈfɛkʃən/
    Affection is love grown in the soil of habit.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ the four loves
  • ▸ to love at all is to be vulnerable
  • ▸ mere Christianity

In the wild 语境例句

Lewis's line — to love at all is to be vulnerable — gets quoted at weddings by people who skip the theology underneath it.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

英语一个 love 覆盖汉语的爱情/亲情/友情/大爱——与希腊四分对照,汉语学习者反而占优。KJV 圣经把 agape 译作 charity,故 charity 在古典英文里指“圣爱”而非慈善捐款。
