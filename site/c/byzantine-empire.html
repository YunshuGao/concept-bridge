拜占庭帝国 Bàizhàntíng dìguóthe Byzantine Empire

also: the Eastern Roman Empire · Byzantium

⏳ 395年–1453年

东罗马帝国（都君士坦丁堡，故称拜占庭帝国）：西罗马亡后延续千年并入“黄金时代”；对基督教、希腊罗马古典传统及西亚北非东方文化兼收并蓄，保存大量古籍，为文艺复兴提供精神营养。历经阿拉伯人进攻、十字军攻占君士坦丁堡，1453年为奥斯曼帝国所灭。

The Eastern Roman Empire, ruled from Constantinople, which outlived the West by a thousand years; a civilisation that fused Christianity, the Greco-Roman inheritance and Eastern influences, and whose preserved manuscripts would feed the Renaissance. Battered by Arab conquests and betrayed by the Fourth Crusade's sack of its capital, it fell to the Ottomans in 1453.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

小写 byzantine 是高频贬义形容词：错综复杂、勾心斗角的（byzantine regulations/politics）——Economist 常用。帝国自称始终是“罗马”（Roman），“拜占庭”为后世史家之名。君士坦丁堡 Constantinople → 1453年后 Istanbul。
