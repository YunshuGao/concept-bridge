生态战略 shēngtài zhànlüèecosystem strategy

🔊 ecosystem strategy /ˈikoʊˌsɪstəm ˈstrætəʤi/

企业不再只做产品，而是编排一群互补者（开发者、供应商、伙伴）共同为客户创造价值，并从中占据关键节点（如操作系统、应用商店、支付）。竞争从公司对公司升级为生态对生态。

A firm orchestrates a web of complementors (developers, suppliers, partners) that jointly create value for customers, while occupying the key nodes (OS, app store, payments). Competition escalates from firm-versus-firm to ecosystem-versus-ecosystem.

Etymology 词源

ecosystem 源自生态学（希腊 oikos 家 + systēma 系统）——商业生态即“互相依存的商业物种群落”。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ complementors /ˈkɒmplɪmɛntərz/
    Complementors make your product more valuable without being on your payroll.
  • ▸ orchestrator /ˈɔːrkɪstreɪtər/
    The orchestrator sets the rules and takes a cut of everyone else's effort.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ build an ecosystem
  • ▸ attract complementors
  • ▸ the keystone player
  • ▸ platform envelopment

In the wild 语境例句

A phone maker's real product is its ecosystem — apps, accessories, services — each of which makes leaving costlier than the hardware alone.
