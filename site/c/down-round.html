降价融资 jiàngjià róngzīthe down round

🔊 down round /daʊn raʊnd/

公司以低于上一轮的估值进行新融资——信号是增长不及预期或市场遇冷。降价融资触发反稀释条款、重挫员工期权价值、打击士气与信心，是创业公司最难堪的时刻之一。

Raising a new round at a lower valuation than the previous one — a signal that growth disappointed or the market cooled. Down rounds trigger anti-dilution provisions, crush the value of employee options, and dent morale and confidence — one of the most bruising moments in a startup's life.

Etymology 词源

down（向下）+ round（融资轮次）——估值“向下”的一轮，与 up round 相对。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ a valuation reset /ə væljuˈeɪʃən ˈrisɛt/
    A valuation reset is the market marking a company back to earth.
  • ▸ the cram-down /ðə kræm-daʊn/
    A cram-down forces existing holders to accept dilutive terms or lose their stake entirely.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ raise a down round
  • ▸ trigger anti-dilution
  • ▸ a valuation reset
  • ▸ wipe out the common

In the wild 语境例句

When the market turned, the once-celebrated unicorn raised a down round at half its peak valuation — anti-dilution clauses then diluted the founders further.
