🔊 parent and carer engagement /ˈpɛrənt ənd ˈkɛərər ɛnˈgeɪʤmənt/

与家长/照护者建立尊重、双向的伙伴关系,共同支持学生学习与福祉:清晰及时的沟通、汇报学习进展、倾听家庭视角、尊重文化多样性、在敏感对话中保持专业。研究表明家庭参与显著影响学生成效。对EAL/D与多元文化家庭,须注意语言与文化的可及性。APST标准7的组成部分。

Building respectful, two-way partnerships with parents and carers to jointly support students' learning and wellbeing: clear and timely communication, reporting on progress, listening to the family's perspective, respecting cultural diversity, and staying professional in sensitive conversations. Research shows family engagement significantly affects student outcomes. For EAL/D and culturally diverse families, attend to linguistic and cultural accessibility. Part of APST Standard 7.

Etymology 词源

engage 源自古法语 engager（使承诺、结约,gage 抵押）——'engagement'即建立相互投入的关系。

    The home-school partnership treats parents as allies in learning, not just recipients of grades.
  • ▸ two-way communication /tu-weɪ kəmˌjunəˈkeɪʃən/
    Two-way communication listens to the family's knowledge, not only broadcasts the school's.

Framing a difficult conversation as 'how can we together help your child' — and offering an interpreter for an EAL/D family — turns a report into genuine parent engagement.
