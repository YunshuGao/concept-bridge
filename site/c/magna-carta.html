《大宪章》 DàxiànzhāngMagna Carta

⏳ 1215年

英格兰1215年颁布的文件：维护教俗贵族特权，规定未经协商国王无权征税，逐渐确立“王权有限”“王在法下”的基本原则；13世纪末英格兰确立上下两院的议会制度，征税权归议会。

The charter of 1215 that, in defending baronial and clerical privilege, fixed the principles that royal power is limited and the king is under the law — no taxation without counsel; by century's end England had a two-house Parliament holding the power of the purse.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

拉丁名 Magna Carta（大宪章）直接用于英语，习惯不加 the。“王在法下”= the king is under the law（布拉克顿名言）；“征税权在议会”即 the power of the purse——美国政治报道高频词。上院/下院 = the House of Lords / the House of Commons。
