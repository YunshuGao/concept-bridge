🔊 cash flow statement /kæʃ floʊ ˈsteɪtmənt/

三大报表之一:按经营、投资、融资三类活动追踪现金的实际流入流出，剔除权责发生制的会计估计。“利润是观点，现金是事实”——它是识别盈利质量与识破财务造假的照妖镜。

One of the three statements: tracking actual cash in and out across operating, investing and financing activities, stripping away accrual estimates. 'Profit is an opinion, cash is a fact' — it is the lens for judging earnings quality and catching accounting fraud.

Etymology 词源

cash 源自法语 caisse（钱箱）——现金即“钱箱里的钱”；现金流量表追踪钱箱的进出。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ operating cash flow /ˈɔpərˌeɪtɪŋ kæʃ floʊ/
    Operating cash flow reveals whether the profit was ever real money.
