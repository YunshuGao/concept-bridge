甲骨文 jiǎgǔwénthe oracle bone script

⏳ 商周时期

商周刻写在龟甲兽骨上的文字，1899年王懿荣从中药“龙骨”上首次发现；出土商代有字甲骨约15万片、单字4400以上、已识1500以上。内容涉祭祀战争农牧官制刑法医药天文历法。使用象形、指事、会意、形声、假借等造字法（象形约40%，形声最进步），已具备汉字基本结构——中国已发现年代最早、体系较完整的文字；我国有文字可考的历史从商朝开始。（青铜器铭文称金文。）

The script of Shang divination, carved on turtle plastrons and ox scapulae — recognised in 1899 when the scholar Wang Yirong noticed writing on 'dragon bones' sold as medicine. Some 150,000 inscribed pieces record sacrifices, wars, harvests and star-signs in over 4,400 characters, built by the same methods (pictographs, compound ideas, phonetic compounds) that still generate Chinese characters: the earliest systematic Chinese writing, and the point where China's documented history begins.

The oracle bones did for Shang what the Rosetta Stone did for Egypt — turned legend into chronicle; unlike hieroglyphs, though, their script never died: today's 日 and 月 are their direct descendants.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“甲骨文”= oracle bone script/inscriptions；“占卜”= divination。汉字英语规范称 Chinese characters（勿称 hieroglyphs——回链 [[hieroglyphs]] 的警告）。金文 = bronze inscriptions。“形声字”= phonetic compounds/phono-semantic characters。汉字未断裂的连续性是中英文明对比常用论据。
