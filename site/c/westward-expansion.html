西进运动 xījìn yùndòngthe Westward Expansion

⏳ 19世纪

美国独立后领土从大西洋岸迅速扩张到太平洋岸，大批移民由东部迁往西部垦殖，出现“小麦王国”“棉花王国”与新兴工厂城市；但大批印第安人被屠杀或驱赶到更远地区，其被迫迁徙之路称“血泪之路”。

Independent America swept from ocean to ocean as settlers poured west, raising wheat and cotton kingdoms and new factory towns — while Native Americans were massacred or driven ever further out, their forced marches remembered as the Trail of Tears.
