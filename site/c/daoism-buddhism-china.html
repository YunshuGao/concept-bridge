道教兴起与佛教传入 Dàojiào yǔ Fójiào chuánrùthe rise of religious Daoism and Buddhism's arrival

⏳ 东汉

东汉末民众苦难深重，张角创太平道（尊奉黄帝老子、以治病传道）、张陵创五斗米道——早期道教受下层民众信奉。佛教（产生于前6世纪古印度）经丝绸之路传入：东汉明帝时西域僧人载佛经至洛阳（白马寺为中国最早佛寺），得上层扶持渐播——丰富了中国文化，在思想文学建筑雕刻绘画诸方面影响深远；“五体投地”“大千世界”“在劫难逃”“因果”等语汇皆源佛经。

In the misery of the late Eastern Han, Zhang Jue's Way of Great Peace and Zhang Ling's Five Pecks of Rice sect turned Laozi into a god and healing into recruitment — religious Daoism was born among the desperate. Buddhism arrived by the Silk Road: sutras carried to Luoyang under Emperor Ming (the White Horse Temple its first house), patronage from the top, and in time a transformation of Chinese thought, letters, building and art — Chinese itself still speaks Buddhist loanwords daily, from 'world' to 'karma'.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

哲学道家（[[laozi]]）与宗教道教（religious Daoism）英语必须分开——中文一个“道”字统摄，英语 Daoist philosophy vs Daoist religion。“白马寺”= the White Horse Temple。汉语佛源词（世界、因果、刹那、方便）是绝佳的语言接触案例，与英语中的拉丁/法语层对照。
