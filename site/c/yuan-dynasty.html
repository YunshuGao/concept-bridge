元朝的统一与行省制度 Yuáncháo / xíngshěng zhìdùthe Yuan unification and the provincial system

⏳ 1271年–1368年

忽必烈1260年继汗位，纳汉儒“行汉法行仁政”之议，1271年改国号元、都大都；1276年陷临安，1279年灭宋残部（文天祥“人生自古谁无死，留取丹心照汗青”，宁死不屈）——结束长期分裂，元版图为我国历史上最大（今新疆西藏云南东北台湾及南海诸岛皆在治内）。中央中书省掌行政（下辖六部）、枢密院掌军、御史台掌监察；地方设10行省，行省之下路府州县——“行省制度”为明清沿用、影响深远（今日之省即源于此）。边疆因地制宜：澎湖巡检司辖澎湖琉球（台湾）——中央政府首次在台湾正式建制；宣政院直辖西藏——中央对西藏正式行使行政管辖；北庭都元帅府辖西域。各族杂居交融，波斯阿拉伯人与汉蒙畏兀儿诸族融成新民族——回族。（元行四等人制民族歧视，为其速亡要因之一。）

Kublai took the khanate in 1260, took Chinese advice ('rule by Han law'), took the dynastic name Yuan in 1271 and took the last Song stronghold in 1279 — Wen Tianxiang choosing death with the line every Chinese schoolchild knows: 'since time began, who escapes death? Let my loyal heart shine in the annals.' The empire that resulted was the largest China has ever been, and its administrative invention outlived it: ten 'branch secretariats' — xingsheng, the ancestors of today's provinces — under a central secretariat, with the Pescadores office giving Taiwan its first central administration and the Xuanzheng Yuan placing Tibet under direct imperial rule. Persians and Arabs mixing with Han, Mongol and Uighur communities crystallised into a new people, the Hui; a four-class ethnic hierarchy, Mongols on top, helped bring the whole edifice down within a century.

In the wild 语境例句

When Beijing says Tibet and Taiwan have been Chinese 'since the Yuan', these are the two institutions being cited — the Xuanzheng Yuan and the Penghu office; the argument is 750 years old and very much alive.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“行省”= province（词源 branch secretariat——今“省”字即元制活化石）。宣政院/澎湖巡检司是台藏主权论述的根史料，英译需准确：the Xuanzheng Yuan (Bureau of Buddhist and Tibetan Affairs)；the Penghu Patrol and Inspection Office。回族 = the Hui。四等人制 = the four-class system（教材列为亡因——民族政策的反面教材，对照 [[xiaowen-reforms]]）。
