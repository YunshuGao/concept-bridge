🔊 transformational leadership /ˌtrænsfərˈmeɪʃənəl ˈlidərˌʃɪp/

通过愿景、感召、智识激发与个性化关怀,激励追随者超越自身短期利益、追求更高集体目标的领导风格——与依赖奖惩交换的'交易型领导'相对。它擅长激发变革与投入,但也可能滑向个人崇拜。

A leadership style that inspires followers to transcend their own short-term interests for higher collective goals, through vision, charisma, intellectual stimulation and individual attention — as opposed to transactional leadership, which relies on reward-and-punishment exchange. It excels at igniting change and commitment, but can slide toward a cult of personality.

Etymology 词源

transform 源自拉丁 trans（越过）+ formare（塑形）——变革型领导即'重塑追随者的动机与目标'。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ visionary leadership /ˈvɪʒəˌnɛri ˈlidərˌʃɪp/
    Visionary leadership sells a future compelling enough to reorganise the present around it.
  • ▸ transactional leadership /trænˈzækʃənəl ˈlidərˌʃɪp/
    Transactional leadership trades rewards for compliance and rarely inspires more.

Collocations 常用搭配

In the wild 语境例句

Transactional leadership gets this quarter's numbers; transformational leadership gets people to reinvent the company — and the best leaders switch between them.
