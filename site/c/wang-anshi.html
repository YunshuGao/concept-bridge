王安石变法 Wáng Ānshí biànfǎthe Wang Anshi reforms

⏳ 1069年–1085年

北宋政风因循保守、行政低效，边战屡败岁输钱物，官俸军费浩大财政入不敷出，土地兼并剧烈起义频仍。1069年宋神宗任王安石主持变法，涉经济军事教育：募役法（收役钱雇人服役、官僚也须交钱——限特权增收入）、方田均税法（核实土地按多少好坏征税——防隐田逃税）、农田水利法、保甲法（编户出丁、平时种田农闲练兵）。变法有成效（财政增收、修水利万余处），但触犯大官僚大地主利益遭强烈反对；神宗死后司马光任相尽废新法。（前有范仲淹庆历新政，数月而罢，开改革风气之先。）

Mid-Song government was a fiscal paradox — huge revenues, huger costs, tribute abroad and stagnation within. From 1069, backed by Emperor Shenzong, Wang Anshi legislated a New Policies programme startlingly modern in kind: a hired-service tax replacing corvée (the privileged now paying too), cadastral survey to tax land by measured quality, state loans for irrigation, and militia organisation of the countryside. Revenue rose and ten thousand waterworks were built — but the programme gored the great officials and landowners it taxed, and when the emperor died, Sima Guang repealed it nearly whole. China's classic reform tragedy: right diagnosis, mobilised interests, dead letter.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“变法”系列译名：the Wang Anshi Reforms / the New Policies (xin fa)。“既得利益”= vested interests——本课的分析主词。与 [[shang-yang]]（成而身死）、[[serf-emancipation]]（自上而下）、[[new-deal]]（国家干预）构成改革比较组。司马光既是政敌又是《资治通鉴》作者——史学与政争同体，绝佳的历史书写案例。
