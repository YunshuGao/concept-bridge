飞轮效应 fēilún xiàoyìngthe flywheel effect

🔊 flywheel effect /ˈflaɪwiːl ˈifɛkt/

柯林斯:卓越不是靠某个决定性时刻,而是持续朝一个方向推动一个沉重的飞轮——起初费力、几乎不动,但每一圈都积累动量,最终它靠自身惯性飞速旋转。它是自我强化的良性循环在企业战略中的隐喻。

Jim Collins: greatness comes not from a single decisive moment but from persistently pushing a heavy flywheel in one direction — at first laborious and barely moving, but each turn builds momentum until it spins fast on its own inertia. It is the metaphor for a self-reinforcing virtuous cycle in corporate strategy.
