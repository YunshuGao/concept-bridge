唯物辩证法（联系、发展、矛盾） wéiwù biànzhèngfǎmaterialist dialectics: connection, development, contradiction

🔊 contradiction /ˌkɑntrəˈdɪkʃən/

⏳ 教材体系

唯物辩证法的总特征：联系的观点（普遍性、客观性、多样性——用联系的观点看问题、掌握系统优化方法）与发展的观点（前进性与曲折性统一——前途光明道路曲折；量变与质变——量的积累到一定程度引起质变，“不积跬步无以至千里”）。实质与核心：矛盾规律（对立统一）——矛盾的普遍性与特殊性（具体问题具体分析）、主要矛盾与次要矛盾、矛盾的主要方面与次要方面（“两点论与重点论统一”）。辩证否定观：扬弃——不是简单肯定或否定一切。

The method behind the slogans: everything connected (hence 'systems thinking' as official method), everything developing ('the future is bright, the road is tortuous' — progress through spirals), quantity turning into quality at thresholds. Its 'living soul' is contradiction analysis: contradictions universal yet each particular ('concrete analysis of concrete conditions'), ranked into principal and secondary with principal aspects — the analytical grid by which the party names each era's 主要矛盾 and thereby its policy priority. Dialectical negation ('sublation' — keep the kernel, shed the husk) rounds out a toolkit that explains why Chinese official prose reasons the way it does.

contradiction 源自拉丁 contra（相对）+ dicere（说）——矛盾即“对着说”:中文取自韩非的矛与盾。

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

矛盾 = contradiction（黑格尔—马克思术语；毛《矛盾论》On Contradiction 使其成为中国政治思维的元语法）；扬弃 = sublation（Aufheben——哲学英语硬词）。量变质变 = quantitative/qualitative change。“具体问题具体分析”= concrete analysis of concrete conditions（列宁语）。链 [[laozi]] 对立转化（福祸相依）——中式辩证的本土根系。
