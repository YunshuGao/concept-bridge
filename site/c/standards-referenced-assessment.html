标准参照评估 biāozhǔn cānzhào pínggūstandards-referenced assessment

🔊 standards-referenced assessment /ˈstændərdz-ˈrɛfərənst əˈsɛsmənt/

依据预设的成绩标准（如A–E水平描述）判定学生表现,而非按学生间排名（常模参照）或简单及格线（标准参照的旧义）。NSW采用此制:每个成绩对应明确的能力描述,学生对标准而非对彼此。它要求量规清晰、评分调节以保证判断一致。

Judging student performance against pre-set grade standards (e.g. A–E level descriptions), rather than by ranking students against each other (norm-referenced) or a simple pass mark. NSW uses this system: each grade corresponds to an explicit description of ability, and students are measured against the standard, not one another. It demands clear rubrics and moderation to keep judgements consistent.

Etymology 词源

reference（参照,拉丁 referre 带回）——评估'参照'既定标准而非彼此;与 norm-referenced（常模参照）相对。

Synonyms 近义词

  • ▸ criterion-referenced /kraɪˈtɪəriən ˈrɛfərənst/
    Criterion-referenced grading asks 'did they meet the standard?', not 'who came top?'
  • ▸ the common grade scale /ðə ˈkɑmən greɪd skeɪl/
    The common grade scale gives every A the same meaning across every classroom.

Collocations 常用搭配

  • ▸ standards-referenced grading
  • ▸ the A-E grade scale
  • ▸ against the standard, not the cohort
  • ▸ the achievement standard

In the wild 语境例句

Under standards-referenced assessment, a whole class can earn an A if they all meet the standard — grades describe achievement, not scarcity.
