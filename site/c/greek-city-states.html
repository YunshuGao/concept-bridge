希腊城邦 Xīlà chéngbāngthe Greek city-states

also: the polis (pl. poleis)

⏳ 前8世纪起

公元前8世纪起希腊出现的以一城为中心、联合周边农村的小国，突出特点是“小国寡民”；多山环海的地理环境阻碍统一、却利于航海与海外贸易。

The small self-governing states that emerged in Greece from the 8th century BC, each a town with its rural hinterland; mountainous, sea-girt geography kept Greece politically fragmented but bred seafaring and overseas trade.

Nuance 陷阱与辨析

“小国寡民”本是《道德经》语，教材借喻希腊城邦；英语可译 small territory, few citizens 或直接说 the polis was tiny by design。“城邦”标准译法 city-state，学术语境常用希腊原词 polis。
